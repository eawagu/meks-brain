## Rationale

Physical verification (~₦3,500 per customer) is prohibitive for small-ticket loans (~₦2M). The current process also excludes "digital customers" without a physical POS. A purely digital lending product is essential for reach.

## Pricing Thesis

Digital product priced for higher risk, accepting 10–20% NPL rate while maintaining the same overall yield as the existing micro-customer portfolio. Customers could later opt into physical verification for lower rates.

## Verification Proxy

For merchant customers, **POS location data** serves as a verification proxy. Verification process team has monitored effectiveness against POS location for several months, building comfort with the substitution. Key unsolved: non-POS merchants.

## Sales Channel Matrix (2×2)

| | Sales Team (BRMs) | Digital |
|---|---|---|
| Non-Digital Lending | Viable (current) | Not viable |
| Digital Lending | Viable | Viable |

Three of four quadrants viable; BRM+digital-channel hybrid opens system-driven growth.
