## Definition

**AI operations support** is the [[Strike team]] mandate (alongside building the new [[Card Management System]]) to drive AI-driven solutions and operationalize AI within Moniepoint's card operations — automating processes, reducing manual request logging, supporting the operations teams.

## Priorities (Apr 21 KT)

1. **Chargeback / Issue Resolution** (highest priority) — [[Aima]] + [[Precious]]'s sub-team. Targets: automating dispute intake, routing, status communication.
2. **Card Production / Logistics** (second priority) — [[Philip]]'s sub-team. Targets: automating card file generation, inventory requests, regional dispatch.

## Strategic framing

AI is positioned as a **strike-team responsibility**, not a standalone initiative — the team that builds the new CMS also drives AI in card operations. This bundling reflects the operating-model assumption that platform builders are best positioned to identify and automate operational workflows during the rebuild.
