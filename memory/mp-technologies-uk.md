## Overview

MP Technologies UK (also referenced as Moniepoint Technologies UK) is the **legal home of the central Platform Organization ([[IPCo/OpCo|IPCo]])** under [[Project Phoenix]]. It owns intellectual property, product management, engineering (including SREs), design, platform roadmap, and SLAs — and charges Operating Companies (OpCos) for services.

## Scope (per Apr 22, 2026 Org Movements brief)

- Intellectual property
- Product management
- Engineering (including SREs)
- Design
- Platform roadmap and SLAs
- Charges OpCos for services

## Key People

- [[Pawel Swiatek]] — Group COO
- Hosts the group platforms: [[TSPP]], [[CI&P]], [[Payment Gateway Platform]], and others in the Business Banking / Digital Banking / Credit clusters
- Platform heads who convert from TeamApt OpCo under Phoenix:
  - [[Frank Atashili]] — Head, TSPP (Product)
  - [[Tracy Ojaigho]] — Head, CI&P
  - [[Damilare Ogunnaike]] — Head, Payment Gateway Platform

## Relationship to OpCos

Serves all Moniepoint OpCos as tenants: [[TeamApt]] (Nigeria), [[MoniePoint MFB]] (Nigeria), [[MonieWorld]] (UK), [[Sumac MFB]] (Kenya), and future markets.
