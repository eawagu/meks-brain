## Overview

**MFB Cards Team** is the existing cards organization inside [[Moniepoint MFB]], historically led by Business Leader [[Olufemi Davies]]. As of April 2026, it is transitioning to central platform leadership under [[Project Phoenix]] while Olufemi continues as Business Leader and [[Tracy Ojaigho]] takes on the Product Leader role for the central platform the MFB consumes.

## Structure (pre-Phoenix as of Apr 2026)

### Product Teams
- **Team 1 — [[Card Sales and Distribution Team]]** (smaller)
  - PM: [[Nadeem Abbas]]
  - EM: [[Elishma Nwobodo]]
  - Scope: pricing, sharing formulas, ordering, distribution chain, customer sales experience
- **Team 2 — [[Card Infrastructure Team]]** (larger)
  - PM: [[Damilola Oyediran]]
  - EM: [[Nitish Chand]]
  - Scope: transaction processing, security modules, integrations, plus deferred VAS (tokenization, ACS)

A planned **third team (VAS)** was shelved pending the broader platformisation initiative. Its scope (tokenization, ACS, etc.) currently remains inside Team 2.

### Operations Sub-teams (embedded)
- **[[Card Production and Logistics]]** — led by [[Philip]], team of 4
- **[[Chargeback and Issue Resolution]]** — co-led by [[Aima]] and [[Precious]]

## Transition to Central Platform (Apr 2026)

Formalized April 14 2026: platform leadership ([[Tracy Ojaigho]] as Head of [[Digital Banking Platforms]], [[Emeka Awagu]] as Head of CI&P Engineering) formally took operational ownership of both MFB cards product teams. Olufemi Davies continues as Business Leader on the MFB side.

### Operating Model — Strike Team + Continuity

- **Continuity track:** existing MFB engineers continue maintaining the current MFB cards systems.
- **Build track:** a strike team (drawn from across the teams that will feed into the central platform) builds the new [[Card Management System]] and broader [[Card Issuance & Processing Platform]].
- Explicitly mirrors the pattern Frank + Alex are running on [[TSP]].
- Commitment: audit existing MFB team's work and incorporate into the CI&P roadmap — existing work is not discarded.

## Market Share

Under MFB cards leadership, Moniepoint's card issuance market share grew from 7th to 3rd nationally. Target: 2nd by end of 2026.
