## Summary

Gap analysis: TSP v2.0 vs Visa and MasterCard scheme requirements. Adequate executive-level coverage (both schemes have dedicated adapters, ISO 8583 library, HSM, 3DS module). Primary gaps at engineering level.

## Key Points

- Both Visa and MasterCard have dedicated adapters in TSP v2.0
- ISO 8583 library, HSM, 3DS module present
- Engineering gaps: ISO 8583 version distinction for MasterCard IPM
- Scheme stand-in advice processing not fully specified
- Dispute switching ownership needs clarification
- Switching vs business logic separation principle applied
- Three elevation items recommended for future briefing
