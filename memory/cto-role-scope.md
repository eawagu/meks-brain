CTO Role Scope defines the boundary of technology accountability held by the CTO — which functions, teams, and decisions the role is directly responsible for versus delegated elsewhere.

## TeamApt CTO Scope (as of 2026-04-16)
Post-restructuring, Mek's CTO role at [[TeamApt]] directly retains only [[Operations]]. All other tech functions have moved to the [[Platform Organization]]. This is a significant narrowing of the traditional CTO direct span, consistent with a platform operating model where technology functions are centralized under a platform org.
