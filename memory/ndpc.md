## Overview
[[NDPC]] (Nigeria Data Protection Commission) is the primary authority for data protection in Nigeria. Co-facilitating the technical coordination secretariat for the proposed Cybersecurity Coordination Council.

## Findings from TeamApt IMS Internal Audit (Apr 2026)
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]]: ISMS clause A5.5 — clerical error flagged where "NDIC" was listed instead of "NDPC" in a policy document. OFI: update document to correctly reflect NDPC as the primary authority for data protection.
