## Overview
[[NDPC]] (Nigeria Data Protection Commission) is the primary authority for data protection in Nigeria, enforcing the Nigeria Data Protection Act. Co-facilitating the technical coordination secretariat for the proposed Cybersecurity Coordination Council.

## Findings from TeamApt IMS Internal Audit (Apr 2026)
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]]: ISMS clause A5.5 — clerical error flagged where 'NDIC' was listed instead of 'NDPC' in a policy document. OFI: update document to correctly reflect NDPC as the primary authority for data protection.

## DDMFB 2026 IT Strategy — Missing Compliance Strategy
Per [[DDMFB_2026_IT_Strategy_Review]], the Nigeria Data Protection Act is in effect but DavoDani Microfinance Bank's 2026 IT Strategy document does not address an [[NDPC Compliance]] strategy — flagged as a critical omission alongside [[Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity]].
