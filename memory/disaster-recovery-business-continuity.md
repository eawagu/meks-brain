Disaster Recovery (DR) and Business Continuity Planning (BCP) cover the systems, procedures, and infrastructure that allow an organization to recover from disruption (system failure, cyber incident, natural event) and continue critical operations.

For a financial institution, DR/BCP is non-negotiable — both because customer funds and PII are at stake, and because [[CBN]] regulations require it.

## Components
- Backup strategy (frequency, retention, off-site/off-region storage)
- Failover infrastructure (hot/warm/cold sites, RPO/RTO targets)
- Documented runbooks for incident response
- Regular DR drills
- Business continuity procedures for critical operations during outages
- Communication plan for customers and regulators
