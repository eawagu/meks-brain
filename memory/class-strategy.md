## Overview

Class Strategy is the framework being developed to select Moniepoint's next international markets after [[Kenya Launch]]. A new hire is developing the framework, due upon June resumption (after the [[Platformization]] change freeze).

## Signal Inputs

- **Post-Kenya list**: South Africa, Morocco, Kenya, DRC (DRC flagged as Nigeria-like margin opportunity — "polarized, high-margin").
- **Smaller markets** can offer lower revenue but better margins + less competition.
- **Per-country cost falls** as platformization lowers incremental-market cost.
- **Digital strategy** raises entry barriers via concentration (fewer higher-retention customers) vs. broad POS dispersion (acts like an "advert" inviting attacks).
