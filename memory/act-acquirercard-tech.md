## Definition

**ACT** (Acquirer / Card Tech) is the [[TeamApt]] acquiring platform under [[TPP|Third-Party Processing]]. It enables KYC-gated merchant onboarding and currently uses manual fee collection. ACT is one of the core systems in [[Moniepoint]]'s TPP department alongside Aptent, EMV Data Prep, and Rsync CMS.

## Bank-partner adoption

- [[Fidelity Bank]] (Fidencia) ACT adoption — observed in the Apr 20 ATPP standup track.
- AC ACT onboarding — mentioned in the Apr 20 standup track.
- Continued operationalization for bank partners observed Apr 27 ("operationalizing ACT for bank partners").

## ATPP track status

### 2026-04-27 standup

- **ACT enhancements deployed successfully** including:
  - **Conversion currency** capability
  - **User room management**
- Operational + technical issues: teams resolving database errors and account deactivations following repository access issues; efforts focus on restoring codebases and operationalizing ACT for bank partners.
