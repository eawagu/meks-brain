## Summary
A 15-slide Google Slides deck titled "OKRS And Ops To-do" submitted by [[Tolulope Obianwu]] as part of her evidence trail response to the PIP. The deck covers 8 product/service lines with operational KRs and a 2026 roadmap. Assessment: substantially broader than initially read, but does not meet the PIP requirement because KRs are largely activities rather than measurable outcomes, lacks department-level consolidation, and does not cascade from [[TeamApt]]'s business framework.

## Key Points
- **Product lines covered (8):** PTSP, Direct Debit, ATS, Monnify (Channel/Onboarding, Disbursement, Card & Account), Juliana Switch/NSS (4 banks), TPP (Acquiring Ops, Issuing Ops), plus 2026 roadmap
- **PTSP KRs:** Zero revenue leakage, SLA for issue resolution, Operations Doctrine for PTSP
- **Direct Debit KRs:** Enhance portal (download reports, hourly/24h cycles), support desk setup, standardized SOPs
- **ATS KRs:** Connect Juliana Switch to ATS, process 1000+ pilot transactions at >99% success
- **Juliana Switch/NSS:** FCMB NSS go-live across Card/DD/Account Transfers; Fidelity Bank TACHA + Account Switch; Sterling Bank Account Switch by March 31, 2026; Union Bank Card and DD
- **TPP Acquiring:** TACHA records, MPGS dashboard visibility, Visa + Mastercard dispute API integration
- **2026 Roadmap:** Q1 — process mapping, bottleneck identification, Zendesk migration; Q2 — automation, inbound support team, reconciliation tool, CSAT deployment
- **Gaps identified:** Format mismatch (activities not outcomes), no cascade from business framework, KR numbering inconsistencies, no cross-functional sign-off, roadmap is directional without targets
