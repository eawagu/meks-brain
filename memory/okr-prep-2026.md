## Summary

Strategic OKR preparation document by Frank Atashili covering TeamApt's 2026 business priorities across five pillars. Revenue target is ₦41.56Bn (up from ₦8.9Bn real in 2025) with expected spend of ₦38.69Bn. Growth plan centers on converting all ATS banks to Direct Debit, expanding NSS to all ATS banks, TPP acquirer processing expansion, and building tokenization capability. Protection covers regulatory renewals (switching license by Aug 2026, PCIDSS by Apr 2026, ISO certifications), LOOM fraud monitoring integration, and Chinese wall with Moniepoint. New business includes issuer processing for MoniePoint (Q2), Visa ecommerce acquiring, tokenization certification, and PTSP expansion.

## Key Points

- 2025 real revenue: ₦8.9Bn (ATS ₦2.68Bn, Monnify ₦5.87Bn, Account Switch ₦310Mn, Juliana ₦0)
- 2025 indirect revenue: ₦8.45Bn (Investments ₦2.76Bn, TSA ₦5.69Bn)
- 2026 expected revenue: ₦41.56Bn (Monnify ₦13.03Bn, ATS ₦3.1Bn, DD ₦1.78Bn, Juliana ₦1.3Bn, Account Switch ₦820Mn, PTSP ₦528Mn, TPP ₦18.78Bn)
- 2025 real spend: ₦24.7Bn; 2025 real profitability: -₦7Bn
- Growth: Convert all ATS banks to DD (Q1: FCMB, Zenith, GTB; Q2: Wema, Union, Sterling + rest)
- NSS bank-by-bank rollout: Q1 (FCMB, Keystone, Union, Stanbic) through Q4 (Ecobank)
- TPP acquirer processing Q3: Access, MoniePoint, Stanbic, Fidelity, FCMB, Polaris
- Regulatory: Switching license renewal by Aug 23 2026; PCIDSS renewal by Apr 2026; ISO 22301/27001/9001/20000/45001
- CBN audit exceptions: DCIR via ATS, CISO appointment
- New business: Issuer processor (MoniePoint Q2), Visa ecommerce acquiring, tokenization, 3DS ACS/MPI, PTSP (Polaris Q1)
- Note: "In expanding direct debit, be silent on onboarding billers" — potential landmine with bill payments business
