## Overview

OHSMS — the Occupational Health and Safety Management System — is the management framework an organization uses to prevent work-related injury and ill health. At [[TeamApt]] it is one of five management systems within the [[Integrated Management System]] (IMS).

## 2026-04-30 — Certification Recommendation

The 2026-04-30 [[ISO Audit]] close-out session **recommended certifying TeamApt's OHSMS against [[ISO 45001:2018]]**, alongside continued maintenance of the other four IMS standards. One of the 10 next-step remediation actions targets OHS-policy training-effectiveness verification: *"Implement measures to verify training effectiveness on occupational health and safety policies."* Source: [[ISO Audit-Close out session – 2026_04_30 16_27 WAT – Notes by Gemini]].
