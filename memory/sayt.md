## Overview

SAYT (Save As You Transact, variously rendered S.A.Y.T. / SOYT / SOIT in transcripts) is [[Moniepoint]]'s transactional-savings product. Customers accumulate savings passively via everyday business transactions.

## Status (Day 1 Retreat 2026)

- **~800,000 users** post-revamp data cleanup (down from >1M of "false reports").
- Growth pattern: **cyclical** — accumulates through the month, dips as funds are deployed for business expenses (e.g., salaries).

## BO Account Decommissioning

The legacy [[Business Owner Account]] into which SAYT auto-withdrew funds has been decommissioned for new customers. Key decision at Day 1 retreat:
- New SAYT customers are no longer exposed to the BO account.
- Existing users remain on the legacy path for now — migration planned.
- Auto-withdrawal was the source of the persistent "Where is my money?" complaint.
