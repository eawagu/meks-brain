## Summary

The [[TeamApt]] Operations team reviews 2025 OKR performance (all KRs achieved: ITSM adoption, Monnify team restructuring, process automation, SOP documentation, TPP/Juliana go-live) and proposes 2026 annual objectives across four areas: zero revenue leakage, process simplification, automation, and cross-functional synergy.

## Key Points

- **2025 achievements**: ITSM adopted company-wide with full Change Management adoption. Monnify team restructured — routine tasks transferred to CS, refund processes optimized. Key processes automated: bulk settlement updates, settlement notifications (Olam), sub-account cycle updates, chargebacks, USSD reversals, HabariPay/Etranzact payouts, Moniepoint virtual account transactions. All Ops and CS processes documented in SOPs. TPP Moniepoint, TPP Monnify, Juliana Account Paystack went live (Juliana Opay discontinued).
- **Rolling into 2026**: Cross-functional synergy, migration to TeamApt Zendesk, building direct bank operational relationships, establishing inbound customer support team, clear performance management indicators, support agent competency assessment, support for other business units.
- **2026 O1 — Zero Revenue Leakage**: Revenue leakage ≤0.1% of monthly transaction value, 100% exception detection/escalation within 24hrs (≥98% resolution within SLA), 100% fee/charge accuracy (≤0.05% variance).
- **2026 O2 — Simplify for Excellence**: Reduce process cycle time by 10%, eliminate ≥20% non-value-adding steps, quarterly leakage audits with 100% critical process coverage, ≥50% automation of SLA tracking/exception resolution/chargebacks/reversals.
- **2026 O3 — Automate Process**: ≥30% automation of core operational processes, ≥50% reduction in manual intervention, 40% automated reporting for SLA/incidents/metrics with real-time dashboards.
- **2026 O4 — Cross-Functional Synergy**: ≥85% cross-functional survey feedback score, ≥80% multi-domain competency (min 2 functional areas), 100% RACI clarity for settlements/reconciliations/disputes, seamless NSS bank operationalization.
- Lessons learned: need for product knowledge training, better cross-collaboration, documented performance management, improved escalation metrics adoption.
