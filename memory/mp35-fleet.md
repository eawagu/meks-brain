## Overview

The **MP35 Fleet** is [[Moniepoint]]'s primary POS terminal family. Includes MP35D and MP35P variants among others.

## Scale (April 2026)

- Total procured: ~5M units.
- Deployed: ~3.3M.
- MP35D: ~2.2M total, ~1.46M deployed.
- Repaired MP35 undeployed: 163K.
- Faulty in DNS: ~176K (>140K at repair centers).
- MP35P specifically: >100K hard to repair.
- ~37K refurbished H9 Pro / Morphon (related fleet) not redeployed.
- Top-wise MP35 terminals MPV: $13M at end of March.
- Active MP35P ~880K, inactive ~300K+ (figures disputed; consolidation action pending).
- NBV dispute: 22B vs ~50B.

## Issues

- **MP35P 3G limitation** ([[MP35P 4G Upgrade]]): discontinue or upgrade on repair.
- **Market perception penalty**: small reliability differences drive user attitudes; competitor [[OPay]] demarketing compounds this.
- **Idle inventory**: 160K+ refurbished not redeployed.
- **Facilities strain**: Magodo and other repair centers overwhelmed.
