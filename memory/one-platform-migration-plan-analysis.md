## Summary

Evaluation of Ravi's 6-month Strangler Fig migration plan consolidating Nigeria/UK/Kenya platforms.

## Key Points

- Stage 1 (2 mo): Core capabilities, Kenya discovery
- Stage 2 (3 mo): Capability decomposition, merge, alpha launch
- Stage 3 (1 mo): Beta, commercial rollout
- Change freeze during Stages 2-3 to protect Nigeria roadmap
- Risks: timeline ambition, TSP scope, Kenya underspecification, leadership transition
