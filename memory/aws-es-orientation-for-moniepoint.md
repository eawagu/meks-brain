## Summary

A 39-slide orientation presentation delivered to [[Moniepoint]] by [[AWS]] Enterprise Support TAM [[Abhinav Arora]] on 23 September 2025. It outlines the full Enterprise Support value proposition: TAM role, support tiers, proactive services, incident detection and response (IDR), Trusted Advisor, concierge billing support, and the phased 90-day engagement plan.

## Key Points

- Enterprise Support provides a designated TAM, 15-minute response for business-critical system down, and unlimited proactive engagements
- TAM engagement follows a phased approach: Day 1 (intro), Day 30 (workload overview), Day 60 (tool workshops), Day 90 (operations review + strategic support planning)
- AWS Incident Detection & Response (IDR) is priced at the greater of $7K/month or 2% of enrolled workload AWS usage
- IDR onboarding includes workload discovery, alarm definition, integration testing, and custom runbooks — full observability takes ~2 months post-onboarding
- Proactive services include Well-Architected Reviews, SCRaM (tabletop incident exercises), Security Improvement Program (SIP), and AWS Countdown for large-scale events
- Concierge team handles billing inquiries, Savings Plans, Reserved Instances, and cost optimization recommendations
- AWS Unified Operations offers 5-minute incident response with a dedicated Engagement Delivery Manager, Support Engineer, and Incident Manager
