## Instance: TeamApt Tech Function Migration (2026-04-16)

All tech functions under Mek's [[TeamApt|TeamApt Limited]] CTO role are moving to the [[Platform Organization]], except [[Operations]]. This is a platform-centric consolidation \u2014 technical functions centralized under a platform org while the CTO retains direct ownership of Operations.

## Instance: TeamApt Platformization Org Movements (2026-04-22)

[[Frank Atashili]]'s [[TeamApt-Platformization-Org-Movements (1)|Apr 22, 2026 brief]] to [[Dennis Ajalie]] and [[Pawel Swiatek]] operationalises the tech function migration above on the people side. Phase 1 is strike-team-only (~15 of ~200 P&E staff move now):

- **OpCo consolidation:** 4 businesses collapse to 2 \u2014 [[Switching & Processing]] (merger of TPP + DS) under [[Babatunde Okufi]] (CBDO double-hat); Monnify + Direct Debit under [[Daniel Ojinaka]] (dual business lead).
- **Three leader conversions (OpCo \u2192 IPCo):** [[Frank Atashili]] \u2192 Head [[TSPP]] Product; [[Tracy Ojaigho]] \u2192 Head [[CI&P]]; [[Damilare Ogunnaike]] \u2192 Head [[Payment Gateway Platform]].
- **Strike teams:** [[TSPP]] Phase 1 (Frank / [[Alex Adeyemo]] / Design TBD / [[Oluwabunmi Oyefisayo]] Lead Tech PM); [[CI&P]] Phase 1 with [[Ketan Dhamasana]] transferring as EM under [[Emeka Awagu]] + Tracy.
- **Hold-the-fort:** [[Wycliffe Ochieng']] EM (Switch + ATS), [[Abdulgafar Obeitor|Gafar]] + AptPay BAU engineers.
- **OpCo retains:** licences (CBN Switching/Processing/PSSP/PTSP), customers & BD, operations ([[Oladapo Onayemi]] pillar), compliance ([[Ibukun Atoyebi]]), audit ([[Olufemi Agbaje]]), people ops (Constance \u2014 reporting moves to Frank).
- **Timeline:** Q2 2026 boundaries + TSPP strike team stood up; Mid-2026 capability specs in Phoenix format; Late 2026 first alpha via [[Strangler Fig]]; 2027 steady state.
