## Overview

**Platform Organization** is the **central, group-level** organizational unit in the [[Moniepoint Group Platformization|Moniepoint Group]] that houses all platform engineering and product functions under [[Project Phoenix]]. It is **not** a TeamApt-internal unit \u2014 it operates at the group level, with [[Moniepoint Technologies UK]] (IPCo) as the legal home for platform IP.

The Platform Organization serves all group business entities (OpCos) as tenants: [[Moniepoint MFB]] (Nigeria), [[TeamApt]] (Nigeria), [[MonieWorld]] (UK), [[Sumac MFB]] (Kenya), and future country entities.

## Structure (per March 2026 Platforms Conference deck)

Led by a **Chief Platforms & Products Officer**. Three operating divisions plus cross-cutting departments:

### Business Banking Platforms
- [[TSP|Transaction Switching & Processing (TSP)]] \u2014 foundational payment kernel (Card Switching, Account Switching, VAS Switching, Integrations sub-platforms)
- Card Acceptance & Processing
- Payment Gateway
- POS Hardware & Devices
- [[Card Issuance & Processing Platform]]
- Cross-Border Payments

### Digital Banking Platforms
(Headed by [[Tracy Ojaigho]])
- Customer Platforms (Mobile App, Web App, POS App, CRM/Product Instrumentation, Design System)
- Banking Operations Platforms (Spend Mgmt, VAS, Savings, Identity/Onboarding/Shell, Rewards)

### Credit Platforms
- TBD nodes

### Cross-cutting CoEs / Departments
- Banking & Ledger Platform (CBA)
- Sales Mgmt Tools (MonieCRM)
- Customer Support Platform (MonieDesk)
- Finance Systems
- Field Verification Platform
- Financial Crime Prevention (Loom)
- MOOS
- Business Management Platform (Moniebook)

## Triad Leadership Model

Each platform node is led by a triad:
- **Product Leader**
- **Engineering Leader**
- **Design Leader**

Example for CI&P: Tracy Ojaigho (Platform / Product) + [[Emeka Awagu]] (Engineering) + Design Lead TBD.

## Relationship to TeamApt

Within TeamApt specifically, per the 2026-04-16 internal restructuring note, all technology functions under [[Emeka Awagu]]'s CTO role are moving **to the Platform Organization** (i.e., are being absorbed into the central group platform org). [[Operations]] is explicitly excluded and remains directly under the CTO at TeamApt.

This positions TeamApt as a business entity (OpCo) that consumes group platforms, with its prior in-house tech functions transitioning into the central platform.

## Strategic Purpose

Enable country expansion without code duplication. A new country becomes a configuration profile, not a new build. One configurable product, built centrally, consumed by in-country business entities as tenants.
