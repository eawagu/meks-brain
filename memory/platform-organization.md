The Platform Organization is the organizational unit at [[TeamApt]] designated to receive all technology functions that were previously under Mek's CTO role. [[Operations]] is explicitly excluded from this transfer and remains under the CTO.

This restructuring (as of 2026-04-16) positions the Platform Organization as the primary home for technical functions at TeamApt, representing a platform-centric operating model.
