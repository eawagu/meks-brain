## Overview
Visa Payments Forum Paris is a Visa-hosted industry conference taking place 30 June – 2 July 2026 at the [[Paris Convention Centre]] (1 Place de la Porte de Versailles, 75015 Paris, France). The event features Visa executives and industry leaders presenting on payments-industry trends and innovations, hands-on demonstrations, and networking with Visa specialists and peers.

## Participation
- [[Emeka Awagu]] is an invitee; attendance is contingent on his [[French Schengen visa application]]. Travel and accommodation are attendee-funded.
