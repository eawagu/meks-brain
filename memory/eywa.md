## Definition

Eywa is [[Moniepoint]]'s **AI artifact framework** — the intelligence layer that delivers on [[Project Phoenix]]'s second pillar (AI-nativeness). Claude-backed.

## Origin

Feb 16–20, 2026 — [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] declared the AI-first mandate at Moniepoint: all functional managers rebuild workflows using AI via Eywa. This was framed as a prerequisite for the Phoenix transformation, not a parallel track.

## Operational Pattern

- Access coordinated through [[Michael Afolabi]] (see [[AI-Native Operations]])
- Embedded across CI&P (chargeback resolution, logistics, ticketing) and design workflows (see [[Phoenix Design Workflow and Handoff]])
- Functional managers expected to redesign their own workflows on top of Eywa rather than wait for centrally-built AI features

## Strategic Framing

Under Phoenix the group's two pillars are:
1. **Platformization** — consolidate duplicate builds across group companies into shared multi-tenant platforms
2. **AI-nativeness** — Eywa

Eywa is the operational mechanism for pillar 2. A new country becomes a configuration profile (pillar 1) with AI-native workflows built in (pillar 2).
