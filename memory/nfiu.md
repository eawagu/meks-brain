## Overview

The NFIU (Nigerian Financial Intelligence Unit) is Nigeria's central AML/CFT agency that receives Suspicious Transaction Reports (STRs) and Currency Transaction Reports (CTRs).

## Moniepoint Work With NFIU

- Moniepoint developed a bulk upload feature to work with NFIU, helping analysts review and complete reports faster
- STR volume increased dramatically: 774 (all of prior year) → 514 (Q1 current year)
- Focus on report quality alongside quantity
