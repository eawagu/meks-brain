## Overview

"Internal Meeting" is a generic title applied by the [[Gemini Meeting Notes Pipeline]] when a Google Meet session has no explicit meeting topic configured. Such meetings surface in ingress as Drive-stub markdown files with metadata-only content (file id, view URL, owner, modified timestamp); substantive content lives in the Drive document itself.
