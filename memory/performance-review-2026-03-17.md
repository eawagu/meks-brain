## Summary
A 1hr 14min performance review with [[Dennis Ajalie]], [[Emeka Awagu]], [[Constance Onyeji-Jarret]], and [[Tolulope Obianwu]]. The core concern is ownership of the settlement engine and NSS bank relationships — [[Tolulope Obianwu]] took an advisory role rather than Director-level ownership. Abrasive communication and OKR non-submission are the other two pillars. [[Tolulope Obianwu]] provided upward feedback on missed 1:1s and rigid communication from [[Emeka Awagu]].

## Key Points
- **Settlement engine ownership:** [[Dennis Ajalie]] expects Director-level "mini CEO" behavior — proactive ownership without explicit direction; [[Tolulope Obianwu]] assumed project delivery staff handled bank relationships
- **NSS bank relationships:** Project delivery staff were managing bank contacts instead of [[Tolulope Obianwu]]; Dennis views this as abdication
- **"I'm not a salesperson":** Statement made by [[Tolulope Obianwu]] about bank relationships — Dennis views this as "docking leadership"
- **Communication feedback:** Persistent external and internal reports of abrasiveness; requires behavioural change regardless of intent
- **OKR non-submission:** [[Tolulope Obianwu]] claimed she had dropped OKR presentation in PMO folder — she hadn't; [[Emeka Awagu]] challenged her in the meeting; this flags a candor concern
- **[[Emeka Awagu]]'s position:** Stressed need for better product-ops collaboration; operational requirements should be established at product lifecycle start, not near launch; notes Tolu preferred things "coming to them"
- **Tolu's defense:** NSS ownership expectations were unclear; "not a salesperson" = role boundary clarification; aggression feedback is subjective; heavily under-resourced; upward feedback channel is absent
- **Upward feedback on [[Emeka Awagu]]:** 1:1 more productive with Dennis; Mek's communication is "rigid," difficult to distinguish feedback from chastisement; missed 1:1 meetings created a gap
- **[[Constance Onyeji-Jarret]]'s role:** Mediation; committed to reestablishing Mek/Tolu 1:1s (mornings, weekly/biweekly)
