## Dynamic

At the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]] (Day 1), competitor [[OPay]] was named as the primary demarketer of [[Moniepoint]] terminals:
- Claims Moniepoint's 3G / Android 5 terminals are illegal.
- Orchestrates swap campaigns against Moniepoint-deployed devices.
- Targets Moniepoint merchants for replacement regardless of Moniepoint's device retrieval activity.

## Why It Compounds

Device retrievals for qualification issues signal accounts to competitors. Without proactive retention outreach, retrieved merchants become replacement targets. Worst-case floated: ~$30M impact — potentially absorbable over time via storage/write-back mechanics.

## Moniepoint Counter

- Regulatory engagement to challenge 3G/Android 5 legality claims.
- Proactive retention outreach when retrievals happen.
- Controlled 4-year MP35 write-off contingent on regulatory relief.
