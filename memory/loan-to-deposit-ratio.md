## Overview

The Loan-to-Deposit Ratio is a prudential ratio measuring the share of deposits deployed as loans. Moniepoint's posture at Day 3 retreat 2026:

- Current: 36%
- Target: 80%
- Treated as a "preferable failure" — missing the target is preferred over accumulating bad loans

## Strategic Framing

Capital ratio is separately a breach concern. Loan-to-deposit remains conservative on purpose to protect credit quality while the rest of the business grows.
