## Overview
Switch monitoring is the operational visibility layer over [[TeamApt Limited]] / [[Moniepoint]] switching infrastructure — back-office consoles, dashboards, alerting, and instrumentation that demonstrate the platform is being actively observed. Switching & Processing is one of TeamApt's four core CBN licenses, so the absence of monitoring on the switch is a material regulatory exposure.

## State (Apr 2026)

**Q1 OKR miss carried forward:** the switch monitoring objective was not delivered in Q1 2026; per [[Adefemi Opeogun]] at the Apr 27 [[Fraud monitoring plan and concerns - 2026_04_27 14_11 WAT - Notes by Gemini|stakeholder call]] (companion: [[Fraud Monitoring Plan and Concerns 2026-04-27]]): "we cannot leave it open for Q2 as well."

**Strategic delivery posture aligned (Apr 27, 2026) — partial-but-visible over wait-and-perfect:** the team committed to producing *something demonstrable* before the next [[CBN Audit]] — even a back-office system or dashboard view — rather than holding for a fully complete monitoring solution. Verbatim framing: "having nothing for switching is a bad exposure" because the switching license is core; even a partial view "is something we can show." Direction to [[Titus Adenugba]] (Moniepoint engineer): "go and perform magic."
