## Summary

Settlement Account Management (SAM) aggregates cleared transactions and calculates net positions. Six daily GCMS clearing cycles (18:00-08:00 CT), T+1 settlement. Three models: two-party direct, three-party with correspondent bank, consolidated through parent principal. Funds via Fedwire (USD), ACH, SWIFT, or local systems.

## Key Points

- Net settlement per acquirer-issuer pair per day per currency with bilateral netting
- Settlement advisements are legally binding authority for fund movement
- Six primary clearing cycles daily feed settlement
- Multi-currency reconciliation supported
