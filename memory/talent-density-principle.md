## Principle

At the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]], [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] stated: if a manager finds themselves needing to actively manage an employee's performance, that person should be removed. At senior levels, managers should operate fire-and-forget.

## Consequences of Tolerating Underperformance

- Manager absorbs the employee's responsibilities.
- Toxicity + unhappiness for both parties.
- Entire organization slows.
- Temporary performance improvements tend to regress.

## Acknowledged Tension

Finding good replacements is hard — which is why solving talent acquisition ([[Chloe]]'s mandate) is critical. No sacrifice of talent density for staffing.
