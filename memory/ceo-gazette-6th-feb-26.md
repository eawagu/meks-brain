## Summary
Weekly executive gazette for week ending Feb 6, 2026. North Star: Monnify Collections hit 400K daily transaction volume Feb 3. Notable signals: Feb 3 NIBSS impact on disbursements + Wema VA downtime; JULS WEMA extreme card failure instability (50.96% avg, 76.92% peak); gateway onboarding comparative analysis; new hire Abayomi Ojamomi (Lead, Data Engineering).

## Key Points
- **North Star:** [[Monnify]] Collections hit **400K transaction volume/day on Feb 3**.
- **Customer incidents Feb 3:** [[NIBSS]] issue impacted disbursement service — merchants had pending/failed disbursements to other bank accounts. Wema Virtual Account failures due to bank downtime (confirmed by [[Wema Bank]]).
- **CSAT:** 95.9% (trending down), 117 tickets, 5 good / 3 bad.
- **Monnify acquisition:** 791 sign-ups, 94 KYC rejected, 32 activations, 4% conversion. Avg approval 53 wks. 19 merchants went live. Virtual AC utilization **14.05% (up from prior week's 10.21%)**. Top new merchant: Afri-Millions Limited (56/59 txns).
- **Gateway onboarding comparison (Monnify vs Flutterwave/Paystack/Interswitch):**
  - Most gateways require Certificate of Incorporation.
  - Monnify + Flutterwave more comprehensive than Paystack on corporate docs.
  - Starter/unregistered business requirements mostly consistent across gateways.
  - Paystack has higher 51% shareholder identity threshold.
  - **Gap: Monnify lacks Directors/Shareholders Proof of Address requirement.**
- **Disbursement:** Peak success 99.24% Feb 1 (1.19M successful). Low 98.08% Feb 4. Highest volume 1.40M Feb 5. NIP + Hydrogen_Pay dominate (Feb 5: 48.71% + 45.25%). NIP peak 102,326 Jan 30; Hydrogen_Pay peak 96,928 Jan 31. PalmPay ~4%.
- **Virtual Account Collections:** Moniepoint 1.60M, Sterling 786K, Wema 156K, Fidelity 3,736, GTB 742. Daily peak 416K Feb 3. Daily low 299K Feb 1.
- **Card failure:**
  - **[[JULS WEMA]] extreme instability — 50.96% avg failure, 36.71 std dev. 76.92% peak Feb 3 → 25.0% Feb 4.**
  - **HABARIPAY** most reliable 1.41% avg.
  - **[[ISW]] benchmark** — 2.87% avg, std dev 0.24.
- **DD Mandate:** CREATE_MANDATE 100% success. **DEBIT_MANDATE dipped to 62.19% Feb 2**, recovered >93% by Feb 5.
- **Compliance:** No update — team annual strategy session.
- **Third-party processing:** No IPM file issues. **New observation: transfer charges being debited for onward settlement to partners — escalated to finance team.**
- **Bank reconciliation:**
  - [[Access Bank]] — awaiting transit account updates for last reconciliations. Exceptions shared with DD team.
  - [[Fidelity Bank]] — some transactions in transaction report lack matching parameters in transit account; inconsistent matching parameters; reconciliation blocked pending resolution.
- **Recovery:** Flat at 73.35% / 73.48%. Same issues as prior week (Polaris ₦159M, Sterling pre-2025 ₦8.52B, automated settlement pending).
- **Chargeback:** [[ISW]] 0.60%, Habari 0.08%, Juliana 0.57%, CoralPay-USD 0.00%. 27.8% reduction in chargebacks vs prior week.
- **People:** Abayomi Ojamomi joined as **Lead, Data Engineering**. Work-from-office day Feb 20 announced.
