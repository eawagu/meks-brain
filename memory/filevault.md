## Overview
[[Filevault]] is the disk encryption solution enabled on [[TeamApt]] laptops.

## Role in IMS Controls
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], Filevault is named in the ISMS risk register as a data-protection control for Laptops and Endpoints (risks: device theft, unauthorized software installation — rated Moderate). Paired with remote-wipe capability on missing devices and [[Sentinel]] endpoint protection for malware scanning.
