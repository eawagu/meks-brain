## Overview
[[Sentinel]] is the endpoint protection solution deployed on [[TeamApt]] laptops.

## Role in IMS Controls
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], Sentinel is named in the ISMS risk register as a control for Laptops and Endpoints (risks: device theft, unauthorized software installation — rated Moderate). Complementary controls: [[Filevault]] for data protection and remote wipe capability for reported missing devices.
