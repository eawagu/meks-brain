## Overview
CBN audit refers to external regulatory inspection of [[TeamApt Limited]] / [[Moniepoint]] by the [[CBN]] (Central Bank of Nigeria). Two flavors operate concurrently:

1. **Scheduled cycles** — annual CBN IT Standards audit (most recently positively closed Jan 23, 2026 per [[CEO Gazette — 23rd Jan 26]]); Payment System Supervision Department face-to-face reviews (Feb 12, 2026 covering switching transactions, partnerships, product pricing).
2. **Ad-hoc / special inspection** — CBN can call special inspections at any time, with little notice. This is the operational pressure source that drives audit-readiness urgency outside the scheduled calendar.

## State (Apr 2026)

**Imminent ad-hoc threat framing (per [[Adefemi Opeogun]], Apr 27 fraud monitoring call — see [[Fraud monitoring plan and concerns - 2026_04_27 14_11 WAT - Notes by Gemini]] / [[Fraud Monitoring Plan and Concerns 2026-04-27]]):**
