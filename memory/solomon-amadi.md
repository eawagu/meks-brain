## Overview

Solomon Amadi is the **Business Owner of the Payments Business at [[Moniepoint MFB]]** — the bank (microfinance bank) subsidiary within the Moniepoint group. His scope is card payment transactions at the bank, making him the internal customer for any card transaction flow passing through TeamApt's [[Domestic Switching]] department (e.g., Stanbic card transactions on Moniepoint terminals).

## Relevance to Stanbic Routing-Restoration Escalation (2026-04-16)

Solomon is a fourth escalation vector for the Stanbic RC91 routing-restoration automation ask — distinct from the engineering-org paths (direct peer to [[Babatunde Okufi]] as CBDO/Domestic Switching owner, dotted-line to [[Felix Ike]] as Group CTO, up through [[Dennis Ajalie]]).
