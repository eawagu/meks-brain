# Moniepoint Group CEO Gazette

Weekly cadence update from [[Tosin Eniolorunda|Moniepoint Group CEO]] covering platform rail health, settlement performance, counterparty exposure recoveries, and open incidents. Scope spans the Moniepoint Group: TeamApt, Moniepoint MFB, and other entities.

## Recurring sections observed
- **Executive Summary** — Top Win, Top Risk, North Star Metric (24 Apr issue introduced this section explicitly)
- **Departmental Deep-Dives** — customer impact, support ticket health, CSAT
- **Monnify Acquisition** — sign-ups, KYC rejections, activations, conversion, time-to-live, time-to-first-transaction, top transacting new merchants
- **Reserve Account Utilization** — generated/paid/% utilization
- **Rail health** — ATS, NIBSS, JULS ACCESS, JULS WEMA, JULS FCMB, T-Switch, ISW, UPSL
- **PTSA Compliance Monitor** — share distribution + per-processor success rates
- **Disbursement Success Rate** — Monnify payout health
- **Virtual Accounts** — Moniepoint vs Sterling share
- **Card Transaction Failure Rate** — JULS, ISW, HABARIPAY
- **DD Mandate Performance** — approval rate, success rate, mandate creation volume
- **Compliance** — ISO audits, bank compliance testing, regulator submissions (CBN/NRS)
- **Recovery / Operations** — Cowrywise exposure, JumiaPay exposure
- **People** — short status
- **Asks** — CEO requests
