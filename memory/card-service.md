## Overview

**Card Service** is the core commercial platform for [[Moniepoint MFB]]'s card selling operations. Owned by Team 1 (Card Sales & Distribution) under PM [[Nadeem Abbas]].

## Scope

- Sharing formulas (agent / PRM / BO splits)
- Pricing tiers
- Agent / PRM / BO commission structures

Commercial decisions are made jointly with the Growth Team before being implemented on this system.

## Position in the MFB Cards Stack

Sits inside the Sales & Distribution side of the MFB Cards stack (alongside [[Logistics Service]] and [[Card Integration Service]]). Distinct from the Infrastructure side (which owns [[Card Manager Service]], [[Card Transaction Service]], etc.).
