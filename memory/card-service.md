Card Service is the core platform for card-selling operations in [[Moniepoint MFB]], owned by the Card Sales & Distribution team under [[Nadeem Abbas]] (PM) and [[Olufemi Davies]] (Head).

## Functions

- Manages all commercial configurations:

- Pricing and commission decisions are made **jointly with the Growth Team** before being implemented on this system.
