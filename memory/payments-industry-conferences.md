## Overview
Payments industry conferences are gatherings where payments executives, network operators (e.g. Visa, Mastercard), fintechs, and issuers/acquirers exchange views on trends, regulation, and technology innovations in the payments ecosystem. For [[Emeka Awagu]] as CTO of [[TeamApt]], attendance is industry-networking and roadmap-signal intake.

## Active Engagements
