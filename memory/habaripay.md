## Overview

HABARIPAY (HabariPay) is a payment provider integrated with [[TeamApt Limited]] / [[Moniepoint]] as a switch-to-switch partner (GTBank route) and a Direct Debit Payment Facilitator. Banks not on TeamApt's ATS can integrate via Coral Pay / HabariPay partnerships (per [[TeamApt MANCo Meeting - 31 March 2026]]).

## Performance signals

- **Week of Apr 24, 2026:** card failure rate held steady at 0.72% (slight improvement WoW). Insulated from the [[JULS Card Crisis]] that hit JULS ACCESS (68.18%) and JULS FCMB (25%) on Apr 23. Confirms the fault line is JULS-specific, not industry-wide. (Source: [[CEO Gazette - 24th April 26]].)
