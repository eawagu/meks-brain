## Overview

Tracy Ojaigho is the **Head of CI&P Product** — Product Leader for the [[Card Issuance & Processing Platform]] at the central [[Moniepoint Group Platformization|Moniepoint platform organization]] (Moniepoint Technologies UK / IPCo). The CI&P platform is a multi-tenant central platform serving [[Moniepoint MFB]], [[TeamApt]], and future market entities (UK, Kenya) as tenants.

CI&P sits within the broader [[Digital Banking Platforms]] cluster (head TBD). Tracy's scope is CI&P specifically — not the parent cluster.

Before Phoenix formalized the group platform org, Tracy led TeamApt's TPP (Third Party Processing) department. Per Frank's Apr 22, 2026 [[TeamApt-Platformization-Org-Movements (1)|Org Movements brief]], her role change is formally confirmed: she leaves TPP commercial/P&L (which folds into the merged Switching & Processing OpCo business under [[Babatunde Okufi]]) and takes platform-level ownership of CMS, EMV prep, card dispute, 3DS, and Authorization Engine across markets.

### Relationship with MFB Cards
Under Phoenix, Tracy is **co-lead with [[Olufemi Davies]]** on the Cards domain: Tracy leads Product (central platform build); Femi continues to lead Business (MFB cards commercial + operational outcomes). Not a succession. The split is by function.

### Phase 1 team (per Apr 22 brief)
- Team 1 Card Processing — Authorization Engine, 3DS/SCA, Card Dispute; Phase 1 strike team spec-and-builds. TeamApt Acquirer Processing team stays in TeamApt on BAU; absorbed in later phases.
- Team 2 Card Issuance — CMS, Card Controls, EMV Data Prep, Perso interface. Phase 1: [[Ketan Dhamasana]] transfers as EM into her engineering team (from AptPay Suite). Issuer Processing and CMS teams stay in TeamApt for now; absorbed in later phases.
- Strike teams draw a handful of TeamApt EMs/ICs plus Cards (MoniePoint MFB) engineers.
