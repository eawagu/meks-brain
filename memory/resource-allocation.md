## What It Is

Resource allocation at Moniepoint/TeamApt refers to how engineering headcount, capacity, and skills are distributed across business lines and strategic initiatives. It is a recurring decision domain — spanning strike team formation, BAU continuity, restructuring, and EM coverage gaps.

## Design Principles

- **Legacy teams stay operational** — no code freeze during TSP migration; BAU continuity is a hard constraint
- **Strike team model** — small SEAL team (lean Phase 1), not full migration
- **Dual-reporting** — engineers report functionally to VP Engineering (Ravi) and business-line to their Head of Engineering
- **2 capable engineers + strong PM > multiple seniors** — founding principle; pulling too many seniors cripples current teams
