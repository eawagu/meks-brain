## Summary

OKR governance structure: two-level system (Annual Business Department OKRs + Quarterly Product Engineering Team OKRs). Scoring convention: ✅ Achieved ≥90%, 🟡 Partially ≥50%, ❌ Not Achieved <50%. Lists 2026 OKRs by department with availability status — several departments missing submissions (Domestic Switching business, MADD, GoSubscribe, SAFE, VAS).

## Key Points

- Two-level OKR system: Annual (Business Department) + Quarterly (Product Engineering Team)
- Scoring thresholds: ≥90% Achieved, ≥50% Partial, <50% Not Achieved
- 2026 OKR status: many submitted, several missing (Domestic Switching business, MADD, GoSubscribe, SAFE, VAS)
- Jira integration for tracking
- Cross-cutting standards across pillar teams (Engineering, SRE, Compliance)
