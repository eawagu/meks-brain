## Definition

The **Visa project** is the launch workload / validation programme for the new Phoenix [[Card Management System]]. The new CMS goes live on Visa first, then Tap-to-Pay, then Tokenization, then CX revamp.

## Cards KT — Apr 21 aligned decision

- **Strategic priorities for new platform rollout** (ALIGNED): platform rollout will launch using the Visa project, with primary strategic focus on migrating services to eliminate [[Postillion]] dependency and improving operational support for the chargeback and logistics teams.
- Source: [[Cards Team Structure Systems Roadmap - 2026-04-21 13:00 WAT]].

## Sequence

1. Visa launches on the new CMS (Reync / Phoenix CMS).
2. **[[Tap to pay]]** ships alongside Visa.
3. **[[Visa Tokenization]]** + Africa details follow.
4. **CX revamp** for card requests + dispute logging.

## Strategic role

Visa serves a dual purpose: it is both a **revenue product launch** and the **validation workload that proves the new CMS** before Tokenization, MFB cutover, and other markets layer on top.
