## Overview

In Q4 2025, [[Verve]] switched from flat pricing via a regulatory loophole, creating a material pricing shock that contributed to Moniepoint's PAT reversal.

## Regulatory Asymmetry

- **Acquirers**: price-capped AND must-accept-all-schemes — no leverage to reject.
- **Schemes**: NOT price-regulated — rent-seeking leverage available.
- The asymmetry enables the dispute.

## Moniepoint Position

- No rent-seeking posture; heritage of [[InterSwitch]]/[[Verve]] acknowledged but current tactics rejected.
- **Action**: [[Dennis Ajalie]] to negotiate Verve pricing down in exchange for committed volumes.
- Pursuing legal reforms and regulator education on the asymmetry.
