## Summary

Executive roadmap for migrating [[AptPay]]'s five products into a unified [[Transaction Switching Platform]] under [[Project Phoenix]]. Maps five products to six platform capabilities, documents current status (engineering ahead, governance behind), proposes three-phase modernization sequence.

## Key Points

- Five AptPay products mapped to six platform capabilities
- Account/Card Switching, VAS Processing, Settlement & Clearing, Dispute Resolution identified
- Tokenisation ownership conflict (three-way)
- TACS cross-cutting auth governance needed
- Juliana Card Switch 42–50% POS market dominance — non-negotiable constraint
- Engineering ahead of governance in readiness
- Phoenix-compliant governance framework proposed
