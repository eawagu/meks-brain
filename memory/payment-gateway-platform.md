## Overview

Payment Gateway Platform (PG Platform) is a group platform under [[Project Phoenix]] within the Business Banking Platforms cluster. The [[Monnify]] system becomes **PG Platform v0** — the starting substrate — and will be built out over time to subsume Monnify's payment rails.

## Leadership

| Role | Person |
|---|---|
| Platform Head | [[Damilare Ogunnaike]] (converting from Monnify Business Lead) |
| Engineering Head | TBD |

## System Seeds the Platform

- Monnify codebase becomes PG Platform v0
- Collections, disbursements, VAS capabilities transfer
- Platform consumes [[TSPP]] for switching/processing and [[CI&P]] for 3DS/SCA
- Phased: core team stays in TeamApt now, phased hand-off as platform matures

## Monnify Business Stays in TeamApt

- Merchant book, PayFac pipeline, commercial KPIs — stay in [[TeamApt]] OpCo
- [[Daniel Ojinaka]] picks up Monnify commercial alongside CDD (dual business lead)
- Onboarding, customer ops, front/back office remain OpCo
- Monnify consumes PG Platform services as they come online
