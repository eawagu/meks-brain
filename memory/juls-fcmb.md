## Overview

JULS FCMB is one of the JULS (Juliana Settlement) rails, routed through [[FCMB]]. Part of the JULS family of bank-specific settlement rails alongside [[JULS ACCESS]] and JULS WEMA.

## Performance Signals

**Mar 13, 2026 CEO Gazette:** 11.11–12.50% intermittent instability — flagged as a notable failure rate alongside the JULS ACCESS 32.58% spike on Mar 12.

Failure rates on JULS rails feed into the broader TeamApt switching reliability picture. Persistent instability indicates bank-side or integration-path issues that require bank engagement to resolve.
