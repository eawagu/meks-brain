## Overview

"Analyst Games" is the internal name for Moniepoint's planned system that auto-resolves low-priority compliance issues and escalates only high-priority cases to human analysts.

## Rationale

Free human analyst capacity to focus on high-priority/high-risk cases. Current bottleneck is engineering capacity to build the tooling.

## Platform Decision

To be built within Loom (vs. creating a separate product thread) — Loom should be AML-compliant.
