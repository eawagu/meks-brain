## Definition

**Change Management** in the [[Project Phoenix]] [[Card Issuance and Processing]] (CI&P) context is the workstream that addresses the organizational and people-side impact of the platform consolidation.

Scope:
- Organizational restructuring (e.g., MFB Cards Team transitioning to CI&P platform leadership)
- Team transitions (e.g., [[Ketan Dhamasana]] moving from [[AptPay Suite]] to CI&P Team 2 Card Issuance)
- Stakeholder communication strategies

## Ownership

[[Olufemi Davies]] — owns the change management plan per the 2026-04-27 CI&P kick-off action items: "Develop and execute change management plan for organizational restructuring."

Femi's positioning as Cards Business Leader at [[Moniepoint MFB]] — co-lead with [[Tracy Ojaigho]] under Phoenix (business vs. product split, not succession) — naturally places him as the right owner of the people-side transition for the MFB cards organisation moving into the central CI&P platform.

## Strike Team + Continuity model

Change Management aligns with the [[Strike Team + Continuity Operating Model]]: existing engineers maintain the legacy stack (continuity); a strike team builds the new central platform. Femi has already committed (per 2026-04-21 Cards KT) to announcing the strike-team formation to the existing MFB Cards Team and reassuring them that current operations will not be immediately affected.

## 2026-04-27 — kick-off scope

Change Management was established as one of the three cross-cutting workstreams alongside [[Governance Structure]] (owned by [[Tracy Ojaigho]]) and Hiring Roadmap (owned by [[Damilola Oyediran]]).
