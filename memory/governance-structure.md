## Definition

The **Governance Structure** for [[Project Phoenix]]'s [[Card Issuance and Processing]] (CI&P) platform was scoped at the 2026-04-27 CI&P kick-off meeting. It comprises three layers:

- **Steering committee** — oversees strategic decisions across CI&P.
- **Working groups** — manage specific domains (operations, infrastructure, product).
- **Escalation protocols** — pathway for issues that exceed working-group authority.

## Ownership

[[Tracy Ojaigho]] — owns the governance setup as part of the CI&P kick-off action items: "Set up governance structure for Project Phoenix including steering committee, working groups, and escalation protocols."

## Relationship to broader Phoenix governance

The broader [[Project Phoenix]] PM Governance Body is led by [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] and approves change-freeze exceptions during Stage 2 (Months 3–5). CI&P's governance structure plugs into this group platform-level body.

## 2026-04-27 — kick-off scope

Governance was established as one of the three cross-cutting workstreams alongside Change Management (owned by [[Olufemi Davies]]) and Hiring Roadmap (owned by [[Damilola Oyediran]]).
