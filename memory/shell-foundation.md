## Role

**Shell Foundation** provides the credit guarantee for [[Moniepoint]]'s SME [[Asset Financing Strategy]] — solar power and smartphone financing.

## Partnership Role (April 2026)

At the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]] (Day 1), Shell Foundation is named as the credit-guarantee counterparty for the initiative. Shell Foundation has also recommended potential distribution partners, although no single recommended partner currently has the 300–2,000 units/day distribution capacity required for scale.
