## Summary

Draft 2026 OKRs for [[TeamApt]] Product Division led by [[Frank Atashili]]. Seven division-level objectives: (1) Scale DD/GoSubscribe (mandate activation 95%, 50 merchants, 100 billers), (2) Improve card acquiring on Monnify (95% success, 30% activation), (3) International acquiring (Visa L3 certs, 10K transactions), (4) Issuer processing (CMS live, EMV Data Prep), (5) Expand domestic switching (22 issuers, 8 partner banks on Account Switch), (6) Grow ACT (4 external clients, TACHA clearing), (7) Reduce MoniePoint POS cost (50% via Juliana).

## Key Points

- 7 division-level objectives with measurable KRs
- DD/GoSubscribe: 95% mandate activation, 50 merchants, 100 billers
- Card acquiring: 95% success rate, 30% merchant activation on Monnify
- International: Visa L3 certifications (3 terminal models), 10K transactions
- Issuer processing: CMS live, EMV Data Prep
- Domestic switching: 22 issuers, 8 partner banks on Account Switch
- ACT: 4 external clients, TACHA clearing
- POS cost: 50% reduction via Juliana Switch
- Department-level OKRs for PMO, Direct Debit, Domestic Switching, TPP, Monnify, Design
