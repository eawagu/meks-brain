## Overview

Cost Champion is a Moniepoint company-wide principle introduced at the 2026 UK Leadership Retreat Day 3 financial session: every employee is a "cost champion" for their own expenses, not just management.

## Practice

Ask "Do I need it?" for each expense (e.g., travel) before seeking approval. Responsibility for cost control is not exclusive to management or finance.

## Strategic Rationale

Lifting PAT margin from 8% to mid-30s requires granular cost-consciousness across the organization, not just top-down programs. Omondi's profitability plan depends on every department owning its cost areas.
