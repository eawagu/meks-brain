## Overview

3D Liveness Verification is a biometric security control in Moniepoint's onboarding flow. It uses Facetech technology to verify that the person onboarding is physically present and matches the submitted identity documents, defeating photo/video spoofing and deepfake attacks.

## Role in Onboarding

Applied during new account creation to prevent account takeover and synthetic identity fraud. Credited as a significant contributor to Moniepoint's 2024 reduction in third-party mail account incidents.

## Outcomes

- Zero third-party mail account incidents from late 2024 through present
- Contribution to 88.2% sign-up conversion (security without excessive friction)
