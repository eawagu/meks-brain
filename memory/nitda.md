## Overview
[[NITDA]] (National Information Technology Development Agency) is a Nigerian government agency directed to host the technical coordination secretariat for the proposed Cybersecurity Coordination Council.

## Regulatory Context — TeamApt IMS
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], NITDA is named among the Nigerian regulatory bodies (alongside [[CBN]], [[NDPC]], AML/CFT requirements) whose compliance culture TeamApt already has in place — framed as a strength that eases [[ISO 45001]] integration.
