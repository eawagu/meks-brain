## Overview

Verve is a Nigerian domestic card scheme operated under the [[InterSwitch]] umbrella. In Q4 2025 Verve switched from flat pricing via a regulatory loophole (acquirers are capped and must accept all schemes; schemes are not price-regulated), creating rent-seeking leverage against acquirers like [[Moniepoint]].

## Moniepoint MFB Footprint (per 2026-04-21 KT)

- **Primary switch for on-us and remote transactions** in the MFB card stack
- **~99% of MFB card transactions route through Verve**
- BIN ranges in MFB CMS: 507880, 507800002, 50612402074 (per [[Card Manager Service CMS Specification]])
- Future [[Dispute Management Service]] roadmap includes a **Verve Online** dispute rail

## Impact (Q4 2025 Pricing Dispute)

- Material driver of Moniepoint's Q4 2025 PAT reversal (scheme pricing shock).
- Framed by Tosin as the exemplar of the asymmetric regulation of acquirers vs. schemes.

## Action

- [[Dennis Ajalie]] to negotiate Verve pricing down in exchange for committed volumes.
