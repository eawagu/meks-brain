## Overview

Nigerian software engineer; Executive Director of [[Optisoft Technology Co. Ltd]]. Sole witness for the Defence in [[Elfrique Solutions v Optisoft Technology — Judgment (31 Mar 2026)]].

## Role

- Software Engineer / Executive Director, Optisoft Technology Co. Ltd.

## Litigation — Elfrique v Optisoft

- Testified at trial 22 Jan 2025. Under cross-examination, stated 20 years' experience in web development. Admitted control-panel access was requested only after the ₦300,000 mobilization fee was received (not at pre-engagement scoping), and that platform inspection occurred post-engagement rather than before quoting.
- Court commented on this failing in its ratio: quoting without inspecting the system was a core professional lapse.
