## Summary
Ahsan Naseem is a Head of Engineering at [[Bazaar Technologies]] with 5 years tenure and 9+ years of backend experience in Java/Spring Boot and Kotlin. He is being assessed for a Director or Sr. Director-level Head of Engineering role at [[Moniepoint]]. His core strength is transitioning complex B2B/Fintech ecosystems into AI-driven B2C operations.

## Key Points
- Current title: Head of Engineering / Tech Lead at Bazaar Technologies (5 years)
- Assessed level: Director or Sr. Director (not VP-level)
- Managed 75–80 engineers and 6–7 EMs; reduced to 30–40 after company re-org
- Scaled Easycarta (digital ledger/inventory app) to 3–4M installs
- Built AI-based customer service achieving near-total automation
- Architected shift from B2B inventory to fintech lending/credit platform for small merchants
- Managed delivery across 4 verticals: B2B Retail, Fintech/Lending, Industrial, 3PL Distribution
- Tech stack: Java/Spring Boot, Kotlin, Go (personal favorite), Cloud Infrastructure
- Motivation to leave: has "conquered" Bazaar org after 5 years; seeking high-stakes challenges
