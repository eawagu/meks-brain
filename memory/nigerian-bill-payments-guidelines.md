## Summary

Regulatory framework for electronic bill presentment and payment, covering billers, PSPs, settlement, and direct debit schemes. Bill payments routed through NIP, NEFT, POS, web, USSD, and mobile app channels.

## Key Points

- Direct Debit regulated under CBN Regulation for Direct Debit (2018)
- Government electronic payments mandatory since 2019; all receipts flow through TSA
- NIBSS Quickteller (Interswitch) aggregates 8,000+ billers
- VAS (Value Added Services) for airtime, data, utilities — distinct from telecom VAS
- Bill payment disputes resolved within T+3
