## Overview

Vicas was the previous [[ACS]] (Access Control Server) provider for [[Moniepoint MFB]] — the 3D Secure authentication service for online card transactions.

## Status

- **Discontinued due to cost**
- Replacement provider discussions are in progress as part of the Visa Tokenization & VAS roadmap (likely Q4 2026)
