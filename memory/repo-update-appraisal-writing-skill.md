## Summary
This archive contains a complete appraisal writing skill for the [[Moniepoint]] Group ([[TeamApt]]), designed to standardize performance review processes across the organization. It includes step-by-step guidance for gathering inputs (OKRs, actuals, self-reviews), calculating attainment percentages, applying calibration rules, and writing evidence-based ratings on a 1.0–6.0 scale. The skill references current 2026 OKRs, business context, and organizational structure, with practical examples of completed appraisals from Q3–Q4 2025.

## Key Points
- Rating scale: 1.0–6.0. Default 2.0 when OKRs not met or missing; 3.0 = meets expectations (baseline); 4.0+ requires ≥90% attainment on controllable items plus above-and-beyond contribution
- Core methodology: OKR attainment drives rating; appraisals require mandatory inputs (team member name/role, agreed OKRs, actuals, self-review, manager context) before writing
- Four-section structure: Performance Rating, Rating Comment (SBI-structured evidence), Growth & Development Areas, Expectations for Coming Quarter
- Calibration rules: <90% attainment on controlled items = default 2.0; gaps attributed to pillar teams fact-checked against CSAT scores (SRE 92.3%, Engineering 84.6%, Infra 76.9%, Ops 69.2%)
- Skill triggers: Appraisals, performance reviews, ratings, Dream Maker reviews, MOOS reviews, quarterly reviews, or uploads of OKR/self-review data
- 2026 strategic context: [[Direct Debit]] positioned as new payment frontier; [[GoSubscribe]] launch; POS volume processing; ATS web enablement; Liquidity Manager; settlement infrastructure
