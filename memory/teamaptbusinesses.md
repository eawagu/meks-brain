## Summary

Quick-reference index for [[TeamApt]]'s four core business lines organized into two tiers: Revenue Drivers (Direct Debit/CDD led by Daniel Ojinaka, [[Monnify]] led by Damilare Ogunnaike) and Enablers (TPP led by Tracy Ojaigho, Domestic Switching led by Babatunde Okufi). Maps business lines to key systems, customer types, and CBN licenses.

## Key Points

- **Direct Debit (CDD):** Daniel Ojinaka; Products: MADD, GoSubscribe, SAFE; Systems: ACS, GoSubscribe, SAFE, TACHA; Customers: Issuing banks, Payment Facilitators/billers/merchants
- **Monnify:** Damilare Ogunnaike; Products: collections (VA, web card, DD), disbursements, VAS, international (MPGS, Cybersource); Systems: Monnify Gateway, Loom, Limit Engine, MPGS; Customers: Merchants, Payment Facilitators, VA provider banks
- **TPP:** Tracy Ojaigho; Products: acquirer processing, issuer processing (CMS, EMV Data Prep), ACT, MPGS dashboard, Non-Bank Acquiring; Systems: Aptent, ACT, EMV Data Prep, Rsync CMS; Customers: Acquiring/issuing banks, card schemes, personalization bureaus
- **Domestic Switching:** Babatunde Okufi; Products: Juliana Card/Account Switch, ATS, PTSP/PTAD, TACHA; Systems: Juliana, ATS, TACHA, NSS; Customers: Banks, switch partners, PTSA, settlement banks, PTSP clients
- CBN licenses: Switching & Processing (foundation), Non-Bank Acquiring (TPP + Monnify), PTSP (Domestic Switching), SuperAgent (Domestic Switching)
