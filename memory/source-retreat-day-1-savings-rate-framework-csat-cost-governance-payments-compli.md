## Summary

Retreat Day 1 operational review: Savings team presentation (penetration 9.9% vs 9% target, 20% stretch), rate framework debate anchoring to MPR minus spread, Cards 2025 recap (7.97T txns, 13.3M cards, market share 5.9%→11.7%, #7→#3), Cards 2026 plan, Acquiring Q1 revenue ahead of target, CSAT & cost elevated to equal-prominence metrics with revenue.

## Key Points

- **Presentation standards:** Tosin — MECE information hierarchy; articulate, cohesive, tied to strategy; OKR/goals-keyresults-initiatives format required for all future decks. Suggest use of AI tools to improve outputs.
- **Savings strategic shift:** Product-push → customer life-scenarios (habit formation). Portfolio: SOYT/SAYT, Target, Flexible, Fixed. Target Savings is the breakout per customer research.
- **Savings performance:** Penetration 9.9% vs 9% target; internal goal raised to 20% by year end. AUM 3B shortfall at recording (1.2B vs 1.6B projection, since passed 1.6B). End-of-month 130B goal not on course (sales launched late, sales prioritized loans). Month-by-month: Jan 67→Feb 82→Mar 106→Apr 135→May 167.
- **Rate framework key decision:** Anchor funding cost cap to MPR minus spread smaller than 6 (e.g., MPR-5 to MPR-10). Treasury buy rate = 15%; current reverse interest ~15; rates in "space between 17 and 20." Stakeholders meet next week to restructure rate + revenue approach.
- **Cost of funds:** Deposits ~4% of total deposits in money funds; blended annualized cost ~8% (breaks + mix influenced). Cohort tests on raised rates to measure AUM uplift vs cost-of-funds impact.
- **NPR-linked operating metric:** Proposed to replace/augment FTP constraints. Discussion next week.
- **SAYT "where is my money" issue:** Customer communication gap persists despite fix — improve customer comms. Legacy BO accounts decommissioned for new customers; gradual migration for existing.
- **SLA + complaints:** Refine SLA measurement to exclude new/unknown issues from known-issue benchmarks. Normalize complaint rate by active customer base (7-day, 30-day trends).
- **Savings roadmap:** CRM rollout by end of April; soft onboarding + personalized recommendations; shift performance tracking to revenue (in addition to AUM/penetration); monthly customer reports starting May (rankings, trends, market comparisons; compliance on naming).
- **Product revamps:** Flexible revamp by May; referral campaigns on goal-based use cases (rent, school fees).
- **New offline savings product:** Collector-contributor model (not round-robin). Launch goal end of Q2. TA to complete PM/designer/engineer recruitment by end Q2.
- **"Studio" personalization platform:** Q3-year-end horizon. Cross-committee penetration into professional business segments.
- **Cards 2025 recap:** 7.97 trillion transactions, ~13.3M cards sold, market share 5.9%→11.7%, position #7→#3. CSAT deprioritized = key 2026 gap.
- **Cards 2026 plan:** 23M cards, ~1.3B transactions, add tap-to-pay, virtual cards, business cards; aim #2 market share. Virtual cards launched; tap-to-pay pending scheme tokenization; exploring Transsion for NFC device penetration.
- **CBN ATM directive:** 1 ATM per 7,500 cards — operational complexity + cost.
- **Acquiring 2025:** 11B revenue (24% growth), ~51% gross margin. 2026 OKRs: 247B revenue (17% growth), 49% gross margin, international revenue ~$45k.
- **Acquiring customer-experienced success rate:** Focus on end-to-end latency goal 20s (P95); HTTP-based multi-attempt verification with 30s user-facing countdown (testing). Contactless read target <500ms.
- **Disputes:** Down to <10%, goal <5%. North-star automatic refunds without customer action. ~94% disputes within control; push-to-card for proactive refunds.
- **Compliance:** SLA for regulatory reporting <7 days. Geofencing: configurable, rapidly activatable control on POS (GPS inaccuracy rules out strict radii). POS app certified with geofencing SDK. Visa/MasterCard partial; pursuing Amex.
- **Q2 acquiring:** Targeted onboarding via MoneyCRM + growth campaigns, HTTP query improvements, contactless speed, proactive settlement-based refunds.
- **CSAT + cost elevation:** Equal prominence with revenue in org reviews. Monthly MPRs with COGS, promotions, commissions, OPEX views starting next month.
