## Overview

Moniepoint's Nigeria revenue outlook as framed at the 2026 Leadership Retreat Day 1.

## Figures

- **$680M** Nigeria revenue this year.
- **6th** in banking revenue among profitable Nigerian banks.
- **~$1.5B** theoretical benchmark revenue.
- **~$1B gap** vs. actual — attributed to lower LTVs in underserved segments.

## Interpretation

- Lower LTVs are a deliberate consequence of serving underserved segments — leadership prioritizes impact over revenue maximization in that tier.
- Deposits are the **only line above budget** — sticky primary accounts acquired via POS.
- Upside levers: [[Key Account Management Strategy]] (Pareto concentration), lending expansion, [[VAS]] UX + rewards.
