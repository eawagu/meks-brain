## Overview

Card Integration Service is the integration plane that connects the [[Moniepoint MFB]] card system with other internal platforms. It sits under the Card Infrastructure team owned by [[Damilola Oyediran]].

## Key Integration

- **[[Money Desk]]** — the MoniePoint helpdesk / customer support tool. Card Integration Service allows support agents to log and resolve card-related issues against a customer record.
