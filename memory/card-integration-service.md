## Overview

**Card Integration Service** sits under [[Moniepoint MFB]]'s Card Infrastructure stack and handles integrations between the card system and internal platforms — notably [[Money Desk]] (the MoniePoint helpdesk/customer support tool). This allows support agents to log and resolve card-related issues against a customer record.

## Scope

- Integration bridge between Cards and internal platforms
- Known integration: [[Money Desk]] (customer support)
