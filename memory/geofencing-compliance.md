## Overview

Geofencing Compliance is Moniepoint's capability-ready POS geofencing feature — certified with [[NIBSS]] ahead of schedule and deployable via POS configuration change if CBN mandates enforcement.

## Practical Limitations

- Current geolocation accuracy ~±20m — tight radius (e.g., 10m) is impractical.
- Best-case use: broad-radius territorial constraint (e.g., country-level), not merchant-address-level.

## Risk Context

- POS terminals operating in international locations (China, Russia, UK) flagged as exposure concern.
- CBN regulation not yet enforced; Moniepoint preparing capability in advance to avoid emergency deployment.

## Certification

- NIBSS POS application + Geo-Fencing SDK certified ahead of schedule (Q1 2026).
