## Overview

MoniePoint MFB (Microfinance Bank) is the **Nigeria microfinance bank operating company (OpCo)** within the Moniepoint group. Under [[Project Phoenix]] it consumes central platforms as a tenant — including [[CI&P]], [[TSPP]], [[Payment Gateway Platform]], and the broader [[Project Phoenix#Platform Cluster Architecture (Official — Tosin's Project_Phoenix_v3)|platform cluster architecture]].

## Role in Phoenix Phase 1 (per Apr 22, 2026 Org Movements brief)

- **Supplies Cards engineers** into CI&P strike teams (both Team 1 Card Processing and Team 2 Card Issuance pull Cards (MFB) engineers).
- Supplies supplements to the [[TSPP]] strike team as well.
- The **existing MFB Cards Team** remains operational during Phase 1 (continuity); a strike team builds the new [[Card Management System]].

## Cards Domain Co-Leadership

- [[Tracy Ojaigho]] — Head, CI&P Product (central platform)
- [[Olufemi Davies]] — leads MFB Cards Business (commercial + operational outcomes)

Co-lead split: Tracy owns Product (central); Femi owns Business (MFB). Not a succession — split is by function.

## MFB Parallel Systems (context)

MoniePoint MFB operates parallel card infrastructure that Phoenix platforms absorb: [[Postillion]]/PostCard, Smart Card Process, Safe Token, [[Card Manager Service|CMS Manager]] (~12-person team under Femi Davies), Aptent (authorization routing). Beyond cards: [[Iris]] (group reconciliation, 15–27B+ txn/month), [[Atlas]] (transfer orchestration, ~500M txn/month, 12+ downstream providers).
