Cybersecurity is the industry discipline of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and data breaches. Encompasses vulnerability management, threat hunting, compliance audits (PCIDSS, ISO 27001, SOC 2), centralized access control (IAM, SSO, PAM), security awareness training, incident response, and security governance.

## Core Domains

- **Vulnerability management** — scanning, triage, remediation, tracking
- **Compliance audits** — PCIDSS, ISO 27001, SOC 2, regulatory frameworks
- **Threat hunting** — proactive detection of adversary activity
- **Access control** — IAM, SSO, privileged access management
- **Incident response** — detection, containment, recovery, post-mortem
- **Security governance** — policy, standards, risk management
