## Overview

Digital Banking Platforms is the [[Moniepoint]] organizational unit that houses the [[Card Issuance & Processing Platform]] as part of [[Project Phoenix]]. It is one of three operating divisions in the central [[Platform Organization]] (alongside Business Banking Platforms and Credit Platforms).

## Cluster Leadership

- **Head of Digital Banking Platforms:** TBD

## Platforms Housed

- [[Card Issuance & Processing Platform]] (CI&P)
- Customer Platforms (Mobile App, Web App, POS App, CRM/Product Instrumentation, Design System)
- Banking Operations Platforms (Spend Mgmt, VAS, Savings, Identity/Onboarding/Shell, Rewards)

### Operating Model \u2014 Strike Team + Continuity

Operating model communicated by [[Emeka Awagu]] to [[Tracy Ojaigho]] on 2026-04-14:
- **Continuity track:** existing MFB engineers continue maintaining the current cards system.
- **Build track:** a strike team builds out the new [[Card Issuance & Processing Platform]].
- Model explicitly mirrors the pattern [[Frank Atashili]] and [[Alex Adeyemo]] are running on their platforms.
- Commitment: audit everything the existing cards team has already built and incorporate it into the CI&P roadmap \u2014 existing work is not discarded.
