## Overview

Digital Banking Platforms is the [[Moniepoint]] organizational unit that houses the [[Card Issuance & Processing Platform]] as part of [[Project Phoenix]].

## Reporting Structure

- **Head of Digital Banking Platforms** ([[Tracy Ojaigho]]) — owns overall platform direction; accountable for delivery across both teams; represents platform at leadership level; sets strategic roadmap aligned with business goals
  - **Head of CI&P Product** (TBD) — owns product vision, prioritisation, stakeholder communication across the platform; translates business/market requirements into roadmap; manages relationships with internal clients; reports to Head of Digital Banking Platforms
  - **Head of CI&P Engineering** ([[Emeka Awagu]]) — owns technical health: architecture decisions, engineering standards, code quality, cross-team technical dependencies; final authority on how the platform is built; reports to Head of Digital Banking Platforms
    - [[Team 1 Card Processing]] — triad (Product Lead, EM, Designer) + mid-to-senior developers
    - [[Team 2 Card Issuance]] — triad (Product Lead, EM, Designer) + mid-to-senior developers

### Operating Model — Strike Team + Continuity

Operating model communicated by [[Emeka Awagu]] to [[Tracy Ojaigho]] on 2026-04-14:
- **Continuity track:** existing MFB engineers continue maintaining the current cards system.
- **Build track:** a strike team builds out the new [[Card Issuance & Processing Platform]].
- Model explicitly mirrors the pattern [[Frank Atashili]] and [[Alex Adeyemo]] are running on their platforms.
- Commitment: audit everything the existing cards team has already built and incorporate it into the CI&P roadmap — existing work is not discarded.
