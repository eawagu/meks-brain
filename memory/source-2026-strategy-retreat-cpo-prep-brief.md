## Product Division Structure
Frank leads the Product Division with 7 division-level OKRs across 4 core businesses:

**Revenue Drivers:**
- [[Direct Debit]] (CDD) — [[Daniel Ojinaka]]: Scale DD as primary payment rail; launch GoSubscribe commercially
- [[Monnify]] — [[Damilare Ogunnaike]]: Grow merchant base; strengthen fraud controls; unlock international revenue

**Enablers:**
- [[Third Party Processing]] (TPP) — [[Tracy Ojaigho]]: Revenue from acquirer processing & ACT; build issuer processing (CMS, EMV)
- [[Domestic Switching]] — [[Tunde Okufi]]: Expand switch network; reduce MoniePoint processing costs; grow ACT

**Shared:** PMO ([[Idris Aliyu]]), Product Design ([[Kelvin Ogumor]])

## OKR Targets (Selected)
- DD: 50 active merchants/billers, 5 new PayFacs, >10% MoM revenue growth
- Monnify: 40,000 new merchants, CSAT 75%, NPS 30, card success rate 95%, $1M+ international revenue
- TPP: N1.09B FY revenue, 3 Visa L3 certifications, Mastercard acquiring license
- Switching: 22 issuers (from 16), 8 Account Switch partners (from 3), 50% POS via Juliana
- PTSP: N150M monthly POS activations, N1.6B monthly fees

## Critical Strategic Topics
1. **[[Project Phoenix]] / TSP Build** — full composable FinOps/TSP platform, 6-month One Platform timeline, 5 proposed teams, 20+ engineers needed, architecture boundary decision (cloud vs on-prem) is critical path
2. **[[GoSubscribe]]** — Strategic Focus Area #1: agent-led distribution (pilot 70 BRMs/3,500 agents → scale 500 BRMs/25,000 agents), POS + Web channels
3. **International acquiring** — Strategic Focus Area #2: primary growth driver for 2027, Visa L3 certs, MPGS + Cybersource, 10,000 international card transactions target
4. **[[Monnify]] market position recovery** — Strategic Focus Area #3: competitive dynamics vs Paystack/Flutterwave, merchant satisfaction, TOMA strategy
