## Summary

Concise headcount and organizational structure document for all engineering staff in Monnify Payment Gateway business. 26 total engineers across 2 Engineering Managers (1 resigned), distributed across Senior Backend, Backend, Frontend, Mobile, and QA roles. Dual reporting: functional to Ravi Kiran Veluguleti (VP Enterprise Engineering), business to Damilare Ogunnaike (Head Monnify).

## Key Points

- 26 total Monnify engineers
- 2 EMs: Prateek Gupta (15 engineers, active) + Vaibhav Bansal (9 engineers, resigned/notice period)
- Geographic spread: Nigeria (15), India (3), Pakistan (3), Kenya (1), UK (2)
- Dual reporting: Functional to Ravi Kiran Veluguleti (VP Enterprise Engineering); business to Damilare Ogunnaike (Monnify Head)
- Organizational risk: 9 engineers under Vaibhav pending reassignment creates temporary instability
