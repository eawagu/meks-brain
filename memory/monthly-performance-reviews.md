## Overview

Monthly Performance Reviews (MPRs) are the monthly governance cadence instituted by [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] at the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]] Day 1. Every business unit participates starting next month.

## Rationale

- CSAT and cost management are the two areas the organization is not handling effectively.
- Single-digit PAT despite strong revenue — cost control across COGS (promos, commissions) and OPEX required.
- Tosin takes personal responsibility — "this focus must start from the top."

## Scope per MPR

- Revenue progress.
- Detailed cost views: COGS, promotions, commissions.
- OPEX.
- Customer-facing metrics (CSAT, success rate, complaints).
- OKR progress.
