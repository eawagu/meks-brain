## Overview
A [[Non-Conformity]] (NC) is a failure to fulfil a specified requirement in an ISO-certified management system. Categories:
- **Major Non-Conformity (MaNC):** systemic failure or absence of a required process
- **Minor Non-Conformity (MiNC):** isolated or partial failure where the process exists
- **Observation (OBS):** finding that could lead to an NC if not addressed
- **Opportunity for Improvement (OFI):** finding where process is compliant but could be enhanced
