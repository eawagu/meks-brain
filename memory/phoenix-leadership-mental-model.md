## Summary

Detailed operational model for leadership at Moniepoint. Defines seven primitives and eight judgment dimensions for assessing and developing leaders recursively.

## Key Points

- Seven primitives: People, Structure, Context, Systems, Goals, Incentives, Governance
- People are decision-makers; everything else is leverage
- Structure removes ambiguity about ownership and accountability
- Context as institutional memory is non-negotiable
- Systems convert recurring decisions into reusable mechanisms
- Three non-negotiable threshold dimensions: No Ego, Candor, Integrity
