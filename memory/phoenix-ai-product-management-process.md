## Summary

New PM methodology integrating Claude as thinking partner. PMs remain accountable; AI assists with research, analysis, and documentation.

## Key Points

- AI helps synthesize research, stress-test problem definitions, draft PRDs
- PM owns all final product decisions
- PRD structure: problem, success metrics, user stories, acceptance criteria, technical considerations, dependencies, risks
- Quality gates required before engineering begins
