## Stub

Checkmarx is a static-application-security-testing (SAST) tool. Under consideration for procurement by the Moniepoint group security team.

## 2026-04-22 weekly meeting — procurement context

[[Yasir Syed Ali]] raised concerns that the internal security team is slowing things down and not equipped with the right tools — banks ([[Access Bank]] raised 200 vulnerabilities; [[Zenith Bank]] raising different ones) consistently discover vulnerabilities the internal team misses. [[Wycliffe Ochieng']] agreed: tooling differences. [[Ravi Veluguleti]] noted **the group is planning to get Checkmarx, which is a better tool**.

Source: [[TeamApt Weekly Team Meeting - 2026-04-22 16:59 IST]].

## Why Checkmarx

The motivation surfaced is the gap between internal scans and bank-side vulnerability discovery: banks raise post-internal-scan vulnerabilities that suggest the internal SAST tooling has lower coverage than what banks are using. Checkmarx is being eyed as the closing-tool for that gap.
