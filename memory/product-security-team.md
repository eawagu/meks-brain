## Overview

The Product Security Team is the internal Moniepoint function responsible for security architecture, vulnerability assessment, fraud prevention, and security controls across Moniepoint products. Reviewed during the 2026 UK Leadership Retreat Day 2 session covering Platform Performance, Security, and Customer Experience.

## Responsibilities

- 3D liveness verification integration (Facetech) for onboarding
- Risk-based challenge system for fraud prevention
- Product security posture for onboarding/channels and account payments
- Coordination with customer experience on fraud-related complaints

## 2024 Impact

Product security and onboarding initiatives contributed to elimination of third-party mail account incidents in late 2024 and fraud reduction posture going into 2025/2026.
