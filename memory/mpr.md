## Overview

MPR (Monetary Policy Rate) is the Central Bank of Nigeria-set policy rate that serves as the benchmark for Nigerian bank pricing.

## Role in Moniepoint FTP

- FTP benefit to deposit-gathering houses: 30% of MPR
- FTP charge to asset-creating houses: MPR − 6

MPR serves as the anchor making FTP a market-sensitive internal rate rather than a flat number.
