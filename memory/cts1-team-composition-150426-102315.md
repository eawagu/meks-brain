## Summary

Team Composition reference for the [[Card Sales & Distribution Team]] (CTS1) — a 13-member Moniepoint team owning the platform that enables card distribution across the partner network, card sales experience for personal and business users, and card lifecycle management (activation, delete, reset PIN, block, unblock). Relevant to [[Project Phoenix]] / [[Card Issuance & Processing Platform]] takeover scoping (companion to [[Note — Existing Cards Team Moniepoint MFB Transition 2026-04-14]]).

## Key Points

- Team total: 13 members.
- Composition: 1 Product Manager, 1 Engineering Manager, 3 Backend Engineers, 1 Frontend Engineer, 1 Mobile Engineer, 4 Site Reliability Engineers, 1 Quality Assurance Engineer, 1 UX Designer.
- Mission: manage/evolve the platform enabling card distribution across partner network; provide seamless card sales experience for personal and business users; run card activation and lifecycle operations (activate, delete, reset PIN, block, unblock).
- Heavy SRE-to-Engineering ratio (4 SRE : 5 Product Engineers) — unusual for a product team; suggests significant operational load on partner-network / distribution infrastructure.
- One QA engineer against 5 product engineers — standard lean ratio.
