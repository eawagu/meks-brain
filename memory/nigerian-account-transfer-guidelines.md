## Summary

Comprehensive regulatory reference for account-to-account transfers in Nigeria, covering NIP and NEFT platforms, participant obligations, settlement procedures, and ISO 20022 migration mandate. NIP is the dominant payment rail: 5.6B transactions, ₦476.9T value in H1 2024.

## Key Points

- NIP: real-time delivery within 60 seconds; Deferred Net Settlement up to 12x daily
- NEFT declining in volume (44.8M txns) but growing in value (₦366T) — used for bulk/batch
- ISO 20022 migration mandatory by October 2025
- Third-party account switches (e.g., TeamApt's Juliana) permitted; must comply with NIBSS interconnection
- Failed transactions: ₦10,000 penalty per item if not reversed within 24 hours; 3-working-day dispute SLA
