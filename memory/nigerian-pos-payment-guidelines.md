## Summary

Extensive regulatory framework for POS terminal operations covering licensing, PTSA connectivity, ISO 20022 migration, geo-tagging, interchange fees, and switching company obligations. Market scale: 5.5M terminals by end 2024; 6.4B transactions H1 2024 (₦85.9T).

## Key Points

- Only PTSPs (licensed entities) may deploy POS terminals nationwide
- September 2024: all transactions must route through licensed PTSAs (NIBSS or UPSL)
- December 2025: mandatory dual PTSA connectivity with automatic failover
- ISO 20022 migration mandatory by October 31, 2025
- POS geo-tagging mandatory: dual-frequency GPS, Android 10+, 10-metre geofence
- Switching companies: ₦2B capital + ₦2B escrow; 24/7 operations; half-yearly DR testing
- 16+ licensed switching companies (Interswitch, eTranzact, TeamApt, Flutterwave, Paystack, etc.)
