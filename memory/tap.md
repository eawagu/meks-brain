## Stub

Tap appears in the [[Direct to Bank program]] context as one of two Moniepoint accounts (alongside [[Money Point]]) submitted to partner banks for **whitelisting** as part of fund-settlement integration. Per [[Abdulgafar Obeitor]] at the 2026-04-21 D2B standup: signatures for both Tap and Money Point accounts are the same, so the team's strategy is to push both accounts forward together; bank-specific issues are handled case-by-case.

## Naming context

Referred to as the "switch" account in some discussions — likely the Moniepoint switch's settlement entity (TMSS or related). Disambiguation pending a clearer source.
