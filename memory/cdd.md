## Definition

CDD (Consolidated Direct Debit) is [[TeamApt]]'s mandate-based bank debit department, one of the two Revenue Driver departments pre-Phoenix. See [[Direct Debit]] for the payment-rail architecture and technical specifics.

## Products

- MADD — core Direct Debit product
- [[GoSubscribe]] — subscription (POS + Web)
- SAFE — framework product

## Leadership

- Pre-Phoenix: [[Daniel Ojinaka]] — department lead
- Under Phoenix: Daniel becomes **dual business lead (Monnify + Direct Debit)** at TeamApt OpCo.

## Under Project Phoenix (Apr 22, 2026 brief)

- **Engineering moves:** CDD engineering (full team, 6 engineers) moves to [[TSPP]] in later phases.
- **Commercial stays:** Daniel retains commercial ownership for CDD in OpCo alongside Monnify commercial.
- **2026 OKRs continue:** >10% MoM revenue growth, 50 merchants, 5 new PayFacs; plus Monnify growth / retention targets.
- **PM:** [[Abiodun Famoye]] (APM, CDD) stays with Monnify + CDD for now; moves to TSPP when CDD is absorbed.

## 2025 Performance

- DD revenue exceeded 200% of KR — driven by [[Access Bank]]
- Key lesson: Integration ≠ Activation (20 billers onboarded, 0% actively transacting)
