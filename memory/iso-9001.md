## Overview
[[ISO 9001]] is the international standard for Quality Management Systems (QMS).

## TeamApt Implementation
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], ISO 9001 is one of five standards within the [[Integrated Management System]] at [[TeamApt]]. Documented objectives:
- Achieve 85% customer satisfaction by Q1 2026
- Reduce product/service defects by 20% by Q1 2026
- Achieve 95% on-time delivery rate by June 2026
- Comply with relevant industry standards and regulations
- Ensure employees are aware of their quality responsibilities

## Implementation Timeline

- **2025-10-14 — Teamapt × OPEX ISO 9001 implementation working session**: early joint working session with [[OPEX]]. Stub Gemini-export only. Source: [[Teamapt X OPEX (ISO 9001_ Implementation Session) - 2025_10_14 09_55 WAT - Notes by Gemini]].
- **2026-04-30 — ISO audit close-out**: audit team recommended continued maintenance of ISO 9001 (along with three other standards) and certification of [[OHSMS]] against [[ISO 45001:2018]]. No major or minor non-conformities recorded for ISO 9001 in this cycle (significant change from the prior Apr internal audit MaNC at Clause 8.4.1). 5 observations and 4 OFIs across the IMS. Source: [[ISO Audit-Close out session – 2026_04_30 16_27 WAT – Notes by Gemini]].

## Major Non-Conformity (Apr 2026 Internal Audit)
**QMS Clause 8.4.1** — no evidence of external provider evaluations, assessments, and actions taken to ensure QMS compliance. This was the single non-conformity across the entire IMS internal audit (other findings are observations and OFIs). Apr 30 close-out indicates remediation completed (audit confirmed no major/minor NCs).

## Key Risks in Scope (Apr 2026)
- Regulatory fines for late returns (Moderate) — controls: training, close monitoring
- Financial/reputational risk from consumer complaints (Moderate) — controls: fraud desk training
- Incomplete employee documentation (Moderate) — controls: HRBP review
- Prolonged deployment downtime (Moderate) — controls: independent pre-deployment review
- Key stakeholder attrition during onboarding (Moderate) — controls: contract review
- Late identification of applicable laws (Moderate) — controls: subscription to formal regulatory channels
