## Overview

The TeamApt Share of Moniepoint Transactions metric tracks what percentage of all transactions on the Moniepoint platform are processed by [[TeamApt Limited]] vs other processors. Tracked weekly in the [[Moniepoint Group CEO Gazette]].

## Trend — four-week decline (per [[CEO Gazette - 24th April 26]])

| Week | Share |
|---|---|
| Wk1 | 35.68% |
| Wk4 (this week) | 32.54% |

Cumulative loss: -3.1pp in 4 weeks. Total platform volumes broadly flat → TeamApt is **losing share to other processors on the same platform**, not being dragged down by market contraction.

## Volatility signal

- **Apr 17, 2026:** lowest share in four weeks at 26.1% — mirrors the [[NIBSS Routing Anomaly]] same day.
- **Apr 22, 2026:** spiked to 41.6% — highest share in four weeks, on the same total volume as Apr 17.
- **Within-week swing of 15.5pp on flat total volume** — extreme day-to-day volatility, impossible to manage to a stable target. Identifying drivers of these swings is as important as addressing the overall downward trend.
