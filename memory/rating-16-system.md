## Overview

Structured peer rating system at Moniepoint designed to improve the BRM-Business Owner relationship — and propagate accountability upward through the sales hierarchy.

## Rules

- **80%** of business owners under a BRM must rate the BRM every **60 days**.
- Minimum acceptable rating: **4.5**.
- Consequence: BRM scoring below 4.5 OR with <80% rating participation → placed on P&D until target is met.

## Hierarchical Cascade

- BRMs rate Cluster Managers.
- Cluster Managers rate State Coordinators.
- State Coordinators rate Regional Managers.

## Purpose

- Make BRM-BO relationship a spontaneously-mentioned driver of customer satisfaction (vs current 3% baseline).
- Bind compensation consequences to lived customer experience, not only quantitative KPIs.
