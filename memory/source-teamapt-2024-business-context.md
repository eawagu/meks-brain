[[TeamApt]] 2024 business context. CEO: [[Dennis Ajalie]]. Subsidiary of [[Moniepoint|Moniepoint Inc.]] (Group CEO: [[Tosin Eniolorunda]]). B2B payments infrastructure — switching, processing, gateway, direct debit.

## Key Points

### Revenue & Financial Context
- Quarterly revenue baseline: $510K → target $1M (at ₦1,500/USD = ~₦6B annualized)
- Revenue primarily driven by [[DCIR]] and existing bank contracts
- 2024 was a foundational year — most new products in build/early deployment

### Product Portfolio (2024)
- **[[DCIR]]** (Direct Card Issuer Routing): 7 banks live at start; target 12. Front-end card processing in-bank (TMS + Aptent)
- **[[Direct Debit]]**: New product line, 0 banks → target 7. Mandate-based collections
- **[[Consolidated Direct Debit]] (CDD)**: Multi-account aggregation layer, target 3 merchant aggregators
- **Consolidated Switch** (later [[Juliana]]): Centralized domestic card switch, target 5 issuer + 5 acquirer integrations
- **[[TPP]]**: Scheme processing — Afrigo/Verve certifications complete, Visa/MC targeted H2

### Performance Results
- **DCIR bank expansion failed**: 0 new banks in Q1 against 3-bank target
- **Direct Debit slow**: 0 banks Q1, 1 bank by Q2 against 7-bank target
- **Consolidated Switch best performer**: 10 issuers integrated (exceeding 7 target), 2 domestic processors (ISW, UP)
- **TPP certifications on track**: Afrigo, Verve, NIBSS acquirer processing all completed by Q2
- **Reliability improved**: Transaction success rate 70% → 96.3%; incident response 240 mins → 60 mins
- **Sprint velocity systemic issue**: DCIR 72.3%, DD 50.5%, Switch 75% (all below targets)

### Key Banks (2024)
Live: [[Access Bank]], [[Wema Bank]]/ALAT, [[FCMB]], [[Fidelity Bank]], [[UBA]], [[Keystone Bank]], [[Union Bank]], [[Polaris Bank]]
Targeted: [[Zenith Bank]], [[Ecobank]], [[Stanbic IBTC]], Parallax

### Strategic Initiatives
1. Become registered processor (Visa/MC certifications)
2. Become non-bank acquirer (NIBSS onboarding)
3. ISO certifications (9001, 22301, 27001) + resolve 24 CBN audit exceptions
4. Mobile-led merchant payments (tokenization, contactless)
5. New support model — 60% managed direct-to-bank instances

### Organizational Structure
- Executive Management, Product, Enterprise Engineering, Finance, Compliance
- Product teams: DCIR, Direct Debit, Consolidated Switch, TPP Engineering, Monitoring/SRE, Tech Support (App + Implementation), PM (Delivery + Coordination)
- Monnify not present as separate OKR track in 2024 (appeared by 2025)

## Cross-Cutting Observations
1. Bank expansion was the biggest bottleneck across all products
2. Consolidated Switch outperformed all new products
3. Sprint velocity underperformed systemically (50-75% across all teams)
4. Reliability improved significantly despite product delivery challenges
5. Mobile payments/tokenization remained aspirational — no recorded results in H1
