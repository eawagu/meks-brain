## Definition

Performance Management is the organizational system for evaluating, developing, and calibrating employee performance — encompassing goal-setting, periodic reviews, feedback mechanisms, and remediation paths.

## Components at Moniepoint/TeamApt

- **Lattice** — platform used for performance reviews and feedback cycles (e.g., Apr 10 2026 review deadline)
- **[[Performance Improvement Plan]]** — formal remediation framework for documented performance gaps
- **Manager Effectiveness Survey** — Q3 2025 survey data used as input to PIP decisions
- **Upward Feedback Report** — Q4 2025 report referenced in PIP documentation
- **[[OKR Planning]]** — quarterly objectives that feed into performance evaluation

## Interaction with Other Concepts

Performance management intersects with [[Resource Allocation]] (underperformers affect capacity planning), hiring pipelines (backfill decisions), and organizational design (role scope changes after PIPs).
