## Definition

A platform ecosystem consists of a core platform and a surrounding community of third parties (developers, content creators, hardware manufacturers) whose contributions expand the platform's value. The platform provider sets the rules; third parties build within them; users benefit from both.

## Why It Matters

Platform ecosystems create compounding value: more developers → more apps → more users → more developers. This flywheel is difficult to replicate once established, creating durable competitive moats.
