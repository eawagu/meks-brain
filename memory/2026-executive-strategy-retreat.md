## Overview

The [[Moniepoint]] 2026 Executive Strategy Retreat is a 3-day event for senior leadership to align on 2026 objectives and roadmap.

## Details
- **Dates:** Tuesday April 14 – Thursday April 16, 2026
- **Location:** Chicago Booth, One Bartholomew Close, Barts Square, London EC1A 7BL
- **Hotel:** Leonardo Royal Hotel London St Paul's
- **Organized by:** [[Pawel Swiatek]], [[Julia Penido]]
- **3 dinners planned** (Thursday dinner is special, attendance mandatory)

## Presentation Requirements
Per Pawel's March 31 instructions, every presenter covers: (1) 2026 OKRs anchored to 5 Group-level Objectives, (2) Q1 progress, (3) Q2-Q4 plan. No presentation template confirmed.

## Group-Level Objectives
1. Become the most liked and habit-forming financial services provider
2. Achieve sustainable market leadership in core markets
3. Grow revenue with disciplined unit economics and capital efficiency
4. Build world-class risk, compliance, and operational resilience without slowing execution
5. Manage operating costs responsibly

## Day 2 Sessions (Apr 15, 2026)

### TeamApt CEO Deck — [[Dennis Ajalie]], [[Frank Atashili]], [[Damilare Ogunnaike]]
32-slide presentation covering TeamApt's strategic position. Key themes: 86% Moniepoint revenue dependency acknowledged as risk, "build alongside then diversify" framing, ₦8.91B/year card pricing gap vs market rates (decision required), FY2026 budget ₦16.9B with July PBITDA breakeven, 6 growth levers (international cards, DD/MADD, GoSubscribe, partnerships, TSC, TSA collections), Monnify Q1 at 130% of budget but VA commoditization forcing pivot.
→ [[source — TeamApt Strategy Retreat 2026 CEO Deck (Dennis Ajalie, Apr 15)]]

### Customer Rewards, Sales OKRs, Growth Strategy
A/B testing revealed rewards have no significant retention impact on existing customers (positive only for "honeymoon" new users). VAS emerged as the strongest retention lever — 80% M12 retention for VAS users vs collapse for transfer-only. Sales OKRs: 4.36M MAU, ₦864B GLPV, 3.9M active POS. BRM network optimization: 30K BRMs but only 10K productive, deactivation policy introduced. CAM team formation approved. Monnify targeting international businesses (Teemu, Udemy, Netflix, Spotify).
→ [[source — Strategy Retreat Day 2: Customer Rewards, Sales OKRs, Growth Strategy (Apr 15)]]

### Platform Performance, Security, Customer Experience
MoneyCRM enhancements (real-time visibility, 3→1 day payouts). Onboarding & Channels: 88.2% conversion, zero third-party account fraud since late 2025, 3D liveness verification, 100% CBN compliance, CSAT 90.4%. USSD billing turned profitable (₦1.95B collected, 120% cost coverage). Account Payments: 70% YoY revenue growth, 23% of company revenue, success rate approaching 98%, 2-3s completion time. [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] mandated percentile metrics. Erroneous transfer self-service solution targeting May launch. Khalil and FaaS presentations postponed to Day 3.
→ [[source — Strategy Retreat Day 2: Platform Performance, Security, Customer Experience (Apr 15)]]

1. **[[Project Phoenix]] / TSP Build** — full composable FinOps/TSP platform, 6-month One Platform timeline, architecture boundary decision (cloud vs on-prem)
2. **[[GoSubscribe]]** — agent-led distribution model, POS and Web channels readiness
3. **International acquiring** — Visa L3 certifications, MPGS + Cybersource, CMS launch
4. **[[Monnify]] market position recovery** — competitive dynamics vs Paystack/Flutterwave
5. **Card pricing decision** — ₦8.91B/year revenue gap, final fee % and cap required
6. **Moniepoint dependency** — 86% concentration, 6 diversification levers
