## Overview

**BNPL** (Buy-Now-Pay-Later) is a lower-risk, lower-yield consumer lending product planned at the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]] as part of the [[Consumer Lending Strategy]] pivot away from cash loans.

## Rationale

Cash loans have higher risk and higher yield; diversifying to BNPL broadens the consumer portfolio and reduces blast radius of any cash-loan risk misstep.
