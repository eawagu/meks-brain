## Opportunity

Research confirms power is a top small-business problem; device prices as low as ₦380,000–₦400,000 make these customers loanable who wouldn't otherwise pre-qualify. Asset financing opens a new SME segment.

## Partnership Model

Moniepoint will not distribute hardware. Partner requirements:
- **IoT-enabled devices** — remote shutoff for non-payment as repayment enforcement (e.g., solar-powered freezers).
- **End-to-end logistics** — partner-owned warehousing, technicians, after-sales support. Service failure causes non-payment.

## Scale Requirement

1,000–2,000 units/day to be "interesting"; floor 300–400/day. No single partner has this capacity today.

## Strategy

- Acquire multiple partners by region.
- Another entity (not Moniepoint) must build the distribution layer.
- Smartphones likely the only category with existing scaled partners.
- Initial solar financing scale: target existing profitable business clusters.

[[Shell Foundation]] provides the credit guarantee and has recommended potential partners.
