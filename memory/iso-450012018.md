## Overview

ISO 45001:2018 is the international standard for Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems ([[OHSMS]]). It specifies requirements for an OHSMS that helps organizations provide safe and healthy workplaces by preventing work-related injury and ill health.

## TeamApt Implementation

ISO 45001:2018 is one of five standards within [[TeamApt]]'s [[Integrated Management System]] (alongside [[ISO 9001]] Quality, ISO 27001 Information Security, ISO 22301 Business Continuity, and ISO 20000 IT Service Management).

## 2026-04-30 — ISO Audit Close-Out Recommendation

The 2026-04-30 [[ISO Audit]] close-out session recommended **certifying TeamApt's [[OHSMS]] against ISO 45001:2018**, alongside continued maintenance of the four other standards. Audit recorded no major or minor nonconformities; 5 observations and 4 opportunities for improvement across the IMS; one of the 10 next-step remediation actions specifically targets OHS training-effectiveness verification.
