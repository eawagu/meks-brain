## Summary

A proposal from [[Dosh Services]] to [[DavoDani Microfinance Bank]] for a full website redesign. The project scope covers design/branding, responsive development, customer tools (loan calculator, application forms), CMS, SEO optimization, and security/compliance. Total cost is ₦7,000,000 with an 8-week timeline and 50/30/20 payment terms.

## Key Points

- **Scope**: Fresh design aligned with brand identity, responsive development, loan calculator, online loan/savings application forms, CMS for staff content updates, SEO-friendly structure with analytics, SSL and secure forms
- **Timeline**: 8 weeks — Weeks 1-2 (research/design), Weeks 3-5 (development), Weeks 6-7 (testing), Week 8 (deployment/training)
- **Cost**: ₦7,000,000 — 50% upfront, 30% after UI screen completion, 20% on project completion
- **Post-launch**: 30 days of support included
- **Reference**: LAPO Microfinance Bank website redesign (no metrics or contact provided)
- **Vendor**: Dosh Services Limited, specializing in digital solutions for financial institutions
