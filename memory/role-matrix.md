## What

A **Role Matrix** is an integration artifact used during bank onboarding to a TeamApt-deployed system — it defines which bank user roles have which permissions on the system. The matrix is jointly authored: the bank supplies its role/permission needs; TeamApt maps them against the system's capabilities and produces a consolidated matrix that both sides agree on. Once the matrix is finalized, the bank can configure its users and the integration can move to training.

## Known instances

### Zenith Bank ATS (Apr 2026)

Bank-supplied matrix was incomplete and lacked specificity. [[Ifeoluwa Oguntona]], Gaffa ([[Abdulgafar Obeitor]]) and the bank met so the bank could explain their needs and knowledge gaps. Gaffa committed to re-editing the team's role matrix document to better explain the matrix and accommodate the bank's requirements so they can finalize their user roles and prepare for training, which is tentatively scheduled for Tuesday and Wednesday of next week.
