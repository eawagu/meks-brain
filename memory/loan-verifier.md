## Overview

The Loan Verifier initiative consolidates customer risk rating, Customer Due Diligence (CDD), and Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) information into a single centralized database.

## Problem Addressed

Prior state: EDD forms were completed by analysts but not stored in a central database — led to repeated work for the same customer and contributed to low CSAT.

## Strategic Role

Foundational for growing revenue through the Fraud Dex channel by directing customers to better-monitored and controlled channels.
