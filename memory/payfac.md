## Definition

A Payment Facilitator (PayFac) is an aggregator that sponsors a pipeline of sub-merchants on its own acquiring rails — the PayFac is the master merchant with the card scheme and acquirer, and it onboards sub-merchants under itself rather than each sub-merchant obtaining its own merchant account.

## At TeamApt

PayFac pipeline is one of [[Monnify]] commercial's principal growth vectors — named explicitly in the Apr 22, 2026 Org Movements brief as a retained OpCo commercial responsibility.

### Under Project Phoenix

- **Stays in OpCo:** Monnify commercial including PayFac pipeline stays with [[TeamApt]] OpCo under [[Daniel Ojinaka]] (dual business lead, Monnify + Direct Debit).
- **Direct Debit 2026 OKRs continue** — including "5 new PayFacs" target for the year.
- **Commercial layer stays** — system moves to [[Payment Gateway Platform]] under [[Damilare Ogunnaike]], commercial layer (merchants, PayFac pipeline) stays with Daniel.
