iCard compliance is a virtual-account compliance initiative under [[Cards and Account All Hands]]. Approach: a virtual account generated by a merchant VN remains exclusively within that merchant's periphery (no cross-merchant leakage). RFCs/PRDs were created the week of Apr 13; dev kickoff scheduled for week of Apr 20. Source: [[Cards and Account All Hands - 2026_04_20 10_28 WAT]]
