## Business Structure (Product Division — Frank Atashili)

**Revenue Drivers:**
- [[Direct Debit]] (CDD) — BL: [[Daniel Ojinaka]]. Products: MADD, [[GoSubscribe]], SAFE
- [[Monnify]] — Head: [[Damilare Ogunnaike]]. Products: Collections, Disbursements, VAS, Channels & Onboarding

**Enablers:**
- [[Third Party Processing]] (TPP) — Head: [[Tracy Ojaigho]]. Services: Issuer Processing ([[Blessing Abel-Oguche]]), Acquirer Processing ([[Taiwo Ogundipe]]). Engineering: Switch Engineering, TPP Engineering (Lead: [[Oluwabunmi Oyefisayo]])
- [[Domestic Switching]] — BL: [[Tunde Okufi]]. Teams: Switching Solutions ([[Kevin Ng'Eno]]), AptPay Suite ([[Abdulgafar Obeitor]]), PTSP ([[Unwana Enang]])

**Shared:** PMO ([[Idris Aliyu]]), Product Design ([[Kelvin Ogumor]])
