Dangote Group's petroleum refinery and petrochemicals entity (DPRP). License no 0020/D/2025, Dangote Industries Free Zone, Lekki. Engaged [[TeamApt]] as a digital platform partner to support subscription and settlement infrastructure, investor onboarding, and payment solutions for its proposed IPO on the [[Nigerian Exchange Group]]. [[Murat Erden]] is CFO and signatory.

## NDA — Fully Executed 2026-04-15

Mutual NDA between DPRP and TeamApt Limited is fully signed as of 07:34 UTC on 2026-04-15 (per Juro confirmation email). [[Dennis Ajalie]] signed on behalf of TeamApt/Moniepoint; [[Emeka Awagu]] also on the signature list. This removes the NDA as a blocker for substantive IPO platform engagement — scope can now widen from the high-level subscription/settlement/investor onboarding framing in the draft to specific technical discovery, pricing, and integration design.

## Partnership

Proposed scope in the NDA covers: payment solutions, subscription infrastructure, and settlement infrastructure for the planned IPO. See [[Dangote Non Disclosure Agreement (Draft)]] for the executed terms and [[Initial Public Offering]] for context on the listing mechanics.
