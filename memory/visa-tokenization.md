## Definition

**Visa Tokenization** refers to integrating the Visa Token Service (VTS) for digital card tokenization — replacing the current pseudo-digitization (PAN stored securely on device, transmitted via EMV NFC) provided by [[Sudo Africa]] with VTS-issued tokens.

## Roadmap position (Apr 21 KT)

Third item on the new Cards platform sequence, after Visa launch and Tap-to-Pay:

1. Visa launches on the new CMS.
2. Tap-to-Pay ships alongside Visa.
3. **Visa Tokenization** + Africa details follow.
4. CX revamp.

## Vendor decision

Under the 2026 roadmap Priority 2 (Visa Tokenization & VAS), [[Sudo Africa]] may be replaced if Moniepoint moves to full Visa tokenization via a proper VTS provider — decision likely Q4 2026.
