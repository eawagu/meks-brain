## Overview

The NDPA (Nigerian Data Protection Agency) is Nigeria's data protection regulator.

## TMAPT Engagement

- Compliance audit completed Q1 2026
- NDPA trust mark issued — confirms TMAPT is NDPA compliant
- Data protection function built from scratch at Moniepoint, integrating consent management into products
- Prior-year penalty drove current gap-closure work; continuous regulator engagement
