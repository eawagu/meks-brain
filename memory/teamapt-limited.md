## Overview

TeamApt Ltd is the payments infrastructure subsidiary of [[Moniepoint]] Inc. Every POS tap, account transfer, and card payment processed by the group flows through TeamApt infrastructure. [[Emeka Awagu]] serves as CTO, reporting to [[Dennis Ajalie]] (CEO). See [[Moniepoint]] for the parent group.

## Scale (Q1 2026)
- 6M average daily card transactions
- Q1 revenue ₦2.45B (125% of budget)
- 16 issuers on card switch
- ₦43B cash position
- 98.11% average uptime
- 250+ staff across Monnify (116), Switching (52), TPP (18), DD (8)

## CBN License Portfolio
- **Switching & Processing** — foundation license for all 4 businesses
- **Non-Bank Acquiring** — TPP acquirer processing & Monnify web acquiring
- **PTSP** — terminal deployment & management
- **SuperAgent** — agent network management

## Moniepoint Revenue Dependency — 86%

Most features, transaction volume, and revenue depend on [[Moniepoint]]'s card payments ecosystem. Imbalanced relationship: Moniepoint controls timelines, delays delivery, reports large revenue while constraining TeamApt from charging market rates. TSA fees ceased — removed largest revenue line. Strategic framing: "Build alongside, then diversify."

## Card Pricing Gap

- Current: 0.1% capped at ₦10; TeamApt share 30% = ₦3/txn
- Market rate: 0.3% capped at ₦15; average fee ₦7.50/txn
- At 5.5M daily transactions: current ₦5.94B/year vs market rate ₦14.85B/year
- **Gap: ₦8.91B/year unrealized revenue**
- Decision required at strategy retreat: final fee percentage and cap

## Diversification — Six Growth Levers

1. **International Card Payments** — Visa & MC acquiring (TPP); newly obtained Visa license + MC processor cert; target $1M+ revenue
2. **[[Direct Debit Program]] & MADD** — high-margin recurring rail; Phase 1: NIBSS BVN account linkage by June
3. **[[GoSubscribe]]** — DD + niche payments on POS (DSTV/GOTV, utilities, micro-pensions, micro-health insurance); agent-led via 25K+ agents
4. **Strategic Partnerships** — new PayFac onboarding (GlobalPay, LendSqr, [[HabariPay]], Paystack, Cyberpay); switch-to-switch integrations
5. **TSC (Settlement & Clearing)** — third-party S&C; Dennis discussed thresholds with NIBSS; planned
6. **TSA Collections** — emerging opportunity; early days
