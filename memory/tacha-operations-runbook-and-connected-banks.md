## Summary

Operationalization checklist, bank readiness matrix, and UAT/test data for TACHA platform onboarding.

## Key Points

- 7-step onboarding: access, FI setup, acquirer/issuer/merchant, participant, ledger, fees, validation, go-live
- 14 connected banks: Polaris (settlement), Keystone, Wema, Fidelity, Access, Union, FCMB, Moniepoint MFB, Zenith, GTB, Ecobank, Sterling, First Bank, UBA
- NSS readiness: Polaris, Wema (live); others planned
- Success criteria: 1-week stability, 100% reconciliation, zero manual intervention
