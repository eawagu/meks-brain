## Summary
Balanced scorecard for [[Oluwayimika Debo-Carpenter]] (Lead, Third Party Processing Operations – [[TeamApt]]). Financial and Internal Process share the highest weight at 35% each, reflecting the dual mandate of financial integrity and operational excellence.

## Key Points
- **Financial (35%):** ≥99% settlement accuracy, 0% revenue leakage, ≤24 hour reconciliation break resolution, ≥99% settlement SLA compliance
- **Customer/Stakeholder (20%):** ≤48 hour dispute resolution, ≥90% internal stakeholder satisfaction, ≤1 settlement complaint/month, ≥99% reporting accuracy
- **Internal Process (35%):** 100% daily reconciliation completion, ≤0.05% outstanding breaks, settlement within SLA, continuous error rate reduction, 100% SOP documentation, ≥95% RAI (reconciliation breaks resolved within 24h), weekly report by 3pm Thursdays
- **Learning & Growth (10%):** ≥4 professional training programs/year, continuous team capability improvement, ≥2 process improvements/quarter, ≥70% automation ratio
- **Reconciliation Aging Index (RAI):** New KPI — ≥95% of breaks resolved within 24 hours
