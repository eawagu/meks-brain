## Summary

Interchange rates 0.5-2.5% by MCC, product type, geography. Chargeback fees: $15-25 retrieval, processing fees, arbitration fees. Regional variations: EMV vs non-EMV, domestic vs cross-border, premium vs standard. Acquirers must publish fees to merchants with advance notice for changes.

## Key Points

- Base interchange: 0.5-2.5% varying by MCC, product, geography
- Chargeback retrieval: $15-25 per request
- EMV transactions get preferential rates vs non-EMV
- Fee transparency: acquirers must publish to merchants
