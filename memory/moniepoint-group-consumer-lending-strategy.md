## Pivot Thesis

[[Moniepoint|Moniepoint Inc.]]'s consumer lending shifts to small ticket / short tenor / high count. 30/60/90-day terms optimized for repeat use; projected 3–4 loans/customer/year.

## Phasing

- **Year 1 (2026)**: capped ₦34.6B exposure on a Moniepoint-book pilot — learn fast, control risk. Small loss expected.
- **Year 2 (2027)**: scale to the full ~6.3M Moniepoint user base after risk validation. Larger profit at 18 cost-of-funds assumption (excl. CAC).
- **End-2027 target**: ₦150B GLPV at ~7% monthly portfolio yield.

## Product Mix

- Reduce reliance on cash loans.
- Introduce [[BNPL]] and other lower-risk / lower-yield products.

## Risk Posture

- 5% monthly cost of risk by end-2027 (conservative).
- 0.1–0.2% for prospective business loans.
- Phantom ₦9.7B 2027 interest expense used as pricing discipline to avoid crashes.

## Collections

Compliant-and-persistent rule — no unethical recovery practices even though sensational market stories exist. Stories often seeded; being perceived as soft spreads fast.
