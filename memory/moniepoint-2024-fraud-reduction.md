## Overview

Moniepoint 2024 Fraud Reduction refers to the 2024 initiative — primarily driven by the Product Security Team in concert with Onboarding — that resulted in zero third-party mail account incidents from late 2024 onward.

## Components

- 3D Liveness Verification (Facetech) for onboarding
- Risk-Based Challenge System across account lifecycle
- Onboarding flow tightening and device sensing
- Monitoring and response upgrades

## Outcome

Zero third-party mail account incidents from late 2024 through 2026 Q1, a material change from prior fraud posture.
