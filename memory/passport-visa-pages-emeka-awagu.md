## Stamp Code Note

Three-letter codes appearing on Nigerian immigration stamps (DTD, SOD, SOA, DPD, ARD, VP) have no authoritative public documentation. Internal evidence across the composite shows SOD appearing on both departures (25 Mar 2017, 23 Aug 2019, 13 Nov 2019, 03 Nov 2024) and arrivals (21 Sep 2025), suggesting the codes are **officer/desk identifiers rather than direction indicators**. Direction labels in this inventory are derived from the stamp's arrival/departure wording and corroborating records (user personal log, subsequent trip stamps), not from the 3-letter code.
