## Summary

[[DavoDani Microfinance Bank]]'s 2026 IT strategic document outlining seven initiative areas for digital transformation. The plan covers SEO strategy, website redesign, AI-driven customer experience, social media dominance, cybersecurity and IT risk management, ATM card deployment, and sourcing a new core banking platform. Total estimated budget is approximately ₦138.5M.

## Key Points

- **SEO Strategy** (₦5.5M): Technical SEO, content SEO (bi-monthly posts), local SEO with Google Business Profile, link building; tools include Ahrefs/SEMrush
- **Website Restructure** (₦7M): New UI/UX, conversion-focused CTAs, AI chatbot, CRM integration, accessibility compliance
- **AI-Driven CX** (₦5M): Omnichannel platform (WhatsApp Business API, web chat, email, social, IVR), WhatsApp chatbot for balance/loan status/FAQs, auto voice calls for loan reminders, CRM ticketing, sentiment analysis; tools: Twilio/Infobip, Freshdesk/Zoho Desk
- **Social Media & Digital Marketing** (₦8M): Video-first content strategy, channel expansion (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube Shorts), paid advertising
- **Cybersecurity** (₦13M): EDR endpoint protection, phishing simulations, security awareness training, incident response drills; certifications: ISO 27001, CISM/CISA, CEH, CISSP
- **ATM Card Deployment** (cost TBD): Verve & Debit cards, usage incentives, reduce cash dependency
- **Core Banking** (₦100M): Sourcing scalable, API-driven, cloud-native platform; candidates: Finova, Vino, Ncube, Microsoft Nav, Fintellia
