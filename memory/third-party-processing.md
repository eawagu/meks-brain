## Definition

Third Party Processing (TPP) is the operations function at [[TeamApt]] responsible for managing payment transactions processed through external switching networks — [[Interswitch]], Habaripay, CoralPay, and IDRS/DCIR. TPP handles settlement reconciliation, chargeback management, and incident reporting for these third-party routes.

## Organizational Structure

- Lead: [[Oluwayimika Debo-Carpenter]] (reports to [[Tolulope Obianwu]])
- Governed by the [[TPP Operations Doctrine]] (v1.0, March 10, 2026)

## Scope

- MPGS (Mastercard Payment Gateway Service) settlement
- MIP (Mastercard Interface Processor) clearing
- Visa transaction settlement
- Chargeback dispute management across Interswitch Arbiter 2.0, Habaripay Sparkpay, CoralPay Cgate, IDRS, and DCIR

## Core Principles (from Doctrine)

Seven operating principles anchored on: accuracy over speed, settlement integrity (traceable, reconciled, supported, explainable), auditability of every financial movement, and regulatory compliance as a floor.
