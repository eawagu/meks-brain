## Definition

Third Party Processing (TPP) is an industry term for payment processing performed by a third-party service provider on behalf of card issuers, acquirers, or merchants. Includes acquirer processing, issuer processing, card scheme-certified processing, and ancillary services (EMV data preparation, chargeback management, personalization). Distinct from first-party processing (where the issuer or acquirer operates their own processing infrastructure).

## Core Capabilities

- **Acquirer processing** — handling transactions on behalf of merchant-acquiring banks
- **Issuer processing** — handling transactions on behalf of card-issuing banks
- **EMV Data Preparation** — cryptographic key and card personalization data generation
- **Card scheme certifications** — Visa, Mastercard, Verve, Afrigo, etc.
- **Settlement reconciliation** — matching scheme and bank records
- **Chargeback management** — scheme dispute handling
