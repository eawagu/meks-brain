## Definition

Third Party Processing (TPP) is the industry term for payment processing by a third-party service provider on behalf of card issuers, acquirers, or merchants — distinct from first-party processing.

## Core Capabilities

- Acquirer processing
- Issuer processing
- EMV Data Prep
- Card scheme certifications (Visa, Mastercard, Verve, Afrigo)

## Under Project Phoenix (Apr 22, 2026 brief)

TeamApt's TPP department is **merging with [[Domestic Switching]]** into a single consolidated OpCo business line: **Switching & Processing (S&P)** — led by [[Babatunde Okufi]] (CBDO double-hat). The S&P OpCo business retains Acquirer/Issuer processing customers, PTSP/PTAD, ATS client relationships, Juliana Switch commercial, and ACT. Platform build moves to [[TSPP]] and [[CI&P]].

Acquirer Processing team stays in TeamApt on BAU in Phase 1 (absorbed later into CI&P Team 1 Card Processing). Issuer Processing and CMS teams stay in TeamApt for now (absorbed later into CI&P Team 2 Card Issuance).

## Known Entity

- [[TeamApt Third Party Processing]] — TeamApt's pre-Phoenix TPP department, led by [[Tracy Ojaigho]] (who converts to Head, CI&P under Phoenix).
