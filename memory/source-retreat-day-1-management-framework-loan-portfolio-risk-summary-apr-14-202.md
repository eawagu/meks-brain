## Summary

Retreat Day 1 summary document (London, 2026 Strategy Retreat) where [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] articulates the organizational operating model: goals cascade, structure serves goals, people fit structure, and seven management principles govern leaders. Deep dive on UX/CSAT systemic failure (no CSAT goal, UX agency-style structure, no feedback system) and four corrective fixes. Secondary topic: loan portfolio strategy — risk-based pricing of high-risk buckets rather than rejection, value-target visibility alongside volume.

## Key Points

- **Management framework (7 principles):** Goals → Structure → People → Incentives → Context → Systems → Governance. Applied as a personal leadership checklist: "for my whole unit, I must ensure…"
- **8 judgment traits for people:** Customer Obsession, Craft, Systemization, Urgency, Ownership, No Ego, Candor, Integrity. Culture = enforceable behaviors (not wall posters).
- **Goals principle:** "If it's not defined, it doesn't exist." [[Pavel]] mandated since start of year on goal setting only. Leaders present their own numbers this retreat — not the CEO announcing them.
- **UX/CSAT diagnosis:** Vacant CSAT role ~6 months, UX structured as separate agency, PMs appraising designers (power imbalance), no cross-org feedback system. Surfacing only via sales = basic/complaint items → suboptimal product.
- **UX/CSAT fixes (Key Decisions):** (1) Fill vacant CSAT role, (2) Embed CX teams in product teams, (3) Link bonuses to CSAT metrics, (4) Build quant + qual feedback systems.
- **Card-transaction incident post-mortem:** Engineer inserted faulty DB records (craft failure), on-call SRE misdiagnosed (judgment failure), system lacked input validation (system failure). Fix: re-emphasize diligence standard; if pattern, reassign/exit; build input validation system.
- **Loan strategy:** Price highest-risk bucket by risk rather than reject. Value target now formally introduced alongside volume. Guarantor/verification reduction being tested for revenue-vs-NPL tradeoff.
- **Solar financing:** No appetite for device supply/maintenance/distribution — Moneypoint stays financier only.
- **Digital loan product:** Priced higher due to increased risk profile.
- **Talent:** [[Chloe]] mandate — build HR org capable of attracting high-caliber talent without sacrificing standards. Pipeline model (hire-for-potential + train) likely needed to achieve scale.
- **Tooling:** "Moose" platform for goals/KPIs/metrics; working with "Cadello" on precise definitions.
