## The Eight Traits

Defined at the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]] by [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] as the evaluation rubric for everyone in the company — for hiring, appraisal, and self-assessment. These are the People component of the [[Organizational Framework]].

1. **Customer Obsession** — fundamental empathy for the customer's situation; investigating reported issues rather than dismissing them.
2. **Craft** — domain expertise from first principles, not rote memorization. Individuals own their own craft development; the company provides training but not mastery.
3. **Systemization** — encoding learnings into repeatable procedures so others don't reinvent the wheel. Expectation increases with seniority.
4. **Urgency** — disciplined speed, not recklessness. Critical in fast-moving environments.
5. **Ownership** — proactive problem-solving; offering to investigate collaboratively vs. demanding evidence first. Timmy cited as exemplar.
6. **No Ego** — flat structure, open information flow; dropping titles and hierarchical deference.
7. **Candor** — telling inconvenient truths. Hiding truths prevents the organization from facing reality.
8. **Integrity** — acting rightly and truthfully even when no one is watching.
