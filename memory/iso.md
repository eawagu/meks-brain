## Overview

The International Organization for Standardization (ISO) publishes management system standards that [[TeamApt Limited]] is certified against. TeamApt undergoes annual ISO surveillance audits.

## Cadence

- The annual ISO Standards Audit commenced Mon Apr 20, 2026 and is ongoing through the following week (per [[CEO Gazette - 24th April 26]]).
- The Mar 31, 2026 [[TeamApt MANCo Meeting - 31 March 2026]] noted ISO Surveillance Audit was scheduled for April 20 (5th standard, OSS), with mock audit ongoing with the OPEX team.
