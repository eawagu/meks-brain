## Summary
TeamApt's OKR framework, cadence, and 2026 status tracking across business departments, product engineering teams, and pillar divisions, with availability gaps noted.

## Key Points
- Two-level OKR structure: Annual (business departments + pillar divisions) and Quarterly (product engineering teams)
- Quarterly OKRs include Q-end recap, lessons learned, KR performance review, delivery recap, and upcoming proposals
- OKR cadence: Annual OKRs at financial year start; Quarterly OKRs per team with structured prior-quarter review
- Scoring convention: Achieved (≥90%), Partially Achieved (50-89%), Not Achieved (<50%), Dropped/Cancelled
- Alignment model: Team quarterly OKRs reference parent annual OKRs; pillar division standards embed in every product team
- Available 2026 OKRs: Direct Debit (Business + Product), Monnify (Business + 3 Product teams), TPP (Business + 2 Product teams), Juliana Switch, PTSP/PTAD, Regulatory, PMO, Design, Engineering, Infrastructure, SRE, DevRel, Legal, Marketing, People Team
- Missing: Domestic Switching (annual), MADD, GoSubscribe, SAFE, VAS, Issuer Processing, Acquirer Processing, KYC, Transaction Monitoring, Fraud Desk, Operations, Data, Security, Project Delivery
