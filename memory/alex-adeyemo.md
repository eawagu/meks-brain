## Overview

Alex Adeyemo is the **Engineering Head of [[TSPP]]** (Transaction Switching & Processing Platform) under [[Project Phoenix]], confirmed in [[Frank Atashili]]'s Apr 22, 2026 [[TeamApt-Platformization-Org-Movements (1)|Org Movements brief]]. Based in the UK. Works alongside Frank (Product Head, Canada), Design Head (TBD), and [[Oluwabunmi Oyefisayo]] (Lead Technical PM, Australia) as the TSPP leadership triad.

## TSP Phase 1 Role

Alex is Tech Lead of the TSP Phase 1 strike teams (per [[TSP Phase 1 Project Plan DRAFT (Apr 10)]]). The team structure: 2 strike teams (15 people) — Team Spine ([[Sulaiman Adeeyo]] EM) + Team Adapters ([[Sunday Ayodele]] EM). [[Ravi Jakhodia]] is Program Lead; [[Ravi Veluguleti]] Card Switching Domain Owner. PM: Bunmi.

Strike team (build) draws TeamApt ICs [[Abeeb Ahmad]] (Juliana Switch), [[Muhammad Siddiqui]] (Principal SE), [[Christopher Ogbosuka]] (built TACHA), plus 2–3 UK engineers from Alex's network, supplements from MonieWorld and Cards ([[MoniePoint MFB]]).

## Position on Phoenix Architecture

Alex endorsed the position that TSP should be a **new build from scratch** — a composable FinOps platform — counter to [[Frank Atashili]]'s original "extend and formalize, not rebuild" framing. This is one of the active tensions in Project Phoenix.
