## Overview

JULS is the family of bank-specific settlement rails that feed [[Juliana]], TeamApt's domestic switching system. Members include [[JULS ACCESS]], [[JULS FCMB]], and [[JULS WEMA]].

## Shared-infrastructure failure pattern (Apr 23, 2026)

Per [[CEO Gazette - 24th April 26]]: JULS ACCESS hit 68.18% failure rate on Apr 23, exceeding the Wk1 peak of 63.64%. JULS FCMB simultaneously spiked to 25% the same day. Two providers collapsing in concert on the same date is a strong indicator of a shared infrastructure failure within the JULS network specifically — [[ISW]] (1.88%) and [[HABARIPAY]] (0.72%) remained insulated. See [[JULS Card Crisis]].
