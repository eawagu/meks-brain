## Summary

The Advisory Board Charter for [[ProduqtEdge]] (PE), revised v.2 dated 02 February 2026. It establishes a non-statutory advisory board of four members to provide strategic counsel across strategy, brand, product, governance, public sector, and talent domains. The charter is designed to mimic a statutory board and eventually transition into one as PE grows.

## Key Points

- **Vision**: Become Africa's most trusted partner for product-led growth and digital transformation
- **Strategic objectives**: Increase brand equity, introduce governance, grow clientele, improve AI-driven efficiency, productize services, develop portfolio products for recurring revenue
- **Revenue ambition**: 10x in 3 years
- **Advisory Board composition** (36-month tenure, renewable):
  - [[Niyi Yusuf]] — Chairman
  - [[Femi Omotayo]]
  - [[Collins Onuegbu]]
  - [[Emeka Awagu]]
- **Scope**: Strategy & Markets, Brand Elevation, Product & Technology, Governance & Org Maturity, Public Sector & Partnerships, Talent & Leadership
- **Compensation**: 1% equity (quarterly vesting over 3 years with 1-year cliff), 3% deal success fee on revenue from direct introductions, priority investor access
- **Meeting cadence**: Quarterly advisory sessions (2-3 hours), ad-hoc rapid consults (15-30 min), quorum requires 3 advisors including Chair
- **Key deliverables**: Quarterly Strategy Pack, minutes/action tracker within 5 working days, annual reflection report
- **Advisors do NOT**: exercise management control, make day-to-day decisions, carry fiduciary duties, have signatory authority
