## Overview

T-MAPS Switch is a third-party payment switch relationship that Moniepoint had routed transactions through. The relationship was terminated by OPay in a conflict escalation, as discussed in the 2026 UK Leadership Retreat Day 2 Platform Performance session.

## Impact

Termination forced Moniepoint to re-route affected traffic and reinforced the case for diversified switch partnerships. Fed into the Q1 2026 reliability picture.
