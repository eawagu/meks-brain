Infobip is an omnichannel cloud communications platform (CPaaS) providing SMS, WhatsApp Business API, voice, email, and chat infrastructure.

## DDMFB 2026 IT Strategy Reference
Per [[DDMFB_2026_IT_Strategy_Review]], Infobip (alongside [[Twilio]]) is cited as an example messaging infrastructure whose per-message fees alone would consume a large chunk of [[DavoDani Microfinance Bank]]'s ₦5M annual [[Customer Experience Platform]] budget for any meaningful transaction volume.
