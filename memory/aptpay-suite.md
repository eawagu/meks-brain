## Overview

AptPay Suite is [[TeamApt]]'s bank-deployed AptPay solution set — bundles ATS (Alternative Transaction Switching), deployed AptPay components at partner banks (Fidelity, Access, Polaris, GTBank-Habari, Union, Interswitch, Money Point, etc.), and related tooling.

Pre-Phoenix: led by [[Abdulgafar Obeitor]] ("Gafar") as PM Lead.

## Under Project Phoenix (Apr 22, 2026 brief)

### Hold-the-fort (stays in TeamApt)
- **[[Abdulgafar Obeitor|Gafar]] + AptPay BAU engineers** — continue BAU operations through TSPP Phase 1. No code freeze.
- AptPay Suite stays in the S&P (Switching & Processing) OpCo business.

### EM transfer out
- **[[Ketan Dhamasana]]** — EM transferring from AptPay Suite to CI&P Card Issuance (CMS, Card Controls, EMV Data Prep, Perso interface) under [[Emeka Awagu]] + [[Tracy Ojaigho]].

### Later phases
- AptPay Suite absorbs into [[TSPP]] — Gafar moves to TSPP ATS PM at that point.
- Switching Solutions and AptPay Suite together seed additional TSPP scope.
