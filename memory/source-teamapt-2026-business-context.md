[[TeamApt]] 2026 business context. CEO: [[Dennis Ajalie]]. Subsidiary of [[Moniepoint|Moniepoint Inc.]] (Group CEO: [[Tosin Eniolorunda]]). B2B payments infrastructure — switching, processing, gateway, direct debit.

## Key Points

### 2026 Strategic Focus Areas (Board Presentation, Feb 20, 2026)
1. Launch [[GoSubscribe]] (POS and Web) — position direct debit as new payment frontier in Nigeria
2. Develop Issuer and Acquirer Processing capabilities — alignment with group platformization, primary growth driver for 2027
3. Grow [[Monnify]] market/mind share in online acquiring, return gateway to improved earnings
4. Expand switching capabilities to other acquirers and switching entities
5. Maintain low operational and staff costs

### Business Portfolio
**Revenue Drivers:**
- **[[Direct Debit]]** (CDD) — [[Daniel Ojinaka]]. Products: MADD, GoSubscribe, SAFE. Key partners: CowryWise (live first merchant), Access Bank & Polaris (live issuers), Fidelity & Keystone (pilot-ready)
- **[[Monnify]]** — [[Damilare Ogunnaike]]. Products: Collections, Disbursements, VAS, Channels & Onboarding. Virtual accounts via Moniepoint (primary), SmartCash PSB, Access Bank. Card processing via Juliana Switch. International via MPGS, Visa Cybersource planned

**Enablers:**
- **[[TPP]]** — [[Tracy Ojaigho]]. Products: Acquirer Processing, Issuer Processing, Switch Engineering (Aptent), TPP Engineering (ACT). Visa acquiring license obtained, Mastercard pending. ACT first external client: [[Fidelity Bank]]
- **[[Domestic Switching]]** — [[Babatunde Okufi]]. Products: Juliana Card Switch (16 domestic issuers), Juliana Account Switch, [[AptPay Suite]] (ATS in banks), PTSP/PTAD

**Juliana Card Switch Issuers** (16 total): Direct — UBA, Access, FCMB, Keystone, Fidelity, Wema, Union, Parallax, Palmpay, Stanbic, Ecobank, Polaris. Via HabariPay — GTBank. Via CoralPay — FirstBank, Zenith, Providus, Unity

### Technology Platform Inventory
Key systems: Aptent (core switch), Juliana Card/Account Switch, [[TACHA]] (clearing/settlement), ATS (in-bank switch), ACS (authentication), GoSubscribe, SAFE, Monnify Payment Gateway, MPGS, ACT (clearing tool), EMV Data Prep, Rsync CMS, [[Loom]] (AML/fraud), Limit Engine, NSS (NIBSS settlement), NPS (ISO 20022), Harness (CI/CD, ~86% migrated)

### Revenue Models
- Direct Debit: ₦17.50 per transaction (₦25 merchant fee minus ₦7.50 issuer fee)
- Juliana Switch: 0.25% capped ₦18 (agency/POS), 0.5% capped ₦1,000 (online)
- TPP: ₦3 per transaction (acquirer processing), ₦40 per card (EMV data prep), 0.25% (DCF converter)

### 2025→2026 Structural Changes
AptPay Suite + Juliana consolidated under single "Domestic Switching" department. MADD absorbed into broader "Direct Debit / CDD" (includes GoSubscribe, SAFE). Previously 5 product units, now 4 business departments.
