## Overview

Revenue Leakage Prevention is the operational discipline of identifying and closing gaps that cause expected transaction revenue to go uncaptured. Distinct from [[Revenue Leakage]] (the failure state) — this concept covers the controls, targets, and processes designed to prevent it.

## Targets (2026)

- **0% revenue leakage** — zero-tolerance target on financial scorecards for Domestic Switching, Direct Debit, and TPP operations
- **≤0.1% of monthly transaction value** — Operations OKR threshold (see [[TeamApt Operations and Support Annual OKR 2026]])
- **≥99.95–99.99% fee reconciliation alignment** — per-department scorecard KRs
- **100% T+1 reconciliation completion** — settlement accuracy enforcer
