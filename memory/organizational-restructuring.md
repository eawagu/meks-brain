Organizational restructuring refers to deliberate reallocation of function ownership and accountability across organizational units. It typically signals a shift in operating model, leadership philosophy, or strategic direction. Generic management concept applied across industries.

## Common Patterns

- **Functional centralization** — consolidating distributed functions under a central owner (e.g., platform org absorbing product engineering teams)
- **Decentralization** — distributing centrally held functions to business units for autonomy
- **Span-of-control adjustments** — narrowing or widening direct reports for a role
- **Operating model shifts** — matrix ↔ functional ↔ divisional
