Organizational restructuring refers to deliberate reallocation of function ownership and accountability across organizational units. It typically signals a shift in operating model, leadership philosophy, or strategic direction.

## Instance: TeamApt Tech Function Migration (2026-04-16)
All tech functions under Mek's [[TeamApt]] CTO role are moving to the [[Platform Organization]], except [[Operations]]. This is a platform-centric consolidation — technical functions centralized under a platform org while the CTO retains direct ownership of Operations.
