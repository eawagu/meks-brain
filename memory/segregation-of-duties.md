## Overview
[[Segregation of Duties]] (SoD) is an [[ISO 27001]] Annex A control (A5.3) requiring that conflicting duties and responsibilities be separated so no single person can both execute and approve sensitive actions.

## TeamApt Finding (Apr 2026)
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], ISMS clause A5.3 OFI:
- "Proper segregation of conflicting duties and responsibilities was not done for the IT team."
- **Remediation:** establish a Segregation of Duties Procedure that outlines all the necessary duties that could conflict.
