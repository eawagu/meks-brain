## Summary
Balanced scorecard for [[Goodness]] (Lead, Domestic Switching & Processing – PTSP Operations). Financial perspective is highest weighted at 35%, with a zero-tolerance target for revenue leakage.

- **Learning & Growth (15%):** Full SME certification for role, 3+ improvement initiatives/quarter, 2 knowledge sessions/quarter, monthly insight reports, active participation in product reviews
- **Strategic objective:** "Zero revenue leakage across domestic switching operations"
