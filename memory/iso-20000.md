## Overview
[[ISO 20000]] is the international standard for IT Service Management (ITSM / SMS).

## TeamApt Implementation
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], ISO 20000 is one of five standards within the [[Integrated Management System]] at [[TeamApt]]. Documented objectives:
- Achieve 90% on-time completion of all active implementation projects by Q1 2026
- Ensure 99.9% service availability by Q1 2026
- Achieve 85% customer satisfaction by Q1 2026
- Ensure employees are aware of their service-management responsibilities
- Test service continuity plans annually for all critical IT services, with at least one major exercise

## Key Risks in Scope (Apr 2026)
- Inadequacy of resources for service delivery (Minor) — controls: proactive resource/system monitoring
- Emergency changes without assessment (Moderate) — controls: change management desk (capture, track, review, approve all changes)
- Inexperienced staff (Minor) — controls: continuous knowledge sharing
- Supplier/bank SLA breaches (Moderate) — controls: business-lead obligation confirmation, legal escalation
- Regulatory non-compliance (Major) — controls: procedure for identifying legal/regulatory/contractual requirements; Legal Register

## Audit Finding (Apr 2026)
SMS Clause 8.2.6 OFI: develop schedule for verification, validation, and accuracy of Configuration Items (CI).
