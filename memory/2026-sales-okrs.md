## Overview

Moniepoint's 2026 sales-side OKRs presented by [[SACE]] at the UK Retreat Day 2.

## Objective 1: BRM Relationship as Retention Driver
- KR1: NPS promoters citing BRM helpfulness 3% → 7%.
- KR2: NPS detractors citing BRM dissatisfaction — reduce (current 3%, target 1%).
- KR3: BRM-related C-SAT (current 72%, target 80%).

- SME MAU via BRMs: **4.36M** by Dec 2026.
- POS market-share gap vs nearest competitor: **67 → 70 percentile points**.
- Active POS: **3.9M** by Dec 2026.
- Total payment volume: **₦16.8B** (incl. bills + airtime) by end-year.
- Top BIO retention: **92%**.
- SME Lending GLPV: **₦864B** by Dec 2026.
- Lending penetration: **3.77%** of eligible (≈190,787 active loan accounts). Flagged too low by [[Tosin Eniolorunda]].
