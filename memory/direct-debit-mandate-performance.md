## Overview

Direct Debit Mandate Performance tracks the health of the [[Direct Debit Program]] across three dimensions: approval rate (upstream), success rate (execution), and mandate creation volume (top-of-funnel). Reported weekly in the [[Moniepoint Group CEO Gazette]].

## Latest reading — week of Apr 24, 2026

Per [[CEO Gazette - 24th April 26]]:

- **Approval rate: 31.66%** — three consecutive weeks with no meaningful recovery. Wk1 baseline 33.76%; Wk2 30.72%; this week 31.66%. The marginal +0.94pp WoW improvement is noise. **Three-week unresolved crisis.**
- **Success rate: 90.71%** (+5.14pp WoW) — highest in three weeks. Debit volume processed +19.8% WoW. Execution layer healthy and handling growing load. Bottleneck sits entirely at the **approval stage**, not in processing.
- **Mandate creation volume: -2.3% WoW, -7.1% since Wk1** — quietly shrinking three weeks running. Active mandate base eroding; compounds revenue impact of approval problem.

## February 2026 baseline (per [[TeamApt MANCo Meeting - 31 March 2026]])

Mandate activations: 1,206 → 1,828 weekly by month-end. Access Bank dominant. 96% mandate success, 38–43% approval, total volume >30K, value ₦50.4B.

## Open

- Why is approval stuck at ~31.66%? Bottleneck is upstream of processing — needs root-cause investigation.
