Mock audit is the [[OPEX]]-led internal-audit-simulation across TeamApt's [[Integrated Management System]] standards. Commenced April 2026, covering [[ISO 27001]] (ISMS), [[ISO 20000]] (SMS), [[ISO 9001]] (QMS), [[ISO 22301]] (BCM), and [[ISO 45001]] (OHS) using PDCA approach (Plan-Do-Check-Act). Results: 83.8% compliant, ~16% in progress, 1 minor non-conformity (already worked on). Precursor to external [[Surveillance Audit]]. Source: [[IMS Management Review Session - 2026_04_20 08_30 WAT]]
