## Overview

PAT (Profit After Tax) Margin is Moniepoint's central profitability ratio. At the 2026 UK Leadership Retreat Day 3 financial session, it was identified as the primary problem metric.

## Current & Target

- Current: 8%
- Target: mid-30s (peer parity with top Nigerian banks)

## Drivers of Low PAT

- Extreme promos (~₦140B in one account)
- Verve pricing (regulatory arbitrage)
- MP35 terminal depreciation
- Operational inefficiencies (COGS, OPEX, commissions, promos)

## 2026 Profitability Run Rate Targets

- 2026: 20%
- 2027: 30%

## Valuation Link

Company conservatively valued at 20x PAT — profitability directly increases employee share/stock value.
