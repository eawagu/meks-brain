## The Question

[[MP35 Fleet]] MP35P variant is locked to 3G / Android 5. Moniepoint must choose:

1. **Discontinue** — immediate write-off of depreciated NBV.
2. **4G upgrade** — invest in motherboard swap during repair cycle.
3. **Free-give** — deploy at no cost to drive commission revenue.

## Constraint: Market Perception

A technical fix reduced failure rate from ~1.7% to ~0.3%, but small reliability differences drive user attitudes. Market still penalizes 3G, and [[OPay]] actively demarkets by claiming 3G / Android 5 devices are illegal.

## Preferred Path

Regulatory intervention to curb anti-competitive claims, then pursue a **controlled 4-year write-off** of MP35. If regulatory relief fails, a larger immediate decision is needed.

## Secondary Reality

Even free MP35P is often refused by smaller merchants. Deployment mixing (e.g., 2 MP35P per 10) difficult to enforce in practice.
