## Stub

GT Bank (Guaranty Trust Bank) is a Nigerian bank in the [[Direct to Bank program]] integration pipeline.

## Activity

### 2026-04-22 D2B standup

[[Taiwo Baptista]] reported that GT Bank was **blocked yesterday due to a service facilitator being down**. An update is expected later today. Source: [[Direct to Bank Daily standup - 2026-04-22 08:21 WAT]].

### 2026-04-27 Project Delivery & Optimization Realignment

- **GT Bank interbank API SLA** — [[Nora]] resumed today. The team's first-pass SLA was the [[Access Bank]]-style template Nora used with other banks; **GT Bank's legal team rejected it** (couldn't track all the changes). GT Bank then provided their own slightly modified SLA with fewer clauses to change. Nora is reviewing this version; expected to revert with changes during the week. Owner: Nora.
- **Production APIs** — still not provided; flagged as the **only active escalation** at the meeting. [[Taiwo Baptista]] noted GT Bank has been the major bottleneck.
- Source: [[Project delivery and optimization realignment - 2026-04-27 - Transcript]].
