## Overview

USSD Fulfillment Billing is the commercial model Moniepoint applies to its USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Service Data) channel. It charges customers on successful transactions and is designed to make the USSD channel self-sustaining.

## Economics

- Charge: ₦10 per successful USSD transaction
- Cost: ₦6.5 per transaction
- Cost coverage: 120% (revenue exceeds underlying telco + infra cost)
- 2025 collection: ₦1.95B

## Strategic Importance

USSD is a critical channel for unbanked/underbanked customers in Nigeria. Self-funding the channel removes subsidy pressure and lets channel investment scale with usage rather than stand on a P&L subsidy line.
