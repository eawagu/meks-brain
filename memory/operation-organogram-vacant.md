## Summary
The org chart shows [[Tolulope Obianwu]] as head of Global Operations with five sub-functions: Core Operations/ITSM ([[Elsie Anucha]]), Monnify Operations ([[Emmanuel Olatunbosun]] / [[Temitope Osinowo]]), Domestic Switch ([[Oluwadamilola Odogbesu]] acting), TPP ([[Oluwayimika Debo-Carpenter]]), and Customer Support ([[Veronica Ebirewanlu]]). Direct Debit Operations is led by [[Mariam Davies]] but has 3 vacant officer roles.

## Key Points
- **Monnify Ops:** Led by [[Emmanuel Olatunbosun]] (Merchant Settlement) and [[Temitope Osinowo]] (Transaction Processing / Settlement & Recon); team includes Blessing Obioha, Chinenye Iloka, Samuel Ojuolape, Anthonia Oragwa, Vivian Iloh, and others
- **Domestic Switch (NDS):** Acting lead [[Oluwadamilola Odogbesu]]; team members include Omotoyosi Sanni, Peace Emmanuel, Christine Ogude, Chidinma Anyanwu; multiple vacancies in Routine Ops, Dispute Resolution (x2), Recon officer, and Team Lead
- **TPP Operations:** Led by [[Oluwayimika Debo-Carpenter]]; team: Ayooluwa Oladega (Routine Ops), Florence Olarinde (Recon), Peace Emmanuel (Dispute Resolution)
- **Direct Debit:** Led by [[Mariam Davies]]; Routine Ops, Recon, and Dispute Resolution roles all vacant
- **Customer Support:** Monnify Support ([[Veronica Ebirewanlu]]), Switch Support ([[Christine Ogude]] acting), Direct Debit Support (vacant)
- **Presented by:** [[Ezinne Ogoke]] (APM, Global Operations)
