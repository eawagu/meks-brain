## Overview

Sumac Microfinance Bank is the Kenyan microfinance bank Moniepoint is acquiring to enter the Kenyan market. The acquisition closed after approximately three years of work.

## Profile

- ~60 people.
- Top-5 Kenyan microfinance bank, originated from a savings-and-lending club.
- Board chair highlighted East Africa ~180M population (excluding South Sudan, Somalia).

## Strategic Role

- Primary rationale: **license acquisition** — not team, not customer book.
- Establishes Moniepoint's license-led M&A market-entry model.
- First non-Nigerian/UK deployment of [[Project Phoenix]] platformized product.
- Functions as the Kenya OpCo under the Phoenix [[IPCo/OpCo]] structure.
