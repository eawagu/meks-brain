## Overview

The Service Management System (SMS) is [[TeamApt]]'s IT service management framework within the [[Integrated Management System]] (IMS), aligned to ISO 20000. It covers IT service delivery, change/incident management, and service-level reporting.

## 2026-04-30 — ISO Audit Close-Out Next-Steps

The Apr 30 [[ISO Audit]] close-out session flagged two SMS-specific remediation actions among the 10 next-steps:

1. **Classify SMS improvements:** Detail and classify identified areas of improvement for the Service Management System.
2. **Define objective review periods:** Define review period and frequency for [[Information Security]], [[Business Continuity]], and Service Management System objectives.

Source: [[ISO Audit-Close out session – 2026_04_30 16_27 WAT – Notes by Gemini]].
