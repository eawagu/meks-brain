## Principle

At the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]], [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] declared: promotion is based on performance, not likability. Performance is the key to progression.

## Dependency on Candor

Candor — telling difficult truths — is foundational to meritocracy. Trust is lost in people who tell the boss what the boss wants to hear; it becomes impossible to know if they're being truthful. Tosin cited a recently-fired employee whose primary failure was trying to please the boss, leading to the failure of the organization he was leading.
