## Current State

Field credit officers are contracted under ICSR. Physical monitoring is critical to loan performance (vs. phone-based collection). Officer-to-loan ratio has degraded from ~100 to 400–500 as loan book outgrew hiring.

## Bottlenecks

- Hiring partner capacity insufficient.
- Remote areas (Bonny, inner lands) especially hard — officers must be local to be effective.
- Experienced-officer talent pool shallow outside urban hubs (Obi, Port Harcourt).
- "Current processes are just not working."

## Candidate Model

[[Paavo]]-referenced model: hire locally without prior experience and train in-house. Implies internal talent pipeline rather than market-hire dependency.
