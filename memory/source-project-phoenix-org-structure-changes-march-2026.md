Source: Moniepoint Platforms Conference deck, March 9-10, 2026. 5 slides detailing Project Phoenix legal entity and organizational structure. Complements existing [[Phoenix Platform Strategy and Vision]] and [[source — Project Phoenix PM Briefing Deck]].

## Summary

Presents simplified legal entity structure for [[Project Phoenix]] and the organizational trees under Moniepoint MFB Nigeria (Products & Services) and Moniepoint Technologies UK (Platforms & Products). Defines division of responsibilities between Operating Companies, IP/Platform Company, and Development Companies.

## Key Points

- **Legal entity structure** ([[Moniepoint Inc.]] USA as parent):
  - **OpCos:** [[Moniepoint MFB]] (Nigeria), [[TeamApt]] (Nigeria), [[MonieWorld]] (UK, fka Moniepoint GB), [[Sumac MFB]] (Kenya)
  - **IPCo:** Moniepoint Technologies UK — owns all platform IP
  - **DevCos:** MP Software Development (Nigeria), Moniepoint Technologies India, Moniepoint Rep Office Spain
- **OpCo responsibilities:** country- and license-specific; customer acquisition/retention/monetization; operational/compliance/risk; own P&L; pay IP royalties to IPCo.
- **IPCo responsibilities:** develops and owns all IP; product management, design, engineering; builds standardized/scalable/configurable platforms; evolves based on OpCo input; coordinates feature prioritization; receives royalty payments and software config/maintenance fees.
- **DevCo responsibilities:** country-specific entities employing product/design/engineering staff; staff assigned to Platform organization roles; operate local payroll; bill IPCo and/or OpCos for employment cost + markup.
- **Moniepoint MFB Nigeria** org tree (CBO-led): Banking Ops, Business Mgmt Software, Market Research, Business Lending, Payment Acceptance, POS & Devices, Direct Network Sales, Enterprise Network Sales, Marketing Business FS, Consumer Lending, Retail Marketing & Network Sales, Account Transfers, VAS Bill Pay/Airtime/Data, Savings, Cards, Onboarding & Account Mgmt, Customer Support, Field Verification, Branch Experience. Each node led by a general manager.
- **Moniepoint Technologies UK** org tree (Chief Platforms & Products Officer): three divisions —
  - **Business Banking Platforms:** Transaction Switching & Processing ([[TSP]]) with sub-platforms (Card Switching, Account Switching, VAS Switching, Integrations); Card Acceptance & Processing; Payment Gateway; POS Hardware & Devices; Card Issuance & Processing; Cross-Border Payments.
  - **Digital Banking Platforms:** Customer Platforms (Mobile App, Web App, POS App, CRM/Product Instrumentation, Design System); Banking Operations Platforms (Spend Mgmt, Value Added Services, Savings, Identity/Onboarding/Shell, Rewards).
  - **Credit Platforms:** TBD nodes.
  - **Other CoEs/Departments:** Banking & Ledger Platform ([[CBA]]), Sales Mgmt Tools (MonieCRM), Customer Support Platform (MonieDesk), Finance Systems, Field Verification Platform, Financial Crime Prevention ([[Loom]]), MOOS, Business Management Platform (Moniebook).
- Each Platforms & Products node is led by a product leader in partnership with an engineering leader and a design leader (triad model).
