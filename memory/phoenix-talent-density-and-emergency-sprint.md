## Summary

Netflix-inspired talent density principles adapted for Moniepoint, plus methodology for high-intensity execution sprints.

## Key Points

- Keeper test: would you fight to keep this person?
- Adequate performance gets generous severance (not long PIPs)
- Pay top of market for exceptional talent
- Freedom + responsibility enables high-talent-density teams
- Emergency sprints are exceptional (not default), time-boxed, with mandatory retrospectives
