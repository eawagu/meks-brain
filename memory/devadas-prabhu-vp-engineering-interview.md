## Summary

Assessment notes for [[Devadas Prabhu]], a Head of Engineering candidate initially considered at VP level for [[Moniepoint]]. Referred by [[Nikhil]] (Head of QA Engineering). Interviewed by [[Chris Purkis]] and [[Adegoke Obasa]]. Assessed as Director-level, not VP+ — failed on clarity of thought despite strong technical credentials in payments platform engineering at scale.

## Key Points

- **Profile:** Self-described "Platform Person" specializing in horizontal charters across Account, Card, and POS payment methods
- **Scale credentials:** Managed 1,200→5,000 TPS growth, maintained 99.99% uptime, led global expansion into Malaysia and Singapore
- **Key achievement:** Migrated legacy API monolith to global microservices architecture during 4x traffic growth
- **Tech philosophy:** Java veteran who led Golang migration (10x scalability, 30% resource reduction); advocates language-agnostic architecture
- **Team size:** 120+ people, 25 direct senior leaders
- **Regulatory knowledge:** Familiar with CBN, PIPG settlements, merchant underwriting in Nigerian context
- **Motivation:** Seeking remote-first culture — current company shifting back to 4-5 days in-office
- **Compensation:** ~1.7 CR CTC + 1 CR annual ESOP (monthly vesting), most equity already vested
- **Decision:** Director-level appropriate, not VP+ — failed on clarity of thought; chose wrong example initially for transaction processing question
