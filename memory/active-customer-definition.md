## Overview

Moniepoint's current definition of an "active customer" is any customer with at least **one outbound transaction**. This definition was flagged by [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] at the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]] Day 1 as inaccurate and must be fixed.

## Why It's Broken

- Underreports activity by excluding customers who perform other value-creating actions (savings deposits, card loads, account-to-account internal moves, etc.) without making an outbound payment.
- Inflates penetration-rate denominators incorrectly in some reports and under-reports in others.
- Distorts complaint-rate normalization (flagged explicitly for Savings complaints dashboard).

## Resolution

Key Decision: definition must be fixed. Specific replacement not yet specified in the meeting.
