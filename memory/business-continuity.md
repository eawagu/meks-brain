## Overview

Business Continuity at [[TeamApt]] is the management discipline within the [[Integrated Management System]] aligned to ISO 22301. It covers planning for disruption tolerance, recovery objectives ([[Business Impact Analysis|BIA]] RPO/RTO), and exercise/testing cadence.

## 2026-04-30 — ISO Audit Close-Out Next-Steps

The Apr 30 [[ISO Audit]] close-out session flagged Business Continuity touch-points in two of the 10 next-step remediation actions:

1. **Define objective review periods:** Define review period and frequency for [[Information Security]], Business Continuity, and [[Service Management System]] objectives.
2. **Update tabletop exercise template:** Update the tabletop exercise template with an actual date field.
3. **BIA familiarity:** Ensure process owners are familiar with [[Business Impact Analysis]] methodology.
