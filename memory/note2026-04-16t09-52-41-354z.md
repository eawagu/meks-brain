## Summary
Mek recorded that all technology functions currently under his [[TeamApt]] CTO role will be transferred to the [[Platform Organization]], with the exception of [[Operations]]. This is an organizational restructuring signal indicating a significant shift in the scope of his CTO accountability.

## Key Points
- All tech functions under the [[TeamApt]] CTO role are moving to the [[Platform Organization]]
- [[Operations]] is explicitly excluded from this move and remains under the CTO role
- This represents a narrowing (or redefinition) of the CTO's direct technology span at [[TeamApt]]

## Entities Mentioned
- [[TeamApt]] — the organization where Mek holds the CTO role
- [[Platform Organization]] — the organizational unit receiving the tech functions
- [[Operations]] — the function that remains under the CTO role
