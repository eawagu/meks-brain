## Current NPL Profile (April 2026)

- **Micro-loan/volume business**: NPL up from 0.3% → 0.6% as volume doubled. Threshold acceptable. Willing to tolerate 1–2% to sustain growth — still profitable.
- **Big-ticket book**: problematic given narrow margin (9% over treasury).

## Levers

- New [[Probability of Default Model]] (Yuki) launching May 2026 — backward-tested with significant predictive power.
- New [[Affordability Model]] (Yuki) replacing simplistic turnover+time model.
- Dedicated underwriters and new approval flow for large loans.
- Physical field-officer monitoring (critical performance driver vs. phone-based collection); field-officer capacity is the current bottleneck.
