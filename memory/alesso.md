## Context

**Alesso** is a borrower example cited by [[Moniepoint]] at the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]] (Day 1) in the context of [[Big-Ticket Lending Strategy]].

## The Example

Alesso pledged its bikes as security to [[FCMB]]. Any subsequent Moniepoint lending would place Moniepoint last in the recovery queue — reinforcing the point that collateral is not Moniepoint's primary mitigant, and that lending to pre-pledged borrowers compounds big-ticket risk.
