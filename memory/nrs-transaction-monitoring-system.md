## Overview

The Nigeria Revenue Service (NRS) Transaction Monitoring System is a regulatory monitoring platform that licensed PSSPs and Switches & Processing Operators are mandated to integrate with, per a [[CBN]] circular dated March 16, 2026.

## TeamApt status (week of Apr 24, 2026)

Per [[CEO Gazette - 24th April 26]]: the compliance status form provided by the regulator has been fully completed and reverted to [[CBN]] within the stipulated timeframe. See [[NRS TMS Integration]].
