## Definition

NSS Smart Det is the unified clearing file generated by [[TACHA]] that aggregates clearing data from all [[TeamApt]] platforms for submission to [[NIBSS]] interbank settlement. It is the mechanism by which TeamApt's domestic switching transactions are settled through the national payment infrastructure.

## Current State

- Generated by TACHA from netting results across all 5 platform consumers
- NSS-ready banks (as of Feb 2026): [[Polaris Bank]] and [[Wema Bank]]
- Manual upload still required (identified as Medium-priority Phoenix alignment gap, owner: [[Kevin Ng'Eno]])
- Only 2 of 10 targeted NSS banks operational as of 2025 year-end

## Centralization Plan

[[Juliana Account Switch]] currently generates its own Smart Det files separately from TACHA. Planned centralization: Account Switch will submit DR/CR entries to TACHA, decommissioning separate Smart Det generation. This will unify all interbank settlement through a single TACHA pipeline.

## Settlement Agent Mapping

NSS is one of 9 settlement agents in TACHA. Banks that are NSS-ready settle via the NIBSS NSS agent; banks that are not NSS-ready settle via bank-specific disbursement APIs (Polaris, Keystone, Union, etc.).
