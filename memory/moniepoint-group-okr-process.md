## Definition

Moniepoint Group OKR Process is the group-level OKR governance framework owned by [[Moniepoint|Moniepoint Inc.]]. Subsidiaries including [[TeamApt|TeamApt Limited]] inherit and apply this process; subsidiary-level OKR documents are scoped applications of the group framework, not independent frameworks.

See parent concept: [[OKR Process]] (generic management framework) — pending reactive creation per Rule 11 when bare-wikilink usage justifies.

## Structure

Two-level OKR system:

- **Annual Business Department OKRs** — owned by business departments (and pillar divisions). Set at the start of the financial year.
- **Quarterly Product Engineering Team OKRs** — owned by individual product engineering teams. Set per quarter with structured prior-quarter review.

## Scoring Convention

| Status | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Achieved | ≥90% |
| Partially Achieved | 50–89% |
| Not Achieved | <50% |
| Dropped/Cancelled | — |

## Cadence

- **Annual OKRs** — set at start of financial year for business departments and pillar divisions.
- **Quarterly OKRs** — set per product engineering team. Each quarter's cycle includes: Q-end recap, lessons learned, KR performance review, delivery recap, and upcoming-quarter proposals.

## Alignment Model

- Team quarterly OKRs reference parent annual OKRs (team → department linkage).
- Pillar division standards embed in every product team's OKRs (Engineering, SRE, Compliance, etc. as cross-cutting standards).
- Jira integration for tracking.

## Scope

Covers: business departments, product engineering teams, pillar divisions across Moniepoint Inc. entities.

## TeamApt Inheritance (2026 snapshot)

TeamApt, as a Moniepoint Inc. subsidiary, applies the group OKR process to its business departments and product engineering teams. 2026 submission status at TeamApt as of Apr 2026:

**Available:** Direct Debit (Business + Product), Monnify (Business + 3 Product teams), TPP (Business + 2 Product teams), Juliana Switch, PTSP/PTAD, Regulatory, PMO, Design, Engineering, Infrastructure, SRE, DevRel, Legal, Marketing, People Team.

**Missing:** Domestic Switching (annual), MADD, GoSubscribe, SAFE, VAS, Issuer Processing, Acquirer Processing, KYC, Transaction Monitoring, Fraud Desk, Operations, Data, Security, Project Delivery.
