Regulatory Compliance is the industry discipline of meeting legal and regulatory obligations imposed by government bodies, industry regulators, and standards organizations. In financial services, includes central bank requirements, data protection laws, anti-money-laundering regulations, know-your-customer rules, and licensing regimes.

## Common Domains

- **Licensing compliance** — operating under authorized license conditions
- **Reporting obligations** — AFS (Audited Financial Statements), regulatory returns
- **Data sovereignty** — data residency requirements (NDPR, GDPR, etc.)
- **AML/KYC** — customer due diligence, transaction monitoring, suspicious activity reporting
- **Financial reporting** — ICOFA (Internal Control over Financial Reporting, e.g., SOX Section 404)
