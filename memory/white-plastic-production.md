## Definition

**White plastic production** is the physical card-manufacturing step in the issuer processing chain — production of the unpersonalised plastic card body that subsequently receives EMV personalisation data and visual print. Sits between [[EMV Data Prep]] (cryptographic preparation) and the personalisation vendor in the Card Production chain.

## ATPP track status

### 2026-04-27 standup

- Two card data generated; awaiting EMV data prep output for white plastic production.
- Cards dispatched to Singapore for [[Visa]] global testing (separate flow, on already-produced cards).
- Document standardization is incorporating white plastic production updates.
