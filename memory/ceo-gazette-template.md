## Summary
The blank master template from which all weekly CEO Gazettes derive. Defines the canonical 11-slide weekly report structure used throughout 2026 by the [[CEO Gazette]] workflow.

## Key Points
- **Slide 2 — Executive Summary** (3-sentence TL;DR): Top Win, Top Risk, North Star Metric (e.g., Total Processed Volume or Active Users).
- **Slide 3 — Customer Experience:** CSAT from Zendesk, issues encountered, resolution time.
- **Slide 4 — Customer Acquisition:** Total Sign-ups, KYC Rejected, Activations, Conversion Rate, Avg Approval Time (Wks), Time-to-initiate-first-transaction, Non-Compliant Merchants.
- **Slide 5 — Stability:** SRE data.
- **Slide 6 — Integration experience:** DevOps figures.
- **Slide 7 — Compliance:** Gap inventory per product (ATS 2, DD 16, Juliana 15, Monnify 46, NBA 21, PTSP 26).
- **Slide 8 — People.**
- **Slide 9 — Operational Efficiency.**
- **Slide 10 — A closer look at the numbers:** TPV, Total Revenue, System Uptime (last week / this week / change).
- **Slide 11 — ASKs.**
