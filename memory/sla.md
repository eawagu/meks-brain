## Definition

**SLA** (Service Level Agreement) is the formal contractual commitment between [[TeamApt]] / Moniepoint and a bank partner specifying response times, availability targets, and processing terms. SLA sign-off is one of the recurring blockers on the [[Direct to Bank program]] pipeline.

## SLA sign-off pipeline (Apr 2026 snapshot)

- **[[UBA]]** — SLA **signed Apr 15, 2026**; legal team follow-up underway as of Apr 28; server deployment commencing next week. Source: [[Direct to Bank Daily standup - 2026-04-28 08:27 WAT]].
- **[[WHMA Bank]]** — sign-off pending.
- **[[Steel Bank]]** — sign-off pending.
- **[[Coral Pay]] / [[Access Bank]]** — sign-off pending.
- **[[Polaris Bank]]** — sign-off pending; "extend until next week" pattern rolled ≥3 times.
