## Definition

**Dispute Management** refers to the chargeback / dispute lifecycle handling required by the card schemes ([[Visa]] and [[Mastercard]]). Within [[TeamApt]]'s [[ATPP]] vertical it covers both the scheme certification chain (Mastercard + Visa dispute management certification commencing in parallel per the Mar 31 MANCo) and the active engineering sprint to deliver the dispute management capability.
