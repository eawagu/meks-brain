Operations is the function at [[TeamApt]] that is explicitly excluded from the tech function migration to the [[Platform Organization]]. As of 2026-04-16, it remains directly under Mek's CTO role.

This makes Operations the primary direct accountability retained by the CTO after the [[Organizational Restructuring]] moves all other tech functions to the platform org.
