## Overview

The **Card Sales & Distribution Team** (CTS1) is a 13-member [[Moniepoint MFB]] team that owns the platform enabling card distribution across the partner network, the card sales experience for personal and business users, and card lifecycle management operations (activation, delete, reset PIN, block, unblock).

Distinct from the [[Team 1 Card Processing]] and [[Team 2 Card Issuance]] engineering teams within [[Project Phoenix]] / [[Card Issuance & Processing Platform]] — CTS1 sits on the sales/distribution side of the card value chain rather than the issuance/processing side.

Notable structural detail: 4 SRE against 5 product engineers — unusually SRE-heavy ratio for a product team, implying material operational load on partner-network / distribution infrastructure.

## Responsibilities

- Platform that enables card distribution across partner network
- Card sales experience (personal + business users)
- Card activation flow
- Card lifecycle management: delete, reset PIN, block, unblock
