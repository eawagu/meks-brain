## Overview
An [[Integrated Management System]] (IMS) is a single unified management system that combines multiple management standards — commonly ISO 9001 (Quality), ISO 27001 (Information Security), ISO 22301 (Business Continuity), ISO 20000 (IT Service Management), ISO 45001 (OHS) — so that shared processes, documentation, audits, and management reviews cover all standards simultaneously.

## TeamApt IMS
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], [[TeamApt]]'s IMS covers [[ISO 20000]], [[ISO 22301]], [[ISO 27001]], [[ISO 45001]], and [[ISO 9001]]. Key facts:

- **Consulting partner:** [[OPEX]]
