## Preferred Path

Controlled **4-year write-off** of the MP35 NBV, contingent on regulatory relief from competitor demarketing (notably [[OPay]] claims on 3G/Android 5 legality).

## Financial Picture

- 4-year depreciation schedule means recent buys still carry high NBV — immediate write-off would realize tens of millions in losses.
- Worst-case impact ~$30M, potentially absorbable over time via storage/write-back mechanics.
- MP35P alternatives: immediate write-off, 4G upgrade investment, or free-give to drive commissions.

## Fallback

If regulatory relief fails, a larger immediate decision is required. Even free MP35P is often refused by smaller merchants, limiting the free-give option's reach.
