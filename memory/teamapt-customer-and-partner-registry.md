## Summary

Comprehensive registry of 50+ entities with relationships across all business lines.

## Key Points

- 14 Tier-1 banks: Access (all 4 businesses), Polaris (settlement primary), Fidelity (ACT client), FCMB, UBA, Wema, Union, Keystone, Stanbic, Ecobank, Sterling, First Bank, Zenith, GTB
- 9 payment facilitators: CowryWise (first live DD), GlobalPay, LendSqr, Paystack, Cyberpay, HabariPay, Kora Pay, Quidax, Mono
- Switch-to-switch: CoralPay (4 issuers), HabariPay (GTB routing)
- Schemes: Visa (certified), Mastercard (certified), Verve (domestic), Afrigo (domestic)
- PTSAs: NIBSS (live), UPSL (pending — CBN dual connectivity mandate)
