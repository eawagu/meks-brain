## Overview

Corporate Registration is the statutory legal process in Nigeria by which a company is formed and registered with the [[Corporate Affairs Commission]] (CAC) under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA). Establishes legal personality, share structure, registered office, and objects of the company.

## Required Documents (Nigeria)

- **CAC Form 1.1** — Application for Registration (names, addresses, subscribers, directors, share capital)
- **Memorandum of Association** — company objects, share capital structure
- **Articles of Association** — internal governance rules
- **Certificate of Incorporation** — issued by CAC upon successful registration
