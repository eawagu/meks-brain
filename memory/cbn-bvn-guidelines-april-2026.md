## Overview

[[CBN]] BVN guidelines update referenced at the Mar 31, 2026 MANCo, governing BVN enrollment and post-activation device-binding controls.

## Key requirements

- **BVN enrollment restricted to 18+** (minors excluded).
- **One phone-number amendment per BVN** (single change cap).
- **Liveness checks** required for online account opening.
- **Mobile apps bound to one device.**
- **₦20,000 Naira outflow cap** in the first 24 hours on a newly activated device.
