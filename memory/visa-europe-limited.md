## Overview
Visa Europe Limited is Visa Inc.'s European operating entity and host of the [[Visa Payments Forum Paris]] industry conference. On 17 April 2026 it issued a formal invitation letter to [[Emeka Awagu]] in support of his [[French Schengen visa application]] for business travel to Paris on 30 June – 2 July 2026.

## Relationships
- Parent brand: Visa Inc.
- European country organisation: [[Visa France]] (signatory of the Apr 17 2026 letter was [[Florence Mélique]], Group Country Manager, Visa France).
- Hosts: [[Visa Payments Forum Paris]].
