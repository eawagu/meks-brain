## Overview

Daniel Ojinaka is the department lead for DD/CDD (Direct Debit / Consolidated Direct Debit) at [[TeamApt]]. Under [[Project Phoenix]] (Apr 22, 2026 [[TeamApt-Platformization-Org-Movements (1)|Org Movements brief]]) his scope broadens into a **dual business lead** role: **Monnify + Direct Debit**.
