## Overview
The Embassy of France / Consulate General, Visa Issuance Division, is the French diplomatic body that processes French and Schengen visa applications. For [[Emeka Awagu]]'s [[French Schengen visa application]] in support of attending [[Visa Payments Forum Paris]], [[Visa Europe Limited]] addressed an invitation letter to this division on 17 April 2026.
