## Definition

Domestic Switching is TeamApt's Enabler business line for routing domestic payment transactions. Led by [[Babatunde Okufi]] (CBDO, Head BD) pre-Phoenix. Holds the CBN Switching & Processing License.

## Core Systems

- [[Juliana Switch]] — Card Switch (POS, ATM; 37% Moniepoint traffic, 16 issuers) + Account Switch (99.89% success, ₦5M+ quarterly target)
- Feeds [[TACHA]] for clearing/settlement
- TSP migration in progress (TSP as Layer 2 orchestration, Juliana as Layer 1 switching)

## Revenue Model

- Enabler tier (not primary revenue driver); T-Switch market share 57% inter-bank (Apr 14)
- 2026 focus: TSP migration positioning; Frank Atashili owns via Product Division OKRs (pre-Phoenix) → transitions to TSPP Product Head under Phoenix

## Key Metrics

- T-Switch 57% inter-bank share
- TeamApt cards 35.68%
- 16 issuers connected
- 37% Moniepoint POS traffic
- Account Switch 99.89% success rate

## Under Project Phoenix (Apr 22, 2026 brief)

DS **merges with [[Third Party Processing]]** into a single consolidated OpCo business line: **Switching & Processing (S&P)** under [[Babatunde Okufi]] (CBDO double-hat). S&P scope retains: Acquirer/Issuer processing customers, PTSP/PTAD, ATS client relationships, Juliana Switch commercial, ACT.

[[Kevin Ng'Eno]] (Lead, Switching Solutions) and [[Abdulgafar Obeitor]] (Lead, AptPay Suite) stay with the S&P business in Phase 1 and join [[TSPP]] when Switching Solutions and AptPay Suite absorb in later phases.
