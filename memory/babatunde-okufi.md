## Overview

Babatunde Okufi is **Chief Business Development Officer (CBDO) at [[TeamApt]]**, reporting to [[Dennis Ajalie]] (CEO, TeamApt). Under [[Project Phoenix]] (Apr 22, 2026 Org Movements brief) he takes a **double-hat**: CBDO + Business Lead of the merged **Switching & Processing (S&P)** OpCo business — the consolidation of [[Third Party Processing]] (TPP) and [[Domestic Switching]] (DS) into one Enabler business line. Owns CoralPay remediation actions and NIBSS engagement.
