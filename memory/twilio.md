Twilio is a cloud communications platform (CPaaS) providing programmable messaging, voice, and chat APIs (including WhatsApp Business API integration).

## DDMFB 2026 IT Strategy Reference
Per [[DDMFB_2026_IT_Strategy_Review]], Twilio (alongside [[Infobip]]) is cited as an example messaging infrastructure whose fees alone — for a bank with meaningful transaction volume — would consume a large chunk of [[DavoDani Microfinance Bank]]'s ₦5M annual [[Customer Experience Platform]] budget. The reviewer flagged the budget as unrealistic for the proposed omnichannel scope (WhatsApp Business API, web chat, email, social inbox, IVR, auto-calls, CRM ticketing, sentiment analysis).
