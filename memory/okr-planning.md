OKR Planning is the process of defining and cascading Objectives and Key Results across an organization. Generic management discipline originated at Intel and popularized by Google. Typically done on quarterly or annual cadence.

## Core Elements

- **Objectives** — qualitative, inspirational, time-bound goals
- **Key Results** — quantitative measures of success for each objective
- **Cascading** — alignment from company → department → team → individual
- **Scoring** — typical convention: ≥70% achievement is on-target; 100% is sandbagged
