## Summary

Comprehensive 2024 business context for [[TeamApt]] — subsidiary of [[Moniepoint]] Inc. Covers the product portfolio (DCIR, ATS, Direct Debit, CDD, Consolidated Switch), organizational structure, revenue targets ($510K→$1M quarterly), strategic initiatives, and year-end performance results across all product lines.

## Key Points

- CEO: [[Dennis Ajalie]]; Group CEO: Tosin Eniolorunda ([[Moniepoint]])
- 2024 revenue baseline: $510K quarterly → $1M target (at ₦1,500/USD = ~₦6B annualized)
- 5 strategic themes: Make Money, Grow the Business, Protect the Business, Support MoniePoint Group, New Business
- **DCIR:** 7 banks live; targeted 12; Q1 result: 0 new banks (bank expansion was biggest bottleneck)
- **Direct Debit:** 0→1 bank live by mid-year (target was 7); extremely slow progress
- **Consolidated Switch** (later Juliana): best-performing new product — 10 issuers (exceeded 7 target), 2 domestic processors (ISW, UP)
- **TPP certifications:** Afrigo ✅, Verve ✅, NIBSS acquirer processing ✅ (Q2); Mastercard/Visa targeted H2
- Sprint velocity was a systemic issue: DCIR 72.3% (vs 90%), DD 50.5% (vs 100%), Switch 75% (vs 100%)
- Reliability improved: transaction success rate 70%→96.3%, incident response 240→60 min
- Feature development time reduced: 4 weeks → 2 weeks 3 days
- 2024 was foundational: most new products in build/early deployment, revenue largely from DCIR
- Monnify did NOT appear as a separate TeamApt OKR track in 2024
