## Summary

Strategic roadmap for international expansion using "one platform, many markets" vision.

## Key Points

- Nigeria is revenue engine and proving ground
- UK targets diaspora remittances and business banking
- Kenya focuses on mobile money integration (M-PESA)
- Platform capabilities required: multi-currency, compliance engine, cross-border routing, market-specific payment methods
- Expansion principles: platform-first, configuration over code, compliance by design
