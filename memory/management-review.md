## Overview
A [[Management Review]] is a periodic review by top management of a management system's suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness, required by ISO management system standards (e.g., clause 9.3).

## TeamApt Practice
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]] itself:
- **Frequency:** annual, captured in Control on management review
- **Scope (Apr 2026):** covers all five IMS standards — [[ISO 20000]], [[ISO 22301]], [[ISO 27001]], [[ISO 45001]], [[ISO 9001]]
- **Standard agenda items:** external/internal issues, needs/expectations of interested parties, legal requirements, risks/opportunities, operational usage, documented information, training, IMS discussion (audits, CAPA, policies/objectives/KPIs, external providers, infosec best practice & incident response, BC strategies & emergency response, QMS, risks/opportunities, awareness/communication, training/development, nonconformities, service reporting)
- **Outputs:** action items, closing remarks
- **Apr 2026 action items:** close NCs/observations by 13–16/04/2026 ahead of surveillance audit; conduct 2026 [[Surveillance Audit]] on 20/04/2026
