## Role

**Chloe** is [[Moniepoint]]'s talent acquisition leader. Given an explicit mandate at the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]] (Day 1) by [[Tosin Eniolorunda]].

## Mandate (April 2026)

Build a "fantastic talent organization" that can source high-quality talent to upscale the organization — without sacrificing talent density. This is framed as a structural dependency: the [[Talent Density Principle]] (remove prolonged underperformers quickly) only works if replacement supply is strong.
