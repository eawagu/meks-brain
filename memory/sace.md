## Overview

SACE is Moniepoint's combined Sales, Acquiring, and Customer Experience function. At the 2026 UK Retreat Day 2, the SACE OKR was co-presented by [[Ezekiel]] and [[Ifine]].

## 2026 OKR Objectives

1. **BRM Relationship as Retention Driver** — increase NPS promoters citing BRM helpfulness (3%→7%), decrease BRM-caused detractors, raise BRM-related C-SAT.
2. **Sustainable Market Leadership** — 4.36M MAU via BRMs, 3.9M active POS, 70-pt POS market-share gap, ₦16.8B payment volume, 92% top-BIO retention, ₦864B GLPV (3.77% lending penetration), 65,773 [[Moneybook]] subscribers (9.17% penetration).
3. **Revenue Growth with Disciplined Unit Economics** — Payment ₦449.91B, Lending ₦311B, POS Hardware ₦14.96B, Savings ₦127.8B, Moneybook ₦4.98B; 12-month POS payback.
4. **Risk, Compliance, Operational Resilience** — metrics pending (geo-fencing, agent limits, risk factors) in partnership with GONAR and [[Sunday]].

## Team Structure

- Execution: BRMs.
- Driver: Cluster Managers.
- Ownership: State Coordinators, Regional Managers.
- Supporting: Key Account Management (KAM) team.
