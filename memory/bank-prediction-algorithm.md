## Overview

The Bank Prediction Algorithm is a Moniepoint-internal service that predicts which bank a destination account number belongs to, so the customer does not need to select the bank manually when initiating a transfer.

## UX Impact

Removes a step from the transfer flow, reducing input errors and improving transfer success rate. Contributes to Q1 2026 transfer success rates of 97-98%.
