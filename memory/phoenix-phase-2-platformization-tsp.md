## Definition

**Phoenix Phase 2 — Platformization (TSP)** is the second phase of [[Project Phoenix]], focused on **Transaction Switching and Processing** platformization. Led by [[Frank|Frank Atashili]] (Product) and [[Alex|Alex Adeyemo]] (Tech).

## Cards KT — Apr 21 framing

"The platformization plan, which is currently in its second phase, focuses on Transaction Switching and Processing (TSP), led by Frank and Alex. A **strike team** will be formed, consisting of people from teams expected to join the card team, with the primary goal of building the new Card Management Service (CMS)."

The new CMS is intended to absorb the functions of the current internal CMS and eventually production aspects from card distribution.

## Strategic linkage with Cards

Phase 2 (TSP) and the Cards strike team are organizationally adjacent — the Cards strike team draws ICs/EMs who currently report into Phase 2 work led by Frank and Alex. This is the operational bridge between the platformization phases.
