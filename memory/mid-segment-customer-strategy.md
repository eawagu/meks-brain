## Strategic Context

Mid-segment is the largest unaddressed value-growth lever in the [[Loan Portfolio Strategy]]. The 5–15M band is already "absolutely in love with" performance; the 50–200M band is too small relative to potential. Large customers (200M+) are retained-at-all-costs, small-ticket is strong — mid is the gap.

## Hypothesis

Product priced too expensively for mid-segment customers; segment is shrinking because of pricing, not product-market fit.

## Experiment

Ongoing price-drop campaign for mid-sized customers. Action item pending: analyze elasticity + effect on acquisition/uptake.
