## Summary

Critical incident analysis covering Q1 2026 (Feb 2–Mar 30). 294 incidents with 3× escalation from 28 to 84 per week. 78.6% classified as P1 critical.

## Key Points

- Switch (Juliana Card) and ATS account for 89.1% of incidents
- RC91 (Issuer/Switch Inoperative) dominates at 60.2% of all incidents
- CoralPay responsible for 24.8% (73 total); VPN instability chronic
- First Bank (41), Providus Bank (38), Access Bank (34) most affected
- NIBSS infrastructure instability: 26 incidents affecting multiple products
- SRE reporter concentration: Qazim Adedigba (40.1%), Olamide Ajibulu (21.4%)
