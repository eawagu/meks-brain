## Overview

Ibukun Atoyebi is **Chief Compliance Officer** at [[TeamApt]]. Named as a key relationship in [[Emeka Awagu]]'s CTO accountability framework — compliance intersection.

Under [[Project Phoenix]] (per the Apr 22, 2026 [[TeamApt-Platformization-Org-Movements (1)|Org Movements brief]]) the **Compliance function is explicitly retained in the TeamApt OpCo** alongside audit, finance, and legal — not affected by the platformization movements.
