Microsoft Dynamics NAV (rebranded as Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central) is a Microsoft ERP product targeting small and mid-sized businesses for financials, supply chain, and operations.

## DDMFB 2026 IT Strategy Reference
Per [[DDMFB_2026_IT_Strategy_Review]], Microsoft Nav appeared on [[DavoDani Microfinance Bank]]'s candidate list for the ₦100M [[Core Banking System]] selection. The reviewer flagged this as a category error — NAV is an ERP, not a core banking system — and treated its inclusion as evidence that the CBS [[Vendor Evaluation & RFP]] criteria were not well-defined.
