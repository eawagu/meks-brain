## Overview

Information Security at [[TeamApt]] is the management discipline within the [[Integrated Management System]] aligned to ISO 27001 (Information Security Management System / ISMS). It covers data protection, access controls, incident response, and supplier security obligations.

## 2026-04-30 — ISO Audit Close-Out Next-Step

The Apr 30 [[ISO Audit]] close-out session flagged Information Security objectives as part of one of the 10 next-step remediation actions:

- **Define objective review periods:** Define review period and frequency for Information Security, [[Business Continuity]], and [[Service Management System]] objectives.

Source: [[ISO Audit-Close out session – 2026_04_30 16_27 WAT – Notes by Gemini]].

## Apr 2026 Internal Audit — ISMS Findings (Selected)

- **A5.20 OFI:** Security requirements (including audit rights) not consistently included in all SLAs with [[OPEX]] and [[Cybervergent]].
