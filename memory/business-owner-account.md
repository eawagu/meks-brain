## Overview

The Business Owner (BO) account is a legacy Moniepoint account type into which [[SAYT]] auto-withdrew funds at the end of a period. Customers unaware of this account type triggered the persistent "Where is my money?" support complaint.

## Decommissioning (Day 1 Retreat 2026)

- **New SAYT customers** are no longer exposed to the BO account.
- **Legacy users** remain on the BO path to avoid disruption; migration planned via customer education + guided withdrawal or portal setup.
- Product/Comms team action item: educate legacy SAYT users; guide withdraw or new-portal setup.
