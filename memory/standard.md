## Overview

Standard is a Nigerian bank competitor analyzed during Moniepoint's 2026 Day 3 retreat as a peer whose growth model and revenue structure are materially different.

## Profile

- CIB (Corporate and Investment Banking) segment: 60-70% of revenue and profitability
- Treasury unit: 17.8B — primary driver of business
- #1 in asset management, stockbroking, pensions
- Top player in FX
- 37% growth rate cited
- Growth partially boosted by FX and Treasury

## Moniepoint Implication

Standard's growth model not directly replicable for Moniepoint given different business structure (retail + SME focus vs CIB).
