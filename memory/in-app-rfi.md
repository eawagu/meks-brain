## Overview

In-App RFI (Request for Information) is a Moniepoint in-app feature that allows the company to request quick feedback from users regarding specific transactions.

## Impact

- P90 resolution time improved from target of 24 hours to current 16 hours
- Most impactful change in the shift from fragmented complaint channels (WhatsApp, direct phone) to centralized handling
- Supporting context for 90% CSAT goal
