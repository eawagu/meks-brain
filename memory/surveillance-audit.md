## Overview
A [[Surveillance Audit]] is a periodic third-party audit conducted between initial ISO certification and recertification (typically annually) to verify that the certified organisation continues to meet the standard's requirements.

## TeamApt 2026 Surveillance Audit
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]]:
- **Date:** 20 April 2026 (day after the management review)
- **Scope:** all five IMS standards — [[ISO 20000]], [[ISO 22301]], [[ISO 27001]], [[ISO 45001]], [[ISO 9001]]
- **Responsible:** [[TeamApt]]
- **Preparation action (closed 16/04/2026):** close identified NCs and observations that could lead to NCs during the external audit ([[OPEX]], TeamApt jointly responsible)
- **Status in action register:** Done — but the audit itself executes on 20/04/2026
