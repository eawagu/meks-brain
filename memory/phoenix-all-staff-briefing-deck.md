## Summary

Strategic reorganization briefing from product-centric to platform-centric model. Five core platforms: TSP, Card Issuance, Card Acceptance, Payment Gateway (Monnify), Identity. OpCos retain licenses, operations, compliance, sales, P&L. Platform Companies own IP, engineering, product management, design.

## Key Points

- Before: Teams built around individual products with full tech ownership and duplication
- After: Teams organized around shared platforms; build once, configure per country
- Non-negotiable: TSP handles all fund movement; callers never build postings
- Timeline: Gradual transition Q1-Q3 2026; current OKRs remain in force
- Migration: Strangler fig with feature flags; no big bang, instant rollback per transaction type
