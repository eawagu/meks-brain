An omnichannel Customer Experience platform integrates customer-facing channels (web chat, WhatsApp Business API, email, social inbox, IVR, voice automation), CRM ticketing, sentiment analysis, and operational tooling (escalation workflows, SLAs).

## Implementation Requirements (Per DDMFB Review)
Per [[DDMFB_2026_IT_Strategy_Review]], a CX platform deployment is not just a tooling purchase — it requires:
- Trained agents
- Defined escalation workflows
- SLAs
- Operations owner
- Regulatory consideration ([[CBN]] has guidelines on automated financial communications)
- Realistic per-message-fee budgeting (CPaaS providers like [[Twilio]] and [[Infobip]] charge per-interaction)

## Phasing Recommendation
Phase 1: WhatsApp Business API + basic chatbot + CRM ticketing.
Phase 2: voice automation and sentiment analysis.

## DDMFB 2026 — Application of Concept
[[DavoDani Microfinance Bank]] budgeted ₦5M annually for a full omnichannel CX platform — assessed as severely underestimated; realistic budget would be ₦8–12M. The strategy made no mention of staffing or regulatory considerations, and reused the 'AI-powered chatbot' line item across the website and CX sections without clarifying which budget covers it.
