## Summary
Detailed reference guide for Moniepoint's performance appraisal writing process, including rating scale, SBI framework, structured comment format, SMART expectations, and worked examples.

## Key Points
- Rating scale: 1.0 (below expectations) to 6.0 (exceptional) with detailed descriptors
- SBI (Situation-Behavior-Impact) framework for structured feedback
- Comment structure: context, observed behavior, impact, development recommendations
- Growth areas guidance: focus on specific, actionable development objectives
- SMART expectations framework (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
- Pre-submission checklist for quality assurance
- Worked examples: Product Lead, Head of Business, PMO Lead roles
