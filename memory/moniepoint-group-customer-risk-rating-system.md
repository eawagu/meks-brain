## Overview

The Moniepoint Group Customer Risk Rating System assigns every [[Moniepoint|Moniepoint Inc.]] customer a risk rating based on a formula including:

- Foreign interactions
- Location
- Business type (e.g., high-risk categories)

High-risk customers receive Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) to determine appropriate controls.

## BRM Application

- System also applied to Business Relationship Managers (BRMs)
- Planned disincentive structure for BRMs to ensure responsible onboarding and accountability for fraudulent onboarded customers (in progress)

## Purpose

Manage costs by controlling risk — target controls where risk is concentrated rather than uniformly.
