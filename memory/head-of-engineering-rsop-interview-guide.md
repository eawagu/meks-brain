## Summary

Standardized recruitment SOP for Head of Engineering hires at Moniepoint, authored by Chris Purkis. Establishes a 5-round interview process (recruiter prescreen → Felix → VP panel → BL/Tosin → workshop), evaluation criteria for hard/soft skills, and post-interview deliberation and offer workflows. Treats VPs of Engineering as business unit CTOs. Includes open role descriptions for Account Payments, Card Payments, POS Channel, Credit & Loans, HRIS/MOOS, and Sales Management Tools.

## Key Points

- 5-round process: Prescreen (30min) → Felix culture+depth (45min) → VP Panel technical (60min) → BL+Tosin bar-raising (45min each) → Workshop (2hr in-person preferred, London)
- VPs of Engineering viewed as "CTOs of business units" — must demonstrate cross-functional depth (mobile, web, FE, BE, infra, DevOps, incident mgmt, monitoring)
- Mandatory hard skills: systems architecture at scale, reliability/observability, security/compliance by design, technical decision-making, fintech domain expertise
- Mandatory soft skills: ownership, high agency, empathy for business/customers, GRIT, clarity of thought
- Geographic policy: core countries Nigeria/India/UK; US excluded; Canada excluded except exceptional referrals
- Java hands-on experience is a must for all HoE candidates
- VP Panel interviewers paired: Chidum/Toba, Alex/Emeka, Goke/John, Pavan/Tosin A.
- Open roles: Account Payments (24 people), Card Payments (35 engineers), POS Channel (17), Credit & Loans (greenfield, 4+), HRIS/MOOS (~20), Sales Management (30-35)
- Workshop tests real unresolved challenges from the business unit — no pre-work
- Deliberation includes all interviewers; compensation prep requires structured spreadsheet with current comp, expectations, band mapping, parity risks
