## Overview

The performance-based compensation model for Moniepoint's D&X (sales) organization, effective 2026.

## Structure by Role

### BRMs
- Default commission: 13.5%.
- 9% guaranteed.
- 4.5% credited to a "performance wallet" — unlocked monthly on achieving:
    - Loan volume
    - Loan value
    - TA
    - TBR
    - Moneybook
    - Savings

### Cluster Managers
- Personal 4.5% performance pay tied to the cluster's overall performance.
- Additional bonus up to ₦400,000 based on cluster results.
- Also act as BRMs in their own right.

### State Coordinators
- 70% guaranteed / 30% performance-based.
- Variable pay unlocks at month-end based on YTD performance.

### Regional Managers
- 60% guaranteed / 40% performance-based.
- Variable pay unlocks at month-end based on YTD performance.

## New Product Owner Roles

- SLOs, SMOs, SSOs introduced at state level.
- Targets align with State Coordinator.
- Bonus tied to state team YTD performance.

## Paired Controls

- [[Rating 16 System]]: BRM→Cluster→State→Regional upward-rating accountability.
- Quarterly BRM reactivation review (unproductive BRMs >12 months managing <30 businesses deactivated).
