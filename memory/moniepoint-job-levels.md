## Summary

Moniepoint's job leveling framework spans 13 discrete levels across two tracks: individual contributors (IC) and managers. IC levels run from 010 Intern through 071 Expert; management levels run from 050 Manager through 200 Senior Vice President. Each level has a clear description of autonomy, scope, and leadership expectations, making it a calibration tool for hiring, promotion, and role scoping.

## Key Points

- 13 levels total, split across IC and management tracks with some level numbers shared (e.g., 050 covers both Principal and Manager)
- IC track: 010 Intern → 020 Junior Associate → 030 Associate → 040 Senior Associate → 051 Principal → 061 Senior Principal → 071 Expert
- Management track: 050 Manager → 060 Senior Manager → 070 Director → 080 Senior Director → 100 Vice President → 200 Senior Vice President
- Autonomy gradient: Intern works under close direction; SVP operates with minimal supervision and partners with executive leadership on company strategy
- Expert (071): world-class SME, thought leader inventing high-impact methodologies — highest IC level
- VP (100): sets strategic vision, designs organization/systems/processes, responsible for department strategic objectives within their domain
- SVP (200): holistically responsible for department business outcomes, partners with executive leadership on company strategy
