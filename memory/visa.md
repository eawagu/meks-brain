## Overview

Visa is a global card payment network. It is currently the **most strategically loaded card scheme** in the Moniepoint group across both issuing and acquiring.

## Issuing (Moniepoint MFB Cards)

- **Visa launch & tap-to-pay rollout** is the most critical 2026 deliverable for the [[Moniepoint MFB]] Cards Team
- Commercial payment for the Visa programme is the **primary dependency** for go-live
- Once live: NFC tap-to-pay (via [[Sudo Africa]] [[Pseudo-Digitisation]]) deploys broadly across the Android user base on OP/PE terminals
- Visa is also the **launch workload for the new [[Card Management System]]** being built by the strike team under [[Project Phoenix]] — validating the platform in production
- Full Visa tokenization (via VTS) planned post-launch (Q4 2026), potentially replacing pseudo-digitisation

## Acquiring (TeamApt TPP)

- Visa + Mastercard acquirer processor certifications **completed (2025)** per [[Tracy Ojaigho]]
- Visa acquiring license: **✅ obtained**
- TeamApt also pursues collections-only Visa implementation and a Visa processing agreement
- Long-term acquiring strategy: route MoniePoint Visa card transactions through [[TeamApt]] (via TMSS settlement switch) rather than UP / [[Interswitch]] — negotiating with UBA, Access
