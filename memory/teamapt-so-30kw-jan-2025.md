## Summary

This is a signed sales order from [[Rack Centre]] to [[TeamApt Limited]] (sales order RC00410, dated 20 January 2025) under Master Services Agreement MSA001810. It provisions 2 rack cabinets with 30KW of committed power and 1 AWS Direct Connect cross-connection, all commencing 1 April 2025. The contract is billed quarterly in advance in NGN.

## Key Points

- **Cabinets:** 2 units at NGN 902,400/unit/month → NGN 1,804,800/month (NGN 21,657,600/year). Installation: NGN 2,400,000.
- **Committed Power (PAL-KW):** 30KW at NGN 726,400/KW/month → NGN 21,792,000/month (NGN 261,504,000/year). Installation: NGN 2,400,000.
- **Total rack + power:** NGN 23,596,800/month (NGN 283,161,600/year).
- **AWS Direct Connect:** 1 cross-connect at NGN 160,000/month (NGN 1,920,000/year). Installation: NGN 800,000.
- **Start date:** 1 April 2025 for all services.
- **Payment terms:** Quarterly in advance; invoiced 30 business days prior; payment due 30 business days from invoice receipt.
- **Annual escalation:** Charges subject to annual escalation on 1 January, guided by inflation indices (RPI, CPI) and legislative changes.
- **Remote hands:** 2 hours/month included during business hours (8:30am–5:30pm Mon–Fri); overtime at NGN 75,000/hr standard, NGN 150,000/hr non-standard.
- **Power config:** Single-phase; 3-phase requires a billable tap-off.
- **Signatories:** [[Dennis Ajalie]] (Managing Director, TeamApt) signed 10 Feb 2025; [[Felix Ike]] (Director, Rack Centre) signed 14 Feb 2025.
- **Contact:** [[Emeka Awagu]] listed as client contact.
- **Audit trail:** Created by [[Nora Chukwurah]], sent for signing by [[Okechukwu Eke]].
