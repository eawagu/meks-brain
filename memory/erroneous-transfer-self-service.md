## Overview

Erroneous Transfer Self-Service is a customer-facing flow that lets a Moniepoint user initiate the recovery of a transfer sent to the wrong destination account without routing through a customer service agent.

## Design Intent

Offloads a high-volume, well-structured complaint category from agents to self-service, reducing resolution time and cost while improving CSAT for the underlying pain point.
