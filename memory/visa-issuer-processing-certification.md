## Overview

Visa issuer processing certification is the scheme certification required for TeamApt to operate as an issuer processor on the Visa rails. Part of the [[TPP|Third-Party Processing]] program build-out.

## Status (per [[TeamApt MANCo Meeting - 31 March 2026]])

- **Certification opened.**
- **System configuration ongoing.**
- Mastercard+Visa dispute management certification commencing in parallel.
