## Definition

**Sterling Bank SLA Clause 5.5** is the clause in the Sterling Bank Direct Debit Service Level Agreement that makes Moniepoint liable for **duplicate payments post-reversal**.

## Aligned response (Apr 23 D2B Project Delivery & Optimization Realignment)

Reject Clause 5.5; insist on **[[TSQ integration]]** to enable real-time transaction confirmation. This removes the duplicate-payment liability scenario from the SLA path by preventing duplicates at the source rather than handling them contractually.

## Why structurally significant

Mek's working preferences: "When a process fails, expect structural fixes — forcing functions, phase gates, external verification — not behavioural commitments." Insisting on TSQ integration is the structural fix; accepting Clause 5.5 with no TSQ would have been a behavioural-commitment fallback ("we'll be careful").
