From the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]], Day 2. Participants include [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] (CEO), plus speakers from Product, Sales (SACE/D&X), and Growth teams. [[Ezekiel]] and Ifine co-present the SACE OKR; [[Khalid]] owns Bills Payment product; Amir inherits rewards; Dapo owns [[MoneyCRM]]; Sunday leads BRM verification.

## Key Decisions

- **Rewards program — clarify or kill.** Derive value or stop. A/B tests for existing customers show no clear retention lift; honeymoon rewards for new users do. Data credibility in question (CRM tool/data pipeline). Total cashback liability only ~₦1.9B despite large user base — confirms users are cashing out; liability ≪ concern.
- **VAS is a critical engagement factor.** Strong correlation between VAS usage in first 30 days and long-term retention (~80% M12 for VAS users vs collapse for transfer-only). Next ad campaign pivots to VAS. [[Khalid]] to expand biller catalog (e-commerce, subscriptions, insurance, pension).
- **BRM force will not expand — reactivation focus.** ~30K BRMs total, ~10K productive. Quarterly review: BRMs >12 months tenure managing <30 businesses are deactivated.
- **Bills-Payment business engagement separated from product.** Product stays with [[Khalid]]; dedicated business team handles engagements and SLAs (Monnify model).
- **15-min break at 4:15 PM.** Rewards deep-dive skipped (not ready), 30 min behind schedule.

## D&X Team Structure and Compensation

### Three Layers
- **Execution:** BRMs.
- **Driver:** Cluster Managers.
- **Ownership:** State Coordinators, Regional Managers.

### BRM KPIs (7)
- Loan volume (monthly, unspecified floor).
- Loan value: minimum ₦4M.
- CA: ₦72 minimum 14 days.
- Retention: ₦92 minimum 14 days.
- [[Moneybook]]: 1/month.
- Savings: ₦1M/month.
- Active new businesses: 6/month.

### Cluster Manager KPIs (7 — higher targets)
- 15 loans/month; ₦100M loan value; CA/Retention = BRM; 15 Moneybook/month; ₦25M savings; 105 active new businesses.

### State Coordinator / Regional Manager KPIs (12)
- CA, CDR, Loan Volume/Value, JR, TV, AUM, New & Active Moneybook, Savings Value, Payment Value/Volume, New Businesses with Turbulence.

### Compensation Model
- **BRMs:** 13.5% default; 9% guaranteed + 4.5% "performance wallet" (unlocked monthly on target hit).
- **Cluster Managers:** personal 4.5% tied to cluster performance + up to ₦400K bonus on cluster results.
- **State Coordinators:** 70% guaranteed / 30% performance.
- **Regional Managers:** 60% guaranteed / 40% performance.
- Variable pay for State/Regional unlocks month-end based on YTD.

## Rating 16 System (BRM–BO Accountability)

- 80% of BOs under a BRM must rate the BRM every 60 days.
- Minimum rating: 4.5.
- Consequence: BRM below 4.5 or <80% participation → P&D until target met.
- Cascade: BRM→Cluster Mgr→State Coord→Regional Mgr.

## Rewards & VAS

### Rewards Program Findings
- Existing-customer rewards: A/B test shows negligible uplift (CRM tool/data credibility uncertain).
- Honeymoon rewards (new users): clear incremental retention.
- Unclaimed rewards liability: ₦1.9B total (both personal + business) — low.
- 2.7M accounts cashed out rewards in last 7 days.
- Strategic intent confirmed: engagement/stickiness — NOT treasury revenue from unclaimed float.
- Amir inherits ownership; data structure review planned.
- Possible feature gaps: partial cash-out (user-requested, on backlog); checkout integration as rewards payment option.

### [[VAS]] as Retention Driver
- Retention data: VAS users ≈80% M12 retention; transfer-only collapses within 12 months.
- Expansion: e-commerce, subscriptions (Jumia), insurance, pension.
- UX enhancements for discovery + USSD revamp.
- VAS higher among personal banking; positioned as key to capture personal banking market.
- Integrated with honeymoon rewards + P2P referral ("buy data to earn money").

### P2P Referral Program
- Separated from sales team referral (which becomes a dedicated sales channel).
- Small test launching month-of — airtime rewards for recommending new users.
- Target: students/universities.
- UX incomplete — discoverability concern.
- Combined growth initiatives projected: +1M digital active users by Dec.

### High-Balance Salary Accounts (new initiative)
- Attract salary accounts without employer integration.
- Target: users >₦120K/month average daily balance.
- Benefits: free transfers, airtime vouchers, premium Visa cards with Visa-sponsored benefits.
