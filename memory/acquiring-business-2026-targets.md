## Overview

2026 targets for the Moniepoint Acquiring business, presented by [[Solomon]] at the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]] Day 1.

## 2026 Targets

- **Revenue**: ₦247 billion (+17% YoY).
- **Gross margin**: 49%.
- **Backend success rate**: 97%.
- **P95 end-to-end latency**: 20 seconds.

## 2025 Baseline

- Revenue: ₦11B (+24% YoY).
- Gross value: ~51%.
- Backend success rate: 94.8%.
- P95 latency: 32s.
- Customer complaints: 0.08%.

## Q1 2026 Results

- Revenue: actual ₦53.4B vs target ₦62.4B (below, not ahead — speaker misstatement).
- Margin: 64.4% actual vs 61% target (ahead — partly recovered money from separate source).
- Customer complaints: 0.04% Q1 (target 0.01%).
- Bank-originated disputes: 30% → 9% (target <5%, north star <1–2%).
- Incident resolution: <20min Q1 (target <5–10min).

## Success-Rate Metric

- [[Customer-Facing Success Rate]] (currently 91%) is the **primary reported metric**; backend SR for internal diagnostics only.

## Key Initiatives

- HTTP query verification (replacing ISO; KR3 target 0.01%).
- [[Push to Card]] proactive refunds within 24h of settlement.
- International card expansion (Mastercard, Visa, Amex).
- Contactless POS card-module revamp: ~2s → <500ms contactless speed.
- Partner transfer revamp + eligibility optimization for international merchants.
- [[Geofencing Compliance]] SDK — capability-ready for CBN mandate.
