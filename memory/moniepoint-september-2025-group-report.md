## Summary

[[Moniepoint]]'s consolidated financial performance report for September 2025. Revenue reached $39.95M (3.8% MoM growth, 118% of budget), EBITDA was $8.8M at 22% margin, and the annualised revenue run-rate of $479M exceeded the FY25 budget of $381M. Lending and treasury income drove outperformance while sales/marketing costs significantly exceeded budget.

## Key Points

- **Revenue**: $39.95M in September, YTD $302.8M (12.2% ahead of budget); annualised run-rate $479M vs $381M budget
- **EBITDA**: $8.8M (22% margin), 0.3% decline MoM; adjusted EBITDA margin 25% after excluding MonieWorld
- **Lending**: $11.4M revenue, 11.3% MoM growth, 59.8% above budget; gross loan book reached $328M vs $229M budgeted
- **Treasury income**: $4.25M, 116.4% above budget, driven by higher investment yields
- **POS & Digital Banking**: $22.4M, 0.7% MoM growth, broadly on budget
- **Monnify (Online Payments)**: Underperformed budget by 33.7% due to weaker merchant activity
- **Sales & Marketing**: 253.6% of budget in September, driven by cashback promos
- **Operating expenses**: 11.3% above budget, driven by cloud computing/software costs (+17.3%) and general admin
- **Balance Sheet**: Total assets $1.025B; customer deposits $683M (+5.3% MoM); net loan book $277M (+11.8% MoM); 12.17M active accounts
- **Constant currency**: Revenue 89.1% of budget; EBITDA 77.9% of budget — FX rate improvement (1,498 vs budgeted ~1,900) explains IFRS outperformance
- **Cash position**: $190M group cash, down 33% MoM; $79M loans disbursed in September (+28% MoM)
- **Q3 2025**: Revenue $115.6M (+18.4% QoQ, +81.7% YoY); EBITDA $20.1M (+11.6% QoQ)
