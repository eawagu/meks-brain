## Pattern

Past loss: ~200K [[Moniepoint|Moniepoint Inc.]] POS devices — "new" in system but missing in physical reality. Three mechanisms:

1. **Cannibalized for parts** — components (e.g., [[Topwise]]) reused by third parties.
2. **Sold off** — unauthorized third-party resale.
3. **Disposed without funds return** — scrapped without returning proceeds to Moniepoint.

## Internal Assumption

~40% loss on the 200K missing devices.

## Mitigation (pending)

Goal shifted from full recovery (impractical) to preventing rebuild of another 200K-loss scenario. System controls to limit stockpiling and unauthorized disposals — retreat action item.
