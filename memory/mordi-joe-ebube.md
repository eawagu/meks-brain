## Overview

Executive Director of [[Elfrique|Elfrique Solutions Limited]]. Sole witness for the Claimant in [[Elfrique Solutions v Optisoft Technology — Judgment (31 Mar 2026)]].

## Role

- **Executive Director, [[Elfrique|Elfrique Solutions Limited]]** — commercial and operational representative for Elfrique in the 2021 Optisoft engagement and the subsequent 2022–2026 litigation.

## Litigation — Elfrique v Optisoft

- Deposed witness statement on oath dated 25 Aug 2022 (filed with Writ of Summons).
- Testified at trial 10 Jun 2024. Under cross-examination, confirmed negotiation timeline (Aug–30 Sep 2021), control-panel access granted on Defendant's request (~28 Oct 2021), and Claimant's position that the scope of work was not altered by the Code Igniter vs Laravel distinction.
