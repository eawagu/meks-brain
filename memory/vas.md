## Overview

VAS (Value-Added Services) is the Moniepoint product line covering airtime, data, bills, and related non-core services.

## Status (Day 1 Retreat 2026)

- **27% MoM growth** — but considered far from potential.
- Needs UX improvement + a rewards layer.
- Named as a **pre-June deliverable** under [[Project Phoenix]]'s change-freeze window.

## Retention Driver Evidence (Day 2 Retreat 2026)

- Customers who use VAS within their first 30 days show **dramatically higher retention and deposit amounts**.
- Graph data: VAS users ≈**80% M12 retention**; transfer-only customers' retention collapses within 12 months.
- Hypothesis: the app where a user handles their VAS becomes their "main account," driving stickiness and broader use.
- VAS usage higher among personal banking vs business banking — positioned as key to capture personal banking market.
- **Next ad campaign pivots to VAS** (per Day 2).

## Key Decision (Day 2)

- **VAS is a critical engagement factor.** Elevated from "stapled on" feature to a highly emphasized part of the product's value proposition.

## Biller Catalog Expansion (Day 2)

- Action owner: [[Khalid]].
- Additions: e-commerce, subscriptions (e.g., Jumia), insurance, pension services.
- UX enhancements for discovery + navigation.
- USSD experience revamp.

## Integration with Other Programs (Day 2)

- [[Honeymoon Rewards]] will include more VAS incentives.
- [[P2P Referral Program]] is VAS-centered (e.g., "buy data to earn money").
