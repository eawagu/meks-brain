## Overview

The 2026 profitability targets set by [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] at the Moniepoint Leadership Retreat Day 1 (UK).

## Gates

- **20% PAT run-rate by end-2026.**
- **30% PAT run-rate by Q2 2026** — conditional on lending expansion.

## Trajectory Context

- Q3 2025 approached profitability; Q4 2025 reversed due to [[Verve]] scheme pricing shock and other disruptions.
- Root causes: lack of fine cost control (COGS/expenses), absent goal systems.
- Remedy: management primitives + stronger goal systems.

## Levers

- Contribution margin (reduced promotions, commissions, processing costs) — biggest lever.
- Opex focus: depreciation, salaries, cloud costs (10% rise last month).
- Deposits: only line above budget; drives contribution margin via sticky primary accounts via POS.
