## Overview
Elfrique is a platform providing events, contests, flights, hotels, tours, and trivia services. The company is owned by Mek's father. Mek sits on the board of directors and is the developer building the new version of the site.

## Platform Features
- **Events & Ticketing** — physical and online events with ticket sales, QR code check-in
- **Contests & Voting** — pageant voting and awards contests with configurable voting controls
- **Flights** — flight booking (not yet demoed on new site)
- **Hotels** — hotel booking (not yet demoed on new site)
- **Tours** — tour listings (not yet demoed on new site)
- **Trivia** — trivia contests (not yet demoed on new site)

## New Site Development
Mek is building the new Elfrique site. The Apr 9, 2026 product meeting covered a demo of the **events and contests features** only — other features (flights, hotels, tours, trivia) will follow in subsequent demos.

## Technical Details
- Current hosting: [[InMotion]]
- Planned hosting: [[Azure]] (migration under assessment)
- Payment gateway: [[Flutterwave]] (single web group for callbacks across old and new site)
