## Definition

**AI and Automation Adoption** is the strategic priority — embedded into [[Project Phoenix]]'s hiring roadmap and operating model — that platform engineering and operations roles must integrate AI/automation capabilities by default, rather than treating AI as an add-on.

## Strategic context

Project Phoenix's two stated goals are (1) Platformization and (2) AI-nativeness. The AI-native mandate was issued by [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] in February 2026: all functional managers rebuild workflows using AI ([[Eywa]] / Claude). See the [[Project Phoenix]] entity for the full timeline.

## 2026-04-27 — ALIGNED decision in CI&P Kick Off

The CI&P kick-off ALIGNED on: "the hiring plan for the new platform expansion will prioritize the adoption of AI and automation capabilities in line with strategic goals." New roles will focus on **machine learning operations (MLOps), data engineering, and automation testing**. [[Damilola Oyediran]] owns developing the detailed hiring roadmap aligned with platform expansion phasing.

Source: [[Project Phoenix - CI&P kick off meeting - 2026-04-27]].

## Operational application — AI-Native Operations

Inside CI&P, AI-Native Operations priorities agreed 2026-04-21:
1. Chargeback & Issue Resolution (highest)
2. Logistics & Production
3. General workflow automation (Jira → AI ticketing, tool evaluation led by Rumulo)

See [[AI-Native Operations]] for detail.
