## Overview

ISO 20022 Reintegration refers to Moniepoint's planned reintroduction of the ISO 20022 financial messaging standard into its payments infrastructure. ISO 20022 is the emerging global standard for interbank messaging, carrying richer structured data than legacy MT / NIBSS formats.

## Strategic Importance

- Enables richer remittance data, better fraud detection, and improved reconciliation
- Alignment with CBN modernization direction and international correspondent standards
- Foundation for international remittance ambitions
