## Summary

Prep brief for [[Frank Atashili]] (CPO/COO, TeamApt) for the [[Moniepoint]] 2026 Executive Strategy Retreat at Chicago Booth, London (Apr 14-16). Covers presentation requirements (OKRs anchored to 5 Group objectives, Q1 progress, Q2-Q4 plan), Product Division structure (4 businesses + 2 shared departments), OKR-to-Group-Objective mapping, Q1 data gathering checklist, and 4 critical strategic discussion topics.

## Key Points

- Product Division: 4 businesses — Direct Debit (Daniel Ojinaka), [[Monnify]] ([[Damilare Ogunnaike]]), TPP ([[Tracy Ojaigho]]), Domestic Switching ([[Babatunde Okufi]]). Shared: PMO (Idris Aliyu), Product Design ([[Kelvin Ogumor]])
- 7 division-level OKRs mapped to 5 Group Objectives
- Revenue targets: DD >10% MoM growth, Monnify 100% of target + $1M international, TPP N1.09B FY, Domestic Switching 50% MoniePoint POS via Juliana/ATS
- TPP: 3 [[Visa]] L3 certifications, [[MasterCard]] acquiring license, CMS/EMV development
- Domestic Switching: 22 issuers on network (from 16), 8 Account Switch partner banks (from 3)
- TSP/MoniePoint integration likely major discussion: 6-month One Platform timeline, 5 proposed teams, 20+ engineers needed
- GoSubscribe: Strategic Focus Area #1 — agent-led distribution (scale: 500 BRMs, 25,000 agents)
- International acquiring: Strategic Focus Area #2 — "primary growth driver for 2027"
- Monnify market position recovery: competitive dynamics vs Paystack, [[Flutterwave]]
- Flight: VS412 to Heathrow, arrival 15:55. Venue near Barbican tube
