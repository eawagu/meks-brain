## Scope

The **Account Management Strategy** covers how [[Moniepoint]] manages customer relationships on the consumer lending platform post-launch — retention, upsell, portfolio-level engagement.

## Retreat Gate (April 2026)

Explicit decision at the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]] (Day 1): the consumer-lending platform launch will **not** proceed if the AM strategy is incomplete. This is a phase gate, not a soft dependency.
