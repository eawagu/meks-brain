## Overview

Optisoft Technology Co. Ltd is a Nigerian software services firm. Defendant in [[Elfrique Solutions v Optisoft Technology — Judgment (31 Mar 2026)]] — breach-of-contract action arising from a failed 2021 website restructuring and travel-platform engagement with [[Elfrique|Elfrique Solutions Limited]].

## Known Personnel

- **[[Andrew Ogochukwu Ughonu]]** — Software Engineer, Executive Director. Sole witness for Defence at trial (testified 22 Jan 2025). Claimed 20 years' experience in web development.

## Commercial Profile

- Represented itself during the 2021 engagement as a software services firm "with extensive experience in software development and management" (per Exhibit C1 — company profile tendered in evidence).

## Legal History

- **2021 engagement with Elfrique** — contracted for ₦1,750,000 over 10 weeks to restructure Elfrique's website and build a travel/tours platform. Received ₦300,000 mobilization fee on 8 Oct 2021. Did not commence work to schedule; subsequently proposed revised cost of ₦4,500,000 after discovering the existing platform used Code Igniter (not Laravel as allegedly represented). Contract terminated by Elfrique on 6 Dec 2021.
- **Elfrique v Optisoft (Suit filed 25 Aug 2022)** — judgment 31 Mar 2026 ordered refund of ₦300,000 plus 10% interest from 6 Dec 2021 until liquidation, plus ₦250,000 costs. Loss-of-revenue (₦12.15M) and general-damages (₦5M) claims refused.
