## Framework decisions

- **"Maybe = Reject"** for senior technical leadership roles — ambiguity translates to risk; "maybe" candidates do not advance.
- **Technical bar (must-haves)**: 10+ years hands-on depth in relevant domains, proven scaling experience, large-team leadership.
- **Strong preferences**: fintech / payments experience, legacy-modernization track record, AI/ML exposure.
- **Process refinements**: pre-screen GitHub / architecture docs; mandatory "war story" section; standardized rubric across interviewers.
