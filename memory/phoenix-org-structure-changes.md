## Summary

Detailed organizational restructuring transitioning from product-centric to platform-centric model. Defines six core platforms, cluster structure, reporting lines, governance, and transition rules.

## Key Points

- Platform-centric teams own capabilities across markets (not products per market)
- Each platform has engineering, product, and design leadership
- Capability teams: 3-7 engineers, cross-functional
- Six core platforms: Merchant Acquiring, Digital Banking, Business Banking, Card Issuance, Payment Processing, Infrastructure
- Clear governance for roadmaps, priorities, API contracts, SLAs
- Transition timeline Q2 2026 with phased ramp
