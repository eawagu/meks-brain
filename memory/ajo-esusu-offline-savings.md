## Overview

Offline savings product in development at Moniepoint to capture the cash-based savings opportunity in Nigerian markets, modeled on the traditional **Ajo / Esusu** collector-contributor system.

## Model Clarified

- **Collector-contributor** (not round-robin): one-to-one relationship between a contributor saving their own money and a collector who facilitates.
- Collector gathers daily cash (e.g., ₦2,500) from market vendors and small business owners.
- Contributor pays the collector a commission.
- Physical card marks the transaction; MoneyPoint platform digitizes the record (likely by debiting the collector's wallet).

## Opportunity

- Research conducted January 2026 across multiple regions identified significant offline savings volume.
- Many [[Moniepoint]] agents already run similar side-businesses collecting daily savings — natural operational fit.
