## Overview

The UBO (Ultimate Beneficial Owner) Register is a centralized database tracking the ultimate beneficial owners of corporate customers for regulatory compliance.

## TMAPT Deployment

A centralized UBO register was successfully deployed for TeamApt/TMAPT as part of 2025 Monify compliance uplift.

## Moniepoint State

- UBO tracking is currently manual for other Moniepoint entities — functional but not scalable
- Tosin Eniolorunda flagged this as a "system problem" requiring tool investment
- A desired governance tool would provide dashboard view of compliance levels and generate treatable tickets for problematic customers
