## Overview

The UBO (Ultimate Beneficial Owner) Register is a centralized database tracking the ultimate beneficial owners of corporate customers for regulatory compliance. Industry-standard requirement under global AML / anti-money-laundering regimes, Nigerian CBN regulations, and equivalent frameworks.
