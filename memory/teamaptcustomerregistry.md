## Summary

Full customer and partner registry for [[TeamApt]] covering 30+ entities organized by category: Tier 1 banks (15), fintechs/MFBs/PSBs (6), switch-to-switch partners (2), payment facilitators/merchants (12), card schemes (4), and PTSAs/infrastructure partners (3). Each entry maps industry roles to TeamApt relationship details across all four business lines.

## Key Points

- **Access Bank:** Active across all 4 business lines — DD live (primary volume driver), Juliana issuer, ATS web, Monnify VA provider, TPP target, settlement API in progress
- **Polaris Bank:** Primary settlement bank; NSS live; DD live; PTSP first non-Moniepoint client (planned); critical infrastructure partner
- **Fidelity Bank:** Settlement bank; first external ACT client (live); Juliana issuer; DD pilot-ready; key strategic partner across multiple lines
- **Moniepoint MFB:** Largest customer by volume — 37% POS traffic via Juliana; PTSP services (₦150M monthly activations, ₦1.6B monthly fees); first issuer processing customer (planned 2026)
- **CoralPay:** Key switch-to-switch routing partner — provides indirect connectivity for FirstBank, Zenith, Providus, Unity
- **HabariPay:** Switch-to-switch partner providing GTBank indirect connectivity; also DD Payment Facilitator
- **CowryWise:** First live DD merchant; bullish on DD as future payment rail
- **Payment Facilitators pipeline for DD:** GlobalPay, LendSqr, Paystack, Cyberpay, KoraPay, Quidax, Mono
- **Card schemes:** Visa (acquirer cert ✅, license ✅), Mastercard (acquirer cert ✅, license pending), Verve (active), Afrigo (relationship established)
- **PTSAs:** NIBSS (primary, NSS live for Polaris/Wema), UPSL (connected, pending go-live — CBN mandated dual PTSA)
- CBN regulatory roles defined: Issuer, Acquirer, Non-Bank Acquirer, Switch, PTSP, PTSA, PSSP, PSB, MFB, MMO, Card Scheme, SuperAgent, Personalization Bureau
