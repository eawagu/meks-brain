## Definition

The **Moniepoint Group Seven Management Principles** are the [[Moniepoint Group Organizational Framework]] elements read as a personal directive for every manager. They are not abstract organizational theory — each manager applies them directly within their own unit.

At the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]], [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] emphasized that everything external to an individual employee within the manager's unit — goals, structure, incentives, context, systems, governance — is the manager's responsibility. "It's not this is wrong, it's me, it's my problem. I will fix this problem."
