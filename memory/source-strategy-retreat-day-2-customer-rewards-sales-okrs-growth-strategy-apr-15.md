From [[2026 Executive Strategy Retreat]], Day 2 (Apr 15, 2026). Participants include [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] (CEO, Moniepoint), plus unnamed speakers from Product, Sales (SACE/D&X), and Growth teams.

## Summary

Strategy retreat session covering customer rewards program effectiveness, sales OKRs for Dec 2026, VAS-driven retention, BRM network optimization, Monnify's international growth strategy, and Key Account Management team creation.

## Key Points

### Rewards Program — A/B Testing Shows No Retention Impact on Existing Customers
- Three-group CRM test (rewards+comms, rewards only, control): no significant uplift in engagement/retention for existing recurring customers
- Rewards DO have positive incremental impact for "honeymoon" (new) users in first few months
- Extremely low cash-out rates — only 7,000 POS customers cashed out in last 30 days despite high app adoption
- Total unclaimed rewards liability: ₦1.9B across personal and business banking (described as "nothing")
- 2.7M accounts cashed out in last 7 days (updated figure, all schemes)
- Strategic ambiguity: is the goal stickiness or treasury revenue from unclaimed funds?
- Proposed: rewards expiration policy (3-6 months), partial cash-out feature, making reward balance a payment option at checkout
- Decision: verify A/B test data before final program decision — findings described as "groundbreaking" and counterintuitive

### VAS as Retention Driver — Key Finding
- Customers who use a VAS (airtime, data, bills) within first 30 days show dramatically higher retention (~80% at M12) vs "transfer only" customers whose retention "collapses entirely"
- Hypothesis: the app where VAS is handled becomes the main financial account
- Strategic pivot: position VAS as core value proposition, not stapled-on feature
- Next ad campaign will be VAS-focused
- Initiatives: expand biller catalog (subscriptions, e-commerce, insurance, pensions), UX improvements, USSD revamp, VAS in honeymoon rewards, P2P referral centered on VAS

### P2P Referral Program
- New program targeting university students — reward with airtime for referrals
- Backend complete, UX not done yet
- Expected to generate additional 1M digital EFTs by Dec 2026

### Sales OKRs — Dec 2026 Targets
- **Market Share:** 4.36M monthly active users (BRM-acquired), 3.9M active POS, 70 percentile point gap to next competitor (currently 67), 16.8B total payment volume
- **Retention:** 92% for top Business Owners
- **Lending:** ₦864B GLPV, 3.77% loan penetration (challenged by [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] as too low — needs revision), ~190,787 active loan accounts
- **MoneyBook:** 65,773 active subscribers, 9.17% penetration
- **Revenue:** Payment ₦449.91B, Lending ₦311B, POS Hardware ₦14.96B, Savings ₦127.8B, MoneyBook ₦4.98B
- **YTD Q1 Status:** Payment volume 104%, MAU 99%, Active POS 98%, but MoneyBook 21%, Savings 19%

### BRM Network Optimization
- 30,000 BRMs total, only ~10,000 productive
- Deactivation policy: BRMs managing <30 businesses after 12 months will be deactivated quarterly
- Rating system: 80% of BOs must rate BRM every 60 days; below 4.5 score or <80% response rate → account placed on P&D
- Bidirectional ratings: BRMs rate Cluster Managers, CMs rate State Coordinators, SCs rate Regional Managers
- Compensation: BRM 9% guaranteed + 4.5% performance wallet; State Coordinators 70/30; Regional Managers 60/40

### Key Account Management (CAM)
- New team: Head of Key Accounts Management + specialized Key Account Managers by sector
- Segmentation: Gold, Silver, Bronze tiers
- Goal: retention, cross-selling, up-selling to top-tier BOs
- Multi-functional relationship structure: low/middle/top management physically meeting key merchants
- Concept approved but operational mechanics TBD

### Monnify International Growth
- Targeting international businesses entering Nigeria (Facebook, Teemu, Udemy) still relying on card payments
- Monnify is default VA provider for Teemu, payment account for Udemy
- Active conversations: Booking.com, Spotify, Netflix (physical meeting held)
- "Pay with Moneypoint" feature planned for Paystack, Flutterwave, Interswitch checkouts
- Next: international payment acceptance + local transactions in other African markets
- Bills Payment business side being separated from product side

### Salary Account Strategy
- Attract salary accounts without employer integration
- Packages for customers maintaining average daily balance >₦120K/month
- Benefits: free transfers, airtime vouchers, Visa card benefits
- Key for retaining young talent
