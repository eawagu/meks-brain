## Summary

2024 was foundation year — expanded from in-bank DCIR processing to launching Direct Debit, CDD, and Consolidated Switch.

## Key Points

- DCIR (legacy): 7 banks live; target 12
- Direct Debit (new): 0→7 banks; slow ramp
- Consolidated Switch (new): 2 processors + 10 issuers by Q2
- Transaction success rate: 70%→96.3%; incident response: 240→60 mins
- Revenue baseline: $510K quarterly; target $1M
