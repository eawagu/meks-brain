## Summary

Moniepoint Talent Acquisition leadership deck positioning the shift from "hiring" to "Bar Raising." Diagnoses a quality and capacity gap (new-hire attrition 21.4% — double tenured staff; without sprint intervention hiring velocity delivers only 45% of roadmap needs) and lays out a 45-day Emergency Engineering Sprint plus a 45-day Data & Analytics Sprint, backed by a specialised TA model, AI-enabled tooling (Metaview, Starred), and an independent "Bar Raiser" programme with veto power.

## Key Points

- **Problem framing:** New-hire attrition 21.4% vs. 10.7% for tenured staff; backlog of business-critical roles means "business-as-usual" velocity hits only 45% of roadmap needs.
- **Strategic shift:** TA moves from reactive order-taking to proactive strategic adviser; Quality of Hire (QoH) elevated to core KPI.
- **Specialised TA structure:** Dedicated Team Leads replacing "recruiter-tag" — [[Isaac]] (Team Lead, Engineering) and [[Lekan]] (Team Lead, Product/Data/Design).
- **Advisory capabilities:** Leveling & Density guidance, Geo-Strategy (location trade-offs), Market Intel on global talent movements.
- **Bar Raiser Programme:** Independent partners with veto power; cultural alignment mandatory. Tools: Metaview for interview capture, Starred for Hiring Manager NPS. Success Profiles replace static JDs.
- **45-Day Emergency Engineering Sprint:** Two-pronged — aggressive external agencies + in-house partnership for leadership; "buyout strategy" to shorten notice periods for A-players; internal Strike Teams on the backlog. Targets: VP/Director, Engineering Managers, Mobile Engineers.
- **Engineering pipeline snapshot:** Engineering Manager (HR Call 32 → Live Coding 22 → System Design 4 → PM 1 → Exec 2). Mobile Combined (Senior Mobile + POS Mobile) with HackerRank 121 passed, HR Call 27, Panel 9, Exec 3. Head of Engineering: HR Call 18 → Round 1 7 → Round 2+ 0; **1 offer accepted**.
- **45-Day Data & Analytics Sprint:** 12 critical hires targeted (DS Manager, DS Senior, DE, Analytics). Specialised external partnerships for leadership; internal technical vetting for core roles. Sourcing maps complete, precision headhunting live.
- **Data pipeline snapshot:** Senior Data Analyst 395 apps, 7 technical, 4 exec. DS Manager (Fraud) 35 / 3 HR / 2 exec. DS Manager (Marketing) 114 / 22 HR. Senior DS (Credit) 12 / 36 HR. Senior DS (Fraud) 19 / 1 exec.
- **Asks of hiring managers:** (1) Involve TA early (before headcount finalised), (2) 24-hour feedback on profiles ("if a profile sits 3 days, they are gone"), (3) Treat sprint interviews with "PO-level" urgency.
