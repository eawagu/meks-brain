## Strategy Retreat Presentation (Apr 15, 2026)

Presented the TeamApt CEO deck at the [[2026 Executive Strategy Retreat]] alongside [[Frank Atashili]] (CPO) and [[Damilare Ogunnaike]] (Monnify Lead). 32 slides covering Scale & Economics, Financial Performance, Monnify Review, Strategic Pivot, Execution Plan, and Risks & Pricing.

### Key Positions
- **Moniepoint dependency:** Openly framed the 86% revenue concentration as a strategic risk requiring confrontation. Described the relationship as "imbalanced" — Moniepoint controls timelines and constrains TeamApt from charging market rates.
- **Strategic framing:** "Build alongside, then diversify" — not a replacement story. Six growth levers identified: international card payments, DD/MADD, GoSubscribe, strategic partnerships, TSC, TSA collections.
- **Card pricing decision:** Presented the ₦8.91B/year revenue gap between current pricing (₦3/txn) and market rates (₦7.50/txn). Requested a decision on final fee percentage and cap.
- **Profitability path:** Positioned FY2026 as the first positive PBITDA year on product-driven revenue. Cost base reduced 32% vs FY2025 and largely fixed — incremental revenue flows to PBITDA.
- **GoSubscribe execution:** Aggressively pushing GoSubscribe (DD on POS) working with VAS team as a key execution priority alongside international card payments.
- **TSC with NIBSS:** Discussed settlement and clearing thresholds directly with NIBSS — planned capability, no revenue yet.
