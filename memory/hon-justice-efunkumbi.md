## Notable Judgments

- **Elfrique Solutions Ltd v Optisoft Technology Co. Ltd (31 Mar 2026)** — breach of contract. Granted mobilization refund (₦300,000) plus 10% p.a. interest from 6 Dec 2021, plus ₦250,000 costs; refused ₦12.15M loss-of-revenue and ₦5M general-damages claims applying *Hadley v Baxendale* reasonable-contemplation rule. Court criticised both parties — the Defendant for quoting without first inspecting the technical stack, the Claimant for claim-scope overreach on damages.
