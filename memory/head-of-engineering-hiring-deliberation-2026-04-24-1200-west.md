## Framework decisions

### "Maybe = Reject" for senior technical leadership

The team explicitly aligned: for senior technical leadership roles, **"maybe" candidates are essentially rejects**. The role requires high technical expertise + leadership capability; ambiguity at this level translates to risk. **Going forward, any candidate receiving "maybe" feedback will not advance to the next stage.**

### Technical bar (must-haves)

1. **Hands-on technical depth in relevant domains \u2014 minimum 10 years.**
2. **Proven experience scaling systems.**
3. **Leadership of large engineering teams.**

### Strong preferences

- Experience in fintech or payments
- Demonstrated ability to modernize legacy systems
- AI/ML exposure

### Assessment method

- Deep technical case-study discussions
- Architectural decision walkthrough
- Behavioural questions about team leadership + handling difficult situations

## Interview process refinements

- **Pre-screen** candidates for verifiable technical contributions (GitHub, published architecture docs).
- **Focus questions on hands-on architectural decisions** rather than theoretical frameworks.
- **Mandatory "war story" section** \u2014 candidate describes complex technical challenges they personally resolved.
- **Standardized rubric** for evaluating technical depth across interviewers.
