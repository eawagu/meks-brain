## Summary

Memorandum and Articles of Association for [[Solhigson Ltd]], filed under CAMA 1990. Establishes the company as a private company limited by shares in the IT services sector.

## Key Points

### Company Objects (Memorandum)

- IT support services, consulting, training services
- Telecommunications infrastructure and services
- E-commerce and e-payment services
- Software developing, designing, customization, implementation, maintenance, testing, benchmarking
- System integration and optimization services
- Hardware/firmware sales and maintenance
- Systems and application software design/development for microprocessor-based information systems
- Off-shore software development projects, ISP services
- Internet/intranet website application solutions, enterprise resource planning

### Share Capital

- Nominal share capital: ₦1,000,000 divided into 1,000,000 ordinary shares of ₦1 each

### Subscribers

1. **[[Emeka Obiorah Awagu]]** — 11 Ojomu Road, Off Alexander Road, Ikoyi, Lagos — 980,000 shares (98%)
2. **[[Awagu Osita Uchenna]]** — 11 Ojomu Road, Off Alexander Road, Ikoyi, Lagos — 10,000 shares (1%)

### Articles of Association

- Standard CAMA Table A articles adopted with minor modifications
- Directors have absolute discretion to refuse share transfers
- Pre-emptive rights for existing shareholders on new share issues
- Stamp Duty Cert No: 2020-2121-43368-65341
