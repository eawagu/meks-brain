## Definition

The **Transaction Switching and Processing Platform (TSP)** is the central platform [[Project Phoenix]] is building to **replace existing switchers** and serve all Moniepoint markets (UK, Nigeria, potentially Kenya).

## 2026-04-22 weekly framing

[[Ravi Veluguleti]]: "We are currently handling both [[TMAP]] and the Transaction Switching and Processing Platform (TSP). The goal of the Phoenix project is to build a single TSP platform to serve all markets (UK, Nigeria, and potentially Kenya) by replacing existing switchers, and new organizational structures are expected to take shape around Q3, with complete restructuring by June."

Source: [[TeamApt Weekly Team Meeting - 2026-04-22 16:59 IST]].

## Restructuring milestones

- **Q3 2026:** new organizational shapes around TSP take form.
- **June 2026:** complete restructuring under Phoenix.
- **Recruitment freeze:** in place until Phoenix restructuring takes complete shape.
