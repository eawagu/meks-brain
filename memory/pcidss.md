## Overview
[[PCIDSS]] (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) is a mandatory compliance standard for organisations handling payment card data.

## TeamApt IMS Exposure
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], failure to comply with PCIDSS alongside [[CBN]], [[NIBSS]], [[NDPA]] and other requirements is rated a Major risk in the Service Management Risk register, with the stated consequence being "sanctions and loss of operating licences." Controls: procedure for identification of legal, regulatory and contractual requirements; Legal Register.
