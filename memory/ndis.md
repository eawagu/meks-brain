## Overview

The NDIS (Nigeria Deposit Insurance Scheme) is the Nigerian deposit insurance mechanism. Premium is scaled by an institution's CBN risk rating.

## Impact of Moniepoint Rating Upgrade

Moniepoint's CBN risk rating improved from "high risk" to "above average" in 2025. This improvement:

- Reduces the premium paid on NDIS
- May yield a refund of previously paid premiums (per Speaker 7)
