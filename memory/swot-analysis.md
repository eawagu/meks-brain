## Overview
[[SWOT Analysis]] is a strategic analysis framework mapping an organisation's Strengths, Weaknesses (internal factors), Opportunities, and Threats (external factors). Commonly used to satisfy ISO management system clause 4.1 on understanding the organisation and its context.

## TeamApt IMS Usage
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], SWOT is conducted in two separate frames:
1. **IMS SWOT ([[ISO 22301]], [[ISO 27001]], [[ISO 20000]], [[ISO 9001]])** — covers cloud infrastructure, staffing, brand, proprietary tech, bank partnerships; threats include market competition, regulatory non-compliance ([[CBN]], [[NIBSS]], [[NDPA]], [[PCIDSS]]), cyberattacks, SLA breaches, application unavailability.
2. **OHS SWOT ([[ISO 45001]])** — separate analysis distinguished by the "OHS -" prefix. Covers regulatory compliance culture, skilled workforce, existing H&S policies, management commitment, OHS infrastructure, centralised admin ownership; weaknesses include low perceived OHS risk, limited competency, unstructured incident reporting; opportunities around Nigerian Labour laws, brand trust, digital reporting; threats around enforcement gaps, resistance to change, cost sensitivity, physical security in field locations, overlooked psychosocial/ergonomic risks.
