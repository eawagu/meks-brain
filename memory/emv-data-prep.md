## Definition

**EMV Data Prep** is the cryptographic data-preparation step that takes card-program parameters and produces the personalisation data file consumed by white-plastic card manufacturers. Part of the Card Production chain alongside the [[HSM]] (key management) and [[Card Management System]] (issuer/program/card hierarchy).
