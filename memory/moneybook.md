## Role

**Moneybook** is [[Moniepoint]]'s ERP product, positioned as an AI-native small-business bookkeeping tool with potential to open millions of non-POS small businesses (Instagram sellers, neighborhood shops) — a potential defensible differentiator.

## Retention Gate (2026 Retreat Day 1)

- Current: M1 retention 60% → 67% — still considered poor by [[Tosin Eniolorunda]].
- **Target**: 80% M1 leading to 60% M6 before aggressive CAC spend is authorized.
- Rationale: retention must demonstrate product fit before growth investment, or CAC will be wasted on churn.

## 2026 SACE OKR Targets (Day 2)

- Active subscribers: **65,773** by Dec 2026.
- Penetration: **9.17%** by Dec 2026.
- Revenue: **₦4.98B** in 2026.
- Working with 608+ ICPs in pipeline.

## Q1 2026 YTD Attainment (Day 2)

- Active Moneybook subscription: **21%** of YTD target — low performance attributed to sales team finding the product difficult to sell.
- Moneybook penetration: 21% of YTD target.
- BRM monthly KPI: 1 Moneybook/month.
- Cluster Manager KPI: 15 Moneybook/month.

## 2026 Retreat Day 1 — Employer/Employee Banking Debate

At the [[Moniepoint 2026 Leadership Retreat UK]] (Day 1), there was debate on whether the [[Employer Employee Banking]] reframe should be linked to Moneybook. Consensus: broader employer/employee banking scope proceeds regardless of Moneybook integration.

- Anchors the "full stack" ambition (payments + credit + savings + insurance + software).
- Expands Moniepoint's reach beyond POS-accessible merchants.
