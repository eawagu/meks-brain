## Overview
[[ISO 22301]] is the international standard for Business Continuity Management Systems (BCMS).

## TeamApt Implementation
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], ISO 22301 is one of five standards within the [[Integrated Management System]] at [[TeamApt]]. Documented objectives:
- Ensure 99.9% availability of all key business resources supporting critical business activities
- Reduce high-priority business continuity risks on the risk register by Q1 2026
- Facilitate rapid recovery of critical processes to meet Recovery Time Objectives
- Provide business continuity training to 95% of key staff before Q1 2026
- Regularly maintain and test the recovery/continuity plan
- Reduce reliance on key personnel
- Maintain compliance with contractual, regulatory, and legal requirements

## Key Risks in Scope (Apr 2026)
- Loss of cooling (Minor) — controls: maintenance schedule, multiple cooling units
- Integration knowledge gaps (Moderate) — controls: supervision, staff training
- Prolonged downtime caused by new deployment (Moderate) — controls: independent feature review pre-deployment
- Untested features deployed (Moderate) — same controls
