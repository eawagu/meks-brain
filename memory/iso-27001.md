## Overview
[[ISO 27001]] is the international standard for Information Security Management Systems (ISMS).

## TeamApt Implementation
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], ISO 27001 is one of five standards within the [[Integrated Management System]] at [[TeamApt]]. Key objectives documented:
- Reduce or avoid information security breaches and related loss
- Ensure information is appropriately protected and available per business requirements
- Promote secure coding practices to minimise vulnerabilities in the SDLC
- Promote information security awareness culture and best practice
- Ensure compliance with contractual, regulatory, and legal requirements
- Protect vital assets owned by TeamApt and assets belonging to others under its care
- Protect against malware and ransomware attacks
