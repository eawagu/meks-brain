## Overview

Mastercard is a global card scheme. Two distinct presences in the Moniepoint group:

## Moniepoint MFB (Issuing Side)

- Mastercard cards are processed via [[Interswitch]] as a legacy network
- **<300K cards outstanding**
- **Issuance halted** — no new Mastercard cards being issued
- Future [[Dispute Management Service]] roadmap includes a **Mastercard Chargeback** rail
- BIN 516227 documented in [[Card Manager Service CMS Specification]]

## TeamApt TPP (Acquiring Side)

- Mastercard acquirer processor certification: **completed (2025)** per [[Tracy Ojaigho]]'s 2025 achievements
- Mastercard acquiring license: **pending**
