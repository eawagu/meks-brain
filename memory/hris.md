## Overview
[[HRIS]] is the Human Resources Information System used at [[TeamApt]] to store employee records and training records.

## Role in IMS Controls
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], HRIS appears in the ISMS risk register as a control for both Employee Records (risks: unauthorized access, identity theft — Moderate) and Training Records (risk: unauthorized access — Moderate). Controls:
- Admin access restricted to People Operations Department only
- HRIS login associated with SSO
