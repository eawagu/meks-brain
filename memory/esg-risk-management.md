## Overview

ESG Risk Management is a dedicated Moniepoint investment focused on environmental, social, and governance risks. Reflects material concern from current and future investors and has direct impact on valuation and funding rounds.

## Scope

Not a standalone topic — integrated across functions:
- **Social:** consumer protection, fair treatment, clear product information (e.g., savings rates), physical safety (e.g., preventing POS repair site fires)
- **Governance:** policy and disclosure frameworks
- **Environmental:** ongoing scope

## Owner

New hire reporting to Tunde dedicated to focusing on, documenting, and measuring outcomes for ESG-related risks.

## Strategic Rationale

Poor ESG performance or lack of focus can negatively impact company valuation and pose challenges in future funding rounds (Series D and beyond).
