Draft Product Division OKRs for 2026. Division Lead: [[Frank Atashili]] (CPO/COO). Review draft status.

## 7 Division-Level Objectives

1. **Scale Direct Debit and GoSubscribe** — mandate activation 95%, 50 active merchants, 100 active billers on GoSubscribe
2. **Improve Card Acquiring on Monnify** — 95% card success rate, 30% merchant activation, 100% Loom screening, CSAT 75%
3. **Build International Merchant Acquiring** — 3 Visa L3 certifications, 10,000 international card transactions, Cybersource 3DS live
4. **Build Issuer Processing** — CMS live with [[Moniepoint]] as first customer, EMV Data Prep launched, 1,000 cards issued
5. **Expand Domestic Switching** — 22 issuers (from 16), 8 Account Switch partners (from 3), tokenization live, 4 L3-certified terminals
6. **Grow ACT and Centralize Clearing** — 4 external ACT clients, all ATS banks on TACHA clearing, 99.5% success rate
7. **Reduce MoniePoint POS Cost** — 50% POS via Juliana/ATS, 96% card success rate, <5s ATS response time

## Department-Level OKRs
- **PMO**: 90% sprint completion, <10% non-initiative work
- **Direct Debit**: 95% activation success, GoSubscribe POS + Web live
- **Domestic Switching**: 22 issuers, 50% routing, 96% success rate
- **TPP**: Visa L3 complete, CMS live, 4 ACT clients, 99.9% availability
- **Monnify**: 100% Loom screening, 30% activation, 95% card success
- **Design**: 100% involvement from discovery, 90% shared design system
