## Overview
[[PESTLE Analysis]] is a strategic framework that examines external macro-environmental factors: Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, Environmental. Often paired with [[SWOT Analysis]] for ISO management system clause 4.1 (context of the organisation).

## TeamApt IMS Usage
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], PESTLE factors identified:
- **Political:** government policy changes, instability, civil unrest
- **Economic:** interest/inflation rates, customer demand changes, spending/festival patterns, local currency depreciation, competition
- **Social:** changing demographics, population growth, attitudes
- **Technology:** emerging tech/innovation, infrastructure (power supply, emergency exits, building safety in rented offices — OHS tagged), supporting tech, AI/automation
- **Legal:** legislative and regulatory changes, data protection, post-COVID employee welfare regulation (OHS tagged)
- **Environmental:** climate change, fire/flood/natural disasters

OHS-specific PESTLE items are tagged within the same table rather than split into a separate analysis (contrast with SWOT, which splits into IMS vs OHS frames).
