## Summary
This document defines the accountability framework for [[Emeka Awagu]] (Mek) as CTO @ [[TeamApt]]. It covers six core accountability areas with green/yellow/red health signals, and maps twelve key load-bearing relationships with health indicators. Review date is 2026-09-18.

## Key Points
- Six accountability areas: Technology Reliability & Security, Technology Scales Ahead of Business, Right People in Right Seats, Clear Structure & Decision Rights, Context & Institutional Memory, Goals Cascade Correctly
- Each area has automated and human-judged signal types with explicit traffic-light thresholds
- Twelve key relationships defined with health signals — including CEO [[Dennis Ajalie]], Group CTO [[Felix Ike]], CPO/COO [[Frank Atashili]], and eight direct reports
- [[Tolulope Obianwu]] carries double load (Head + acting NDS Ops Lead) — flagged as highest transition risk
- [[Ravi Kiran Veluguleti]] owns the largest engineering pillar and productivity measurement for hire pause review
- Dual reporting model (Mek / Frank) explicitly named as ambiguity requiring active management
- P95 API response times and MTTR within SLA are key automated reliability signals
- OKR cascade quality is both automated (submission status) and human-judged
