DCI narration is the transaction-narration label previously used to identify transactions processed by DCI on the [[Direct service]]. On 2026-04-13, [[Abdulgafar Obeitor]] explained the narration is being replaced with **'ATS'** for [[Fidelity Bank]] because 'DCI' is considered a regulatory violation / 'sin in the industry'; team must ensure it is not shown to comply with regulator rules.
