## Overview

Disbursement Success Rate measures the success of [[Monnify]] payout / disbursement transactions. Tracked weekly in the [[Moniepoint Group CEO Gazette]].

## Latest reading — week of Apr 24, 2026

Per [[CEO Gazette - 24th April 26]]:

- **Failures dropped 68% WoW** — 123K → 39K.
- **Success rate: 97.52%** (+3.93pp WoW). Direction firmly positive.
- **Recovery incomplete:** still 1.78pp below pre-crisis benchmark. No day reached 99%+. Apr 17 (95.6%) and Apr 23 (96.1%) remain low.
- **Volume caveat:** total volume 1.53M vs 1.95M last week; reduced volume means system is being tested less hard. Full validation requires performance at normal volume levels.

## Historical baseline

Per [[Monnify]]: 2025 payout success rate was 99.99% — current performance materially below that baseline.
