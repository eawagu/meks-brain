## Summary

2025 transition year from build-phase products to revenue-generating platforms. Five products (MADD, Monnify, AptPay Suite, TPP, Juliana) with ₦24.2B–₦30B annual revenue target.

## Key Points

- Monnify: ₦9.05B achieved vs ₦13B target (79%); VTU exits caused decline
- Direct Debit: revenue exceeded 200% of KR; Access Bank major volume driver
- TACHA released in beta; Juliana Card Switch 98.96% success, 99% uptime, 16 issuers
- TPP: Visa processor certified, Mastercard certified, MPGS live
- Card success rate improved 35%→84.42%; payout success 99.99%
- Key lessons: integration ≠ activation, concentration risk, DevRel outperformed (CSAT 94%, NPS 88%)
