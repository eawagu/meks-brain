## Overview

Integration project for [[TeamApt Limited]] (and other licensed PSSPs and Switches & Processing Operators) to integrate with the [[NRS Transaction Monitoring System]], per a [[CBN]] circular dated March 16, 2026.

## Status (week of Apr 24, 2026)

Per [[CEO Gazette - 24th April 26]]:

- The compliance status form provided by the regulator has been **fully completed**.
- The report has been **officially reverted to CBN** within the stipulated timeframe.
