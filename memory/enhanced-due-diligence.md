## Overview

Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) is the extended due-diligence process applied to customers identified as high-risk via the Customer Risk Rating System.

## Legacy Problem

Prior state: EDD forms completed by analysts were not stored in a central database — caused repeated work for the same customer and lowered CSAT.

## Current Direction

Centralize all EDD information via the Loan Verifier initiative, consolidating customer risk rating, CDD, and EDD into one location.
