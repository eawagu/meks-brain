## Overview

The Moniepoint MFB Cards Team is the existing cards organisation within [[Moniepoint MFB]] that is being absorbed into the [[Card Issuance & Processing Platform]] under [[Project Phoenix]] leadership ([[Tracy Ojaigho]] + [[Emeka Awagu]]).

This is **not** the platform-side Phoenix team structure (which is [[Team 1 Card Processing]] + [[Team 2 Card Issuance]] under the Spine-and-Module architecture). This is the operating team that runs Moniepoint MFB's existing card business — the teams that currently execute card sales, distribution, infrastructure, production, and disputes on the legacy stack ([[Postillion]] + [[Card Manager Service]] et al.).

## Head

[[Olufemi Davies]] — Head of Cards, Moniepoint MFB

## Structure (Per 2026-04-21 KT)

### Two Product Teams
1. **Card Sales & Distribution** (MFB Cards Team 1)
   - PM: [[Nadeem Abbas]]
   - EM: [[Elishma Nwobodo]]
   - Scope: Card pricing, sharing formulas, ordering, distribution chain (HO→RO→SRO→BO), customer sales experience for Visa and Africa card programmes

2. **Card Infrastructure** (MFB Cards Team 2)
   - PM: [[Damilola Oyediran]]
   - EM: [[Nitish Chand]]
   - Engineering roster: Backend (Spandan Mishra, Oreoluwa Somuyiwa, Abdullah Ismail, Amar Sharma), QA (Oluwatosin Awodire, Ridwan Abdulazeez), Mobile FE (Taiwo Enikuomehin), SRE (Mohammed-Nasir Ajoge, Moshood Idris)
   - Scope: Transaction processing, security modules, system integrations. The larger of the two teams. Includes deferred VAS scope.

### Deferred Third Team
A planned **VAS team** (tokenization, ACS) was shelved pending the broader platformisation initiative. VAS scope currently sits under Card Infrastructure.

### Two Operations Sub-Teams
1. **Card Production & Logistics** — lead [[Philip]]; team of 4. Physical card stock, card-file generation, warehouse intake, RO distribution.
2. **Chargeback & Issue Resolution** — leads [[Aima]] / [[Precious]]. Chargeback processing, L2 customer support for card issues.

## Transition Status (Apr 2026)

- 2026-04-21: Knowledge-transfer meeting (Olufemi → Tracy + Mek) covered structure, platforms, integrations, roadmap.
- Strike team being formed ahead of full team transition — drawn from teams that will eventually join the Cards platform. Mandates: build the new [[Card Management System]] and drive AI automation.
- Market-share framing: grew from 7th → 3rd nationally in card issuance; target 2nd by end of 2026.
