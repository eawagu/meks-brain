## Overview

UPSL is one of the Nigerian payment processors / switches that participates in PTSA volume distribution. Referenced alongside [[NIBSS]], [[TeamApt T-Switch]], and [[ISW]] in [[PTSA Compliance]] reporting.

## Performance signals

- **Apr 17, 2026 routing anomaly:** UPSL absorbed 3.2M transactions when NIBSS dropped to ~20% of normal daily volume (per [[CEO Gazette - 24th April 26]]).
