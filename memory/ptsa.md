## Overview

The PTSA is a Nigerian payment stack/standard that TMAPT targets 100% transaction processing through. Missing the 2025 year-end 100% goal due to external factors ("Nigeria happened" and "the switch wars").

## Performance

- 2025 average: 53.77% (vs 100% goal)
- End-Q1 2026: 95.30%
- Current: 95.40-95.7%
