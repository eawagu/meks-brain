## Overview

[[Ecobank]] is a pan-African bank on [[TeamApt / Moniepoint]]'s integration pipeline. Direct to Bank SLA signature pending as of Apr 2026.

## ATS RC91

**Apr 13, 2026 (new):** [[Moses Ajani]] (NIBSS PTSA Operations) reported Ecobank card transactions routed to Moniepoint NUS nodes being declined with RC91. Email sent at 03:04 WAT to [[Qazim Adedigba]], aptpaytechnicalsupport, [[Mustapha Ajibade]], [[Saheed Yusuf]], Innocent Nwaokorie, [[Daniel Armstrong]]. Reply-to: majani@nibss-plc.com.ng. Attached: ECOB.xlsx with sample declined transactions.

This is the first NIBSS-escalated RC91 report specifically for Ecobank NUS nodes. Same NIBSS contact who attributed [[Stanbic Bank]] RC91 to Moniepoint timeout on Apr 12. Adds Ecobank to the active [[RC91 Multi-Bank Failure Pattern]].
