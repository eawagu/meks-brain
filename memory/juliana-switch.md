## Overview

Juliana Switch is [[TeamApt]]'s domestic switching platform — the central system that powers the [[Domestic Switching]] business line. Two products:

- **Juliana Card Switch** — POS and ATM card transaction switching. 37% of Moniepoint POS traffic (target 70%), 16 domestic issuers connected.
- **Juliana Account Switch** — account-to-account transaction switching. 99.89% success rate. ₦5M+ quarterly target.

Holds its traffic via the CBN Switching & Processing License. Feeds [[TACHA]] for clearing and settlement.
