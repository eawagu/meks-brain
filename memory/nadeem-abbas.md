## Overview

Nadeem Abbas is the **Product Manager** on Moniepoint MFB Cards Team 1, one of the two MFB cards teams transitioning to [[Card Issuance & Processing Platform]] leadership under [[Project Phoenix]].

Within the MFB Cards Team structure, his team is **Card Sales & Distribution** — the smaller of the two product teams.

## Role

- **Team:** Moniepoint MFB Cards Team 1 / Card Sales & Distribution
- **Role:** Product Manager
