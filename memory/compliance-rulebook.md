## Overview

The Compliance Rulebook is a planned digital tool to replace the current manual process of calling meetings to discuss new CBN regulations and circulars.

## Design Intent

A dashboard where new circulars and associated tasks are visible, with clear tracking of:
- What needs to be done
- By whom
- By when

## Strategic Framing

Identified as a "system problem" — current manual process cannot scale with regulatory pace.

## Key Related Priorities

- Customer Service Assessments
- Automation rulebook
