## Overview

A 2026 Moniepoint initiative to attract salary accounts via customer-side incentives, without needing direct integration with employers.

## Eligibility

- Customers maintaining >₦120,000/month average daily balance.

## Benefits

- Free transfers.
- Airtime vouchers.
- Premium Visa cards with Visa-sponsored benefits.

## Strategic Context

- Complements [[Employer Employee Banking]] (employer-side track) with a customer-side track.
- Part of combined growth initiatives projected to add 1M DAU by December.
