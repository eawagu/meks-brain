## Definition

A **chargeback** is the customer-initiated reversal of a card payment dispute. Moniepoint MFB handles chargebacks via the [[Dispute Management Service]] inside [[Card Infrastructure Team|Card Infrastructure]], with operations led by [[Aima]] and [[Precious]] (Chargeback & Issue Resolution sub-team).

## How chargebacks work in Moniepoint MFB

The [[Dispute Management Service]] handles customer-logged disputes by checking their existence on systems like [[IDS]] (Internal Dispute System) and [[AITA]], and communicating the resolution back to the customer.

## Strike team priority

At the Apr 21 Cards KT, Chargeback / Issue Resolution was named the **highest-priority AI automation target** for the Phoenix CI&P strike team — specifically: automating dispute intake, routing, and status communication.
