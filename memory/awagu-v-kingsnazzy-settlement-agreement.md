## Summary

A binding settlement agreement (Case No: ICMC/ODR/2503) between [[Elizabeth Awagu]] (claimant) and [[KingsNazzy Global Limited]] (respondent, represented by [[Adeyemi Kamar]]), mediated through the [[ICMC]] Online Dispute Resolution Center on 23 July 2025. The dispute concerns delayed building construction and is resolved with a ₦60M payment and building handover.

## Key Points

- KingsNazzy Global Limited (Mr. Adeyemi Kamar) must complete and hand over Mrs. Awagu's buildings by 25 August 2025 per the approved design
- Mr. Kamar to pay ₦60,000,000 as full and final settlement of delay claims, due by 25 August 2025
- In exchange, Mrs. Awagu will cause a deed of transfer/assignment of title for a building owned by Mr. Kamar on the day of handover
- Mr. Kamar waives his claim of ₦3,000,000 (loan to Mrs. Awagu) and ₦26,282,020 (C of O costs)
- Settlement is confidential; to be registered as consent judgment in the High Court of Justice of Edo State
- Mediator: Amb. Victor A. A. A. Ojaide; any disputes from execution referred back to the mediator
- Witnesses with claimant: Arc. Thomas C. Awagu, Arc. Chuka O. Awagu
- Counsel: Uchechi C. Anyanele Esq. (claimant), Derek Chibuike Oguine Esq. (respondent)
