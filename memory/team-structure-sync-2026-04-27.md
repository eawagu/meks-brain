## **ALIGNED**

  - **CMS and ATS team structure finalized** The team composition for the CMS/Phoenix project and ATS is set, with Razak, Steven, and Olawale moving to the CMS team, while Chuku Mecca, Daniel, Fortunate, Eden, and Jamu remain at ATS.
  - **Senior engineer placement flexibility established** Wycliffe is authorized to determine the placement of senior engineer Chuku Mecca between the switch and ATS teams based on operational requirements.

### Next steps

  - [[Wycliffe Ochieng']] Assess Engineer Transfer: Decide if Chuku Amea needs shuffling between core switch and ATS teams based on senior engineer needs.
  - [[Emeka Awagu]] Discuss Team Composition: Discuss and finalize team members who remain and continue existing work with other engineering managers.
  - [[Emeka Awagu]] Finalize Team Structure: Determine final structure, including SRE and DevOps dedicated members, by end of the week.

### Details

  - **Discussion of New Team Formation and Personnel Movement**: The purpose of the call was to discuss the formation of new teams, specifically moving members from ATS to a new team for CMS, referred to as the strike team. [[Ketan Dhamasana]] initially proposed moving [[Razak]] and [[Fortunate]] into the CMS strike team, and moving [[Chuku Mecca|Chuku Amea]] back to ATS from core switch, following the departure of Emanuel and Wabuto. The key concern raised by [[Ketan Dhamasana]] was whether the ATS team would still be able to deliver on their Q2 goals with the movement of these members.
  - **ATS Team Composition and Workload Concerns**: [[Emeka Awagu]] joined the meeting, apologizing for being late due to a meeting on platformization. The discussion confirmed the proposed move of team members to the new project and the remaining team in ATS: [[Chuku Mecca]], [[Daniel]], and [[Eden Lenmark]]. [[Wycliffe Ochieng']] expressed concern about the team's capacity, noting that the engineers being moved ([[Razak]] and [[Fortunate]]) are the most active on ATS projects.
  - **Debate on Retaining Key ATS Engineers**: [[Wycliffe Ochieng']] suggested swapping the engineers, proposing that [[Ketan Dhamasana]] take Daniel, Eden, and one other member, while leaving [[Fortunate]] and a second engineer in ATS. [[Ketan Dhamasana]] countered that the new project, Fenix, requires the best resources to establish the platform, and that [[Razak]] and [[Fortunate]] are the most experienced and familiar with their working methods, which focus on quality and performance. [[Emeka Awagu]] agreed that the new platform needs the best, but stressed that ATS cannot suffer, as it is a major source of revenue.
  - **Final Decision on Team Member Movement**: A compromise was reached to retain one strong engineer in ATS, since both [[Razak]] and [[Fortunate]] are highly capable. [[Ketan Dhamasana]] agreed to leave [[Fortunate]] with the ATS team, while taking [[Razak]] to the new team, noting that [[Fortunate]] has the capability to manage operations and provide technical coverage for ATS. The team confirmed that they would leave [[Chuku Mecca]], [[Daniel]], [[Fortunate]], and [[Eden Lenmark]] with ATS.
  - **Discussion on QA and Other Staffing**: The meeting addressed the staffing of QA, confirming that [[Jamu]], who is the most experienced, would stay with ATS. They agreed to move [[Olawale]] to CMS if [[Jamu]] is not moving. The team finalized that they would take [[Razak]] and [[Steven]], and [[Sie]] could also come as well, while leaving [[Chuku Mecca]], [[Daniel]], [[Fortunate]], [[Eden Lenmark]], and [[Jamu]] with ATS.
  - **Upcoming Project Fenix Kickoff and Staffing**: The meeting confirmed that they will be starting the work for [[Project Fenix|Fenix]] today, with a kickoff meeting to discuss the plan and team formation. [[Ketan Dhamasana]] confirmed that they are currently negotiating OKRs with [[Gafar]]. [[Emeka Awagu]] indicated that they still need more people for the strike teams and that they will be looking to take SRE/DevOps team members from the bank, as they do not want to disrupt the current SRE team at Team Up.
