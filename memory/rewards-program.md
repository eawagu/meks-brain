## Overview

The Moniepoint Rewards Program rewards customers through CRM-based schemes, with tests comparing: (1) rewards + communication, (2) rewards only, (3) control group.

## Key Findings (2026 UK Retreat Day 2)

### Effectiveness
- **Existing customers**: A/B tests show no clear retention/engagement lift.
- **New users (honeymoon rewards)**: clear incremental retention + engagement impact.
- Data credibility is uncertain — concerns about the CRM tool and the data transfer pipeline into the database.

### Liability
- Total cashback liability (personal + business): ~₦1.9B — considered low.
- 2.7M accounts cashed out rewards in the last 7 days → users ARE cashing out.

### Strategic Intent
- OKR is to make product sticky / improve retention.
- NOT to generate treasury revenue from unclaimed float. [[Tosin Eniolorunda]] explicitly stated intent to write back unclaimed liability, not invest it.

### Gap Hypotheses
- Communication insufficient; users may not be aware of rewards feature.
- Feature discoverability within the app questioned.
- Partial cash-out not possible (user-requested, on backlog).
- Checkout integration (rewards as payment option) proposed.

## Decisions

- **Clarify or kill**: the program must be improved to derive value, or be stopped. Tosin confirmed they cannot eliminate rewards due to sales team / field / morale pressure, despite data ambiguity.
- **Amir** inherits ownership.
