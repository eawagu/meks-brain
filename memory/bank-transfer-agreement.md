## What

The **Bank Transfer Agreement** is the legal agreement between [[TeamApt Limited]] and bank partners that governs bank-transfer services (likely including account white-listing rights). One of four legal agreements currently sitting unsigned with [[Felix Ike]] (Group CTO, Moniepoint Inc.) — and identified as the **critical blocker** in the set because it prevents TeamApt from white-listing accounts.
