## Overview

PalmPay is a Nigerian fintech operating in mobile money, agent banking, and payment processing. Competitor to [[Moniepoint]] / [[TeamApt]] in the Nigerian payments landscape.

## Recent Activity

**Mar 13, 2026 CEO Gazette:** PalmPay dropped as a Monnify disbursement provider — flagged as a top risk. CEO ask: onboard PalmPay via [[Juliana]] account switch to mitigate the disbursement channel loss.

## Competitive Context

One of several Nigerian fintech competitors referenced in Moniepoint strategy sessions alongside [[Opay]], [[Paystack]], Flutterwave, and others.
