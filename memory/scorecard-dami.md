## Summary
A balanced scorecard created by [[Tolulope Obianwu]]'s team for [[Dami]] (Lead, Domestic Switching & Processing Operations) covering quarterly KPIs, OKR alignment, and a scoring framework. Financial perspective is weighted heaviest at 35%.

## Key Points
- **Financial (35%):** ≥99.98% revenue capture accuracy, 100% settlement accuracy, ≥95% exposure recovery, ≥99.95% fee reconciliation alignment, 5–8% QoQ cost reduction
- **Customer/Partner (25%):** ≥99.5% SLA compliance, ≥99.8% settlement timeliness, <2 hours critical incident resolution, <0.15% escalation rate, ≥4.5/5 stakeholder satisfaction
- **Internal Process (25%):** 100% daily reconciliation completion, <3 major incidents/quarter, ≥70% automation coverage, ≥99.99% transaction accuracy, ≥95% risk closure
- **Learning & Growth (15%):** Minimum 1 specialized certification/year, ≥80% team training coverage, 3+ process improvements/quarter, 100% critical workflow documentation
- **Scoring:** KPI achievement × KPI weight; thresholds at 80/95/100/110% of target mapping to 0/0.8/1.0/1.2 score multiplier
