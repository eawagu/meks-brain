## Overview

Recovery Operations is Moniepoint's ongoing discipline of recovering counterparty exposures — funds owed to Moniepoint by partners, merchants, or banks following failed settlements, unfunded positions, or contested transactions. Reported weekly in the [[Moniepoint Group CEO Gazette]] as a top-line item.

## Scale

- **Total recoverable (peak):** ~₦6.34B across all exposure chains
- **Recovery progress (Feb 13, 2026):** 73.94% of total recovered
- **Recovery progress (Mar 6, 2026):** ₦147M weekly recovery

## Major Exposure Chains

### Polaris DD → Monnify → CowryWise Chain
Origin: ₦159M exposure from Polaris Direct Debit to Monnify. Recovery flowed through [[CowryWise]] — ₦110M received Feb 13 of ₦124M recoverable in that segment. Active CowryWise line as of Apr 17: ₦35.2M still to recover. See [[Cowrywise Exposure]].

### Jumiapay
- Total exposure: ₦105.5M
- Liened (secured): ₦87.9M
- Unfunded: ₦17.6M
- Recovery progress (17 Apr 2026): 78.07%

### Other Lines
Weekly CEO Gazettes track additional smaller recovery lines across the counterparty ledger.
