NDPC Compliance covers an organization's adherence to the Nigeria Data Protection Act, enforced by the [[NDPC]]. Compliance obligations include lawful basis for processing, data subject rights handling, breach notification procedures, data protection officer designation (where required), and data processing audit trails.

For financial institutions, NDPC compliance interacts with [[CBN]] data residency, retention, and security requirements.

## DDMFB 2026 — Application of Concept
Per [[DDMFB_2026_IT_Strategy_Review]], [[DavoDani Microfinance Bank]]'s 2026 IT Strategy does not address NDPC compliance despite the Act being in effect. Flagged as a critical gap. A compliance strategy should be added explicitly, covering data governance, breach response, and ongoing audit posture — distinct from but coordinated with [[Cybersecurity Strategy]] and [[Disaster Recovery & Business Continuity]].
