## Overview

Loom is Moniepoint's internal platform for fraud prevention and AML monitoring. Currently designed with bank operations in mind.

## Sub-Teams

Per Speaker 2's reference at the 2026 Day 3 retreat, Loom has multiple product threads: AI, Feedback Resolution, FCIN, Trust, Platform.

## 2025/2026 Directions

- Expand AML/CFT compliance tooling within Loom (vs. creating a separate product thread)
- Implement "analyst games" to auto-resolve low-priority compliance issues; escalate high-priority to analysts
- Adapt Loom to work for a switch (Monify) — current design is bank-focused; new PM re-evaluating requirements
- Credited (alongside Onboarding, Awa team) for fraud reduction turnaround
