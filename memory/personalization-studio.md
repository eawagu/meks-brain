## Overview

The Personalization Studio is a Moniepoint tool under development that allows Product Managers or business owners to configure their own financial products without engineering involvement.

## Configurable Elements

- **Financial rules**: interest accruals, rollovers, halal compliance.
- **Customer experience**: onboarding flows, plan management UI.

## Output

- Customer signs up for a plan created with the studio and receives a corresponding contract.

## Timeline

- Main focus for Q3 2026 through end of year.

## Strategic Significance

- Reduces engineering bottleneck on product launches.
- Supports multi-tenant platformization under [[Project Phoenix]] — configuration vs code for product variants.
