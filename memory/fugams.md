## Overview

"Fugams" is Moniepoint's internal shorthand for the top-tier Nigerian banks — referred to as the big five, including GT — that Moniepoint aspires to join.

## Requirements to Join

- Revenue: ~5x current level (from ~680 baseline referenced in Day 3 retreat)
- Growth rate: 60% YoY sustained
- Timeframe: 3-5 years at current trajectory (best case 3)
- ROE: ≥60% — driven by nature of revenue (transactions / high-interest loans)

## Context

- Peer typical YoY growth is 20%; recent 80-100% growth is atypical and unlikely to persist
- Moniepoint cannot yet compete on deposits/loans size but can compete on efficiency and ROI
- 2026 fee target: $56-57M (internal plan to get toward Fugams)
