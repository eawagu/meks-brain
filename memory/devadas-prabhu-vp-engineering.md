## Summary
Evaluation notes for [[Devadas Prabhu]], a VP Engineering candidate referred to [[Moniepoint]] by Nikhil (Head of QA Engineering). Devadas specializes in platform engineering and has led large-scale migrations (API monolith → global microservices, Java → Golang). He was assessed as strong at Director level but not VP+, failing on clarity of thought when selecting examples under pressure.

## Key Points
- Referred by Nikhil (Head of QA Engineering at Moniepoint).
- Self-describes as a "Platform Person" — specializes in horizontal charters across payment methods (Account, Card, POS).
- Key achievement: migrated API monolith to global microservices while handling 4x traffic growth.
- Scale operated at: 1,200 → 5,000 TPS; 99.99% uptime during hyper-growth; led global expansion to Malaysia and Singapore.
- Tech background: Java/Spring Boot veteran, led Golang migration (10x scalability, 30% resource reduction), now language-agnostic in philosophy.
- Currently leads 120+ person org with 25 senior leaders; familiar with CBN and Nigerian regulatory context.
- Primary motivation for moving: company shifting back to 4-5 days in-office; seeking remote-first culture.
- Compensation: ~1.7 CR cash + 1 CR annual ESOP (monthly vesting; most equity already vested).
- Verdict: OK for Director level; NOT VP+. Failed on clarity of thought (chose a wrong example before correcting himself).
