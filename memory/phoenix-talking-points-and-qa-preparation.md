## Summary

Comprehensive FAQ and talking points guide for three Phoenix briefing sessions. Consistent messaging across audiences. Tone: All-staff (reassuring), PM/Leads (detailed, collaborative), HR (precise, action-oriented).

## Key Points

- Key message: "Same mission. Better structure. Bigger reach."
- What NOT to say: cost reduction, jobs at risk, all decided, no choice in assignment
- Risks to address: Juliana Card Switch 42-50% POS market share, parallel-run validation critical
- Recurring: no big bang, instant rollback per transaction type, OKRs remain
