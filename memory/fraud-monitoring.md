## Overview
Fraud monitoring is the compliance-and-operations discipline that detects, attributes, and responds to fraudulent activity on [[TeamApt Limited]] and [[Moniepoint]] platforms — terminal, switch, acquiring, and processing layers. It operates as an adjacent thread to [[Switch Monitoring]] (transactional reliability and routing visibility) and shares regulator-driven urgency from [[CBN]] inspection cycles.

## State (Apr 2026)

- The fraud monitoring workstream was the nominal scope of the Apr 27, 2026 [[Fraud monitoring plan and concerns - 2026_04_27 14_11 WAT - Notes by Gemini|stakeholder call]] (analyzed companion: [[Fraud Monitoring Plan and Concerns 2026-04-27]]) — most stakeholders absent; agenda collapsed into rescheduling and switch-monitoring-strategy alignment.
- Q1 2026 OKRs on monitoring tracks were not fully met; Q2 carryover not acceptable per [[Adefemi Opeogun]].
- Per [[Monify]] entity, fraud-related compliance uplift in the "David phase" included transaction monitoring (limit monitoring by Damilari, specific monitoring by Awal), centralized UBO register, customer screening / risk rating dashboard, switch failure test, and a fraud policy document — all completed.
