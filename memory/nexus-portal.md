## Overview
[[Nexus portal]] is the TeamApt-internal tool used to maintain the inventory of computing assets.

## Role in IMS Controls
Per the [[TeamApt Management Review 19_04_2026]], during the internal audit (ISMS A5.9) the team demonstrated the Nexus portal for computing assets and [[Wiz]] for cloud assets. Asset inventory is maintained with serial numbers and assigned users. Gap flagged: Supporting Utilities (CCTV, biometric scanners, software) inventory not sighted — OFI to extend inventory scope.
