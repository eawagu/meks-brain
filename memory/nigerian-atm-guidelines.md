## Summary

Regulatory framework for ATM operations covering terminal standards, interoperability, fees, dispute resolution, and recent tech mandates. ATMs declining in volume but remain critical for cash access.

## Key Points

- On-us ATM failures must be reversed instantly; not-on-us via NCS within T+1
- PCI DSS and EMV Level 1 & 2 mandatory; anti-skimming devices required
- ISO 20022 migration and geo-tagging (dual-frequency GPS, 10-metre geofence) mandated August 2025
- ATM fees: ₦100 per ₦20,000 for on-site; ₦100 + surcharge (up to ₦500) for off-site
- Card-scheme neutrality enforced; downtime cannot exceed 72 consecutive hours
