## Summary

Formal reference letter from [[Emeka Awagu]] (signed as CTO, Hydrogen Payment Services) to [[CBN]] Banking Supervision Department, supporting [[Ina Alogwu]]'s appointment as Non-Executive Director to the Board of [[9PSB]]. Dated October 8, 2024.

## Key Points

- Emeka has known Ina for over 16 years (since 2008)
- First met at [[Interswitch]] — Ina joined as Risk Specialist, Emeka was a software engineer
- Reconnected in 2016: Emeka as Head of Commerce & Digital Channels, Ina leading Product Strategy and Innovation
- Ina joined [[ARM Holding Company]] in 2021 as Director of Digital Transformation
- Letter highlights Ina's expertise in risk management, product innovation, and digital transformation
- Letter addressed to CBN Banking Supervision Department (regulatory approval for board appointment)
- Emeka signed as CTO of Hydrogen Payment Services (prior role/company name context)
