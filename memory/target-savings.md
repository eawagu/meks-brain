## Overview

Target Savings is [[Moniepoint]]'s goal-based savings product. Customers set a target amount and save towards it — created in direct response to customer research showing the majority save "towards something." Launched November 2025.

## Status (Day 1 Retreat 2026)

- **Breakout product** — substantially surpassed AUM goals.
- Described as the "break-back platform" of the Savings department.
- Growth pattern: **straight line** (goal-driven accumulation).
- Customers onboard directly to Target Savings as their first product (not cannibalizing other products).
- **Only product being marketed** — exclusively via social media; email and other channels unused.

## Strategic Significance

- Demonstrates organic demand even without competitive rates — signal that performance could be substantially higher with proactive marketing.
