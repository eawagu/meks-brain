## Overview

The CBN (Central Bank of Nigeria) Tiered Pricing Directive is an anticipated regulatory change that would tier transfer fees by transaction size — notably ₦10 for transfers under ₦5,000 vs the currently common flat ₦20 fee for all transfer sizes.

## Moniepoint Posture (2026 Q1)

Forecasted in internal planning but not implemented in Moniepoint's fee structure as of the 2026 Day 2 retreat. Revenue impact modeling is underway.

## Strategic Implication

If implemented, would reduce per-transaction revenue on the high-volume low-value transfer segment and require re-pricing other fees or monetizing other levers (float, VAS, premium features) to preserve payments contribution.
